Woo Woo

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusTropical

Ingredients

  • Vodka45 ml
  • Peach Schnapps30 ml

Garnish

Lime wedge or cherry

Instructions

Build over ice. Add vodka and peach schnapps to a highball glass filled with ice. Top with cranberry juice and stir gently. Garnish with a lime wedge or cherry.

A sweet and fruity highball cocktail, popular for its ease of preparation and refreshing taste. The Woo Woo is a simple mix of vodka, peach schnapps, and cranberry juice, typically served over ice in a highball glass.
