Bloody Maria

Flavor Notes

SaltyUmamiSpicyCitrusHerbalSavory

Ingredients

  • Tequila60 ml
  • Tomato Juice120 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Hot Sauce2 dashes
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 dashes
  • Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Black Pepper1 pinch

Garnish

Celery stalk, lime wedge, olives, and/or spicy pickled vegetables.

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Roll gently between the shaker halves to mix (or stir gently). Strain into an ice-filled glass. Garnish as desired.

Savory Tequila Cocktail

A spicy and savory variation of the classic Bloody Mary:

  • Tequila instead of vodka for a distinct flavor.
  • Tomato juice, spices, and hot sauce create a complex, layered taste.
  • Garnishes like celery, olives, and lime enhance the experience.
