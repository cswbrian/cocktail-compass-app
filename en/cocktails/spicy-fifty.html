Spicy Fifty

Flavor Notes

SpicyFloralCitrusVanilla

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Vodka50 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur15 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Red Chili Pepper1 slice

Garnish

Thin chili slice or lime wedge.

Instructions

Muddle chili pepper in a shaker. Add other ingredients and shake with ice. Double strain into a chilled glass.

A Modern Classic

The Spicy Fifty is a modern cocktail known for its balance of sweet, spicy, and tart flavors. It features:

  • Vanilla vodka for a smooth base.
  • Elderflower liqueur for floral notes and sweetness.
  • Lime juice for acidity.
  • Chili pepper for a spicy kick.
