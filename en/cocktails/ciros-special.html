Ciro's Special Cocktail

A classic cocktail featuring a blend of gin, fresh lemon juice, sugar, and egg white, creating a smooth and balanced drink.

  • Gin provides the base spirit.
  • Lemon juice adds acidity and brightness.
  • Sugar balances the tartness.
  • Egg white contributes to a silky texture and frothy head.
