Rusty Nail

Flavor Notes

HerbalBitterSmokyWoody

Ingredients

  • Generic Whisky45 ml
  • Generic Liqueur25 ml

Garnish

Lemon peel twist (optional).

Instructions

Build over ice. Stir gently to combine.

A simple yet sophisticated Scotch-based cocktail.

The Rusty Nail combines the robust flavor of Scotch whisky with the sweet, herbal notes of Generic Liqueur:

  • Typically served over ice in an old-fashioned glass.
  • Easy to make and enjoy.
