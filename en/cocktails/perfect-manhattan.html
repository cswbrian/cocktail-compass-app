Perfect Manhattan

Flavor Notes

BitterFruityHerbalSpicyWoody

Ingredients

  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth15 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Garnish

Luxardo Cherry

Instructions

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into chilled glass.

A variation on the classic Manhattan cocktail.

It balances the boldness of rye whiskey with the herbal sweetness of both sweet and dry vermouth, creating a complex and refined drink.

  • Equal parts sweet and dry vermouth
  • Bitters to enhance the flavors
  • Luxardo Cherry for garnish
