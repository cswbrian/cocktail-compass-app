Fish House Punch

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusTropicalWoody

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum375 ml
  • Cognac375 ml
  • Peach Brandy125 ml
  • Sugar250 g
  • Water750 ml
  • Lemon Juice375 ml

Garnish

Lemon slices and/or nutmeg

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a large punch bowl. Add a large block of ice. Stir well. Let sit for at least 2 hours before serving.

Historical American Punch

A potent and flavorful punch, traditionally served at social gatherings:

  • Based on a recipe from the Fish House (State in Schuylkill)
  • Features a blend of dark rum, cognac, and peach brandy
  • Balanced with citrus and sugar for a refreshing finish.
