Corpse Reviver #2

Flavor Notes

CitrusHerbalFloralBitter

Ingredients

  • Gin30 ml
  • Lillet Blanc30 ml
  • Orange Liqueur (Cointreau)30 ml
  • Absinthe2 dashes
  • Fresh Lemon Juice30 ml

Garnish

Lemon twist

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a lemon twist.

A potent and complex cocktail designed as a 'hair of the dog' remedy.

  • Features a blend of gin, lemon juice, Lillet Blanc, Cointreau, and a dash of absinthe.
  • Perfectly balanced between sweet, sour, and herbaceous notes.
