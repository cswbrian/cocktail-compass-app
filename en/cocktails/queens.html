Queens

Flavor Notes

TropicalFruityNuttyVanilla

Ingredients

  • Pineapple Rum30 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup22 ml
  • Vanilla Extract2 drops

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and/or a Maraschino cherry

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a glass filled with ice.

A Rich and Tropical Delight

A smooth and creamy cocktail that balances rum with tropical fruit flavors and vanilla.

  • Pineapple rum provides the base
  • Pineapple and lime juices add zest
  • Orgeat syrup and vanilla extract give it sweetness and body
