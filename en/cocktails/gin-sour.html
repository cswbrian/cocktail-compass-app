Gin Sour

Flavor Notes

CitrusHerbalFruityFloral

Ingredients

  • Gin60 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Garnish

Lemon or orange peel, cherry (optional)

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled glass. If using egg white, dry shake first for better foam.

Classic Sour Cocktail

A simple yet balanced cocktail that is both refreshing and flavorful.

  • A combination of gin, lemon juice, and sugar.
  • Often includes egg white for a silky texture.
  • Garnished with a lemon or orange peel.
