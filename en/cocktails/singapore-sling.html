Singapore Sling

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusHerbalTropicalSpicy

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Cherry Heering30 ml
  • Cointreau15 ml
  • Benedictine7 ml
  • Pineapple Juice15 ml
  • Lime Juice10 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and Maraschino cherry

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a hurricane glass filled with ice. Garnish.

A Classic Tropical Cocktail

The Singapore Sling is a complex and refreshing cocktail with a balance of sweet, sour, and herbal flavors. It features:

  • Gin as the base spirit
  • Cherry liqueur (Cherry Heering) for sweetness and color
  • Benedictine for herbal and honeyed notes
  • Cointreau for citrus complexity
  • Pineapple juice for tropical flavor
  • Lime juice for acidity
  • A dash of Angostura bitters for added depth
