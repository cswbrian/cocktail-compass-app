Moose Milk

Flavor Notes

CreamyVanillaNuttyChocolateCoffee

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur30 ml
  • Whole Milk90 ml
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk30 ml
  • Vanilla Extract15 ml

Garnish

Grated nutmeg or chocolate shavings

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and heat gently, stirring until warm. Do not boil. Alternatively, combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice, shake, and strain into a glass. Warm or cold as preferred.

Rich and Decadent Holiday Drink

A creamy and warming beverage, often enjoyed during the winter months. Variations exist, but the core ingredients create a comforting and indulgent experience.

  • Traditionally served warm.
  • Variations can include coffee or chocolate liqueur.
  • Often associated with festive gatherings.
