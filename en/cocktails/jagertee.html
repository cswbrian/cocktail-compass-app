Flavor Notes

HerbalSpicyFruityWoody

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum40 ml
  • Herbal Liqueur20 ml
  • Black Tea20 ml
  • Orange Slice1 piece
  • Cinnamon1 piece
  • Clove1 piece
  • Lemon Slice1 piece

Garnish

Lemon wedge or orange slice (optional)

Found data issue, a bug or have feedback? Let me know!

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a heat-resistant glass or mug. Stir well. Heat until warm, but do not boil.

Traditional Austrian Winter Warmer

A potent and flavorful hot beverage perfect for cold days, featuring:

  • A blend of dark rum and Austrian herbal spirit.
  • Sweetened and spiced with various ingredients.
  • Served hot for maximum comfort.
