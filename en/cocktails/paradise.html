Paradise

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusHerbal

Ingredients

  • London Dry Gin30 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur15 ml
  • Orange Juice20 ml

Garnish

Orange peel or cocktail cherry

Found data issue, a bug or have feedback? Let me know!

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

A pre-Prohibition classic

The Paradise cocktail offers a harmonious blend of gin, apricot liqueur, and orange juice, resulting in a balanced and fruity drink with a slightly tart finish.

  • Sweet and tart balance
  • Fruity profile
  • Easy to drink
