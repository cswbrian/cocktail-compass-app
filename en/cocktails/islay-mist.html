Islay Mist: A Gentle Introduction to Peat

Islay Mist is a blended Scotch whisky that offers a milder, sweeter introduction to the typically intense flavors of Islay whiskies. It features:

  • A blend of Islay single malt (Laphroaig is a key component) and Highland malts
  • A noticeable but not overpowering peatiness
  • A honeyed sweetness and notes of spice
