Rose Kennedy

Flavor Notes

CitrusFruityHerbal

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Lime Juice, freshly squeezed30 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml

Garnish

Lemon twist

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

A simple and classic cocktail:
