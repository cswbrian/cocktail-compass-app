Egg Nog

Flavor Notes

CreamyVanillaNuttySpicy

Ingredients

  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Large Eggs2 pc
  • Granulated Sugar60 ml
  • Whole Milk120 ml
  • Heavy Cream60 ml
  • Pinch of Nutmeg1 pinch

Garnish

Grated nutmeg and cinnamon stick

Instructions

Separate egg yolks and whites. Beat yolks with sugar until light and fluffy. Gradually add milk, cream, bourbon, and rum. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gently fold egg whites into the yolk mixture. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Rich and Festive Holiday Drink

A classic creamy beverage, traditionally served during the winter holidays. It features:

  • A blend of dairy, eggs, and spirits
  • Often spiced with nutmeg and cinnamon
  • Can be served chilled or at room temperature
