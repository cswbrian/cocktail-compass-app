Smoked Peach Bellini

Flavor Notes

FruitySmokyTropical

Ingredients

  • Smoked Peach Puree60 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Garnish

Peach slice

Instructions

Combine smoked peach puree and simple syrup in a champagne flute. Top with Prosecco. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with a peach slice.

Smoked Peach Bellini: A Modern Twist

A sophisticated take on the classic Bellini cocktail, featuring:

  • Smoked peach puree infuses a delightful smoky flavor.
  • Prosecco adds effervescence and a crisp finish.
  • A touch of simple syrup balances the tartness of the peach.
