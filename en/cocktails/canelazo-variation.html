Canelazo (Variation)

Flavor Notes

HerbalCitrusSpicyFruity

Ingredients

  • Aguardiente45 ml
  • Lime Juice20 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Garnish

Lime or orange wedge

Found data issue, a bug or have feedback? Let me know!

Instructions

Shake with ice and strain into glass filled with ice. Optionally top with soda water.

Colombian Anise Delight

A refreshing mix showcasing Colombia's signature spirit, Aguardiente, often enjoyed in festive occasions:

  • Aguardiente provides a unique anise flavor.
  • Lime/Orange juice adds a citrusy touch.
  • Sugar balances the drink with sweetness.
