Smoked Pineapple Margarita

Flavor Notes

SmokyTropicalCitrusSpicyFruity

Ingredients

  • Blanco Tequila50 ml
  • Chili Liqueur15 ml
  • Lime Juice25 ml
  • Agave Nectar20 ml
  • Smoked Pineapple Juice50 ml

Garnish

Pineapple wedge or smoked sea salt rim

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a glass. If desired, rim the glass with smoked sea salt before pouring.

A Smoky Twist on a Classic

This margarita variation features the addition of smoked pineapple, creating a delightful blend of:

  • Sweet and smoky flavors
  • The refreshing tartness of lime
  • A hint of spice from the chili liqueur
