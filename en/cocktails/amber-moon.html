Amber Moon

Flavor Notes

HerbalBitterWoodyGrassy

Ingredients

  • Whisky45 ml
  • Beer (Pilsner or Lager)120 ml

Garnish

None typically, but a lemon wedge or orange slice can be added.

Instructions

Pour whisky into the glass, top with beer. No stirring required.

A simple yet classic cocktail, the Amber Moon is essentially whisky lightened and lengthened with beer. It's typically served as a light, refreshing drink, often enjoyed in the morning or early afternoon.

  • Easy to prepare
  • A refreshing alternative to a straight whisky
  • Best with lighter-bodied beers
