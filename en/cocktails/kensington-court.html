Kensington Court

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusFloralCreamy

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Fresh Raspberry15 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Egg White20 ml

Garnish

3 Fresh Raspberries

Instructions

Muddle raspberries, then combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously until well-chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with fresh raspberries.

A modern classic cocktail

This vibrant cocktail offers a delightful balance of flavors:

  • Gin provides a botanical base.
  • Fresh raspberry provides tartness and fresh taste.
  • Lemon juice adds brightness and acidity.
  • Egg white creates a smooth, creamy texture.
  • Sugar syrup balances the tartness.
