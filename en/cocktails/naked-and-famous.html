Naked and Famous

Flavor Notes

SmokyCitrusBitterHerbal

Ingredients

  • Mezcal22 ml
  • Yellow Chartreuse22 ml
  • Aperol22 ml

Garnish

Lime wedge or zest (optional)

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

Modern Classic Cocktail

A vibrant and complex cocktail with a balance of:

  • Smoky mezcal
  • Herbal Chartreuse
  • Bitter Aperol
  • Sour Lime Juice
