Flavor Notes

HerbalCitrusFloralFruity

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur22 ml
  • Cucumber6 slices
  • Lime Juice22 ml

Garnish

Cucumber ribbon or wheel

Found data issue, a bug or have feedback? Let me know!

Instructions

Muddle cucumber, add other ingredients, shake with ice, double strain into chilled glass.

Refreshing Herbal Cocktail

A vibrant and herbaceous cocktail that combines gin, elderflower liqueur, lime juice, and cucumber. Ideal for warm weather and aperitivo hour.

  • Balanced sweetness and tartness
  • Aromatic and refreshing
  • Easy to prepare
