Kangaroo

Flavor Notes

BitterHerbal

Ingredients

  • Gin60 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml

Garnish

Lemon twist or olive (optional)

Instructions

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

Simple and Strong

The Kangaroo is essentially a Vodka Martini, but made with Gin. It is a dry, spirit-forward cocktail that showcases the quality of the gin used.

  • Minimal ingredients.
  • Dry and crisp flavor profile.
  • Easy to prepare.
