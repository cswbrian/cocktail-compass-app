Whiskey and Coke

Flavor Notes

SpicyVanillaFruityWoody

Ingredients

  • Whiskey50 ml
  • Cola120 ml

Garnish

Optional: Lime wedge or cherry.

Instructions

Pour whiskey into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with cola. Add garnish if desired.

A simple highball cocktail made with whiskey and cola. It's a classic, easy-to-make drink often enjoyed for its refreshing and familiar taste. The type of whiskey can significantly impact the overall flavor profile. Some variations include the Jack and Coke (using Jack Daniel's Tennessee Whiskey).
