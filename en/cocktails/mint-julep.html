Mint Julep

Flavor Notes

HerbalGrassyWoody

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Mint Leaves6 leaves

Garnish

Mint sprig

Instructions

Muddle mint and sugar, add bourbon, fill with crushed ice, stir, and garnish.

Classic Southern Cocktail

A refreshing and aromatic cocktail, featuring:

  • Bourbon as the base spirit
  • Fresh mint for herbal notes
  • Sugar for sweetness
  • Crushed ice for chilling and dilution
