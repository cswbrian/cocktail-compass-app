Smoky Paloma

Flavor Notes

SmokyCitrusFruitySaltyHerbal

Ingredients

  • Tequila Blanco45 ml
  • Mezcal15 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda90 ml
  • Smoked Salt0 to taste

Garnish

Grapefruit wedge

Instructions

Rim glass with smoked salt, add ingredients, and top with grapefruit soda. Gently stir.

Smoky Paloma: A Modern Twist

A refreshing take on the classic Paloma cocktail featuring:

  • Smoked salt rim for savory depth
  • Mezcal adds a smoky flavor
  • Grapefruit soda provides effervescence and citrus notes
