Ramos Gin Fizz

Flavor Notes

CitrusFloralCreamyHerbal

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Heavy Cream30 ml
  • Egg White15 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Orange Flower Water2 dashes
  • Chilled Carbonated Water90 ml

Garnish

Orange twist or edible flower

Instructions

Dry shake (without ice) gin, lemon juice, lime juice, simple syrup, egg white, cream, and orange flower water vigorously for 30 seconds. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled. Strain into a highball glass, top with chilled carbonated water. Garnish with an orange twist or edible flower.

A Classic New Orleans Cocktail

A complex and creamy cocktail known for its distinctive texture and refreshing flavors:

  • Gin base provides the spirituous backbone.
  • Citrus juices (lemon and lime) add a vibrant tartness.
  • Egg white contributes to a silky smooth texture and frothy head.
  • Orange flower water imparts a delicate floral aroma.
  • Cream enriches the body and enhances the overall smoothness.
  • Carbonated water creates a light and effervescent finish. The long shake is essential to create the fizz.
