Green Beast

Flavor Notes

HerbalCitrusBitterGrassy

Ingredients

  • Absinthe50 ml
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Soda Water80 ml

Garnish

Mint sprig (optional)

Instructions

Add absinthe, lime juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with soda water. Garnish with a mint sprig (optional).

Refreshing Absinthe Cocktail

A vibrant and potent cocktail, characterized by:

  • Strong absinthe flavor
  • A sweet and sour balance
  • A distinctive green hue

Perfect for those who enjoy the bold taste of absinthe.
