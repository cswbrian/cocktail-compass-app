Sherry Cobbler

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusNuttyYeasty

Ingredients

  • Fino Sherry90 ml
  • Sugar1 tsp
  • Orange Slice2 pc

Garnish

Orange slice and seasonal berries

Instructions

Muddle sugar and orange, add sherry and ice, shake briefly, garnish.

A refreshing and elegant fortified wine cocktail.

This classic drink showcases the unique character of Sherry with:

  • A touch of sweetness from sugar.
  • Bright citrus notes from orange.
  • Topped with seasonal fruits
