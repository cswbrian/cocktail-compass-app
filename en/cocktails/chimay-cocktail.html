Chimayó Cocktail

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusHerbal

Ingredients

  • Tequila Blanco45 ml
  • Creme de Cassis15 ml

Garnish

Apple slice

Instructions

Shake with ice and strain into glass filled with ice. Top with apple cider.

A Southwestern Delight

The Chimayó cocktail is a regional specialty from New Mexico. It combines tequila with apple cider and crème de cassis for a sweet and tart experience.

  • Named after the town of Chimayó, NM
  • A delicious blend of tequila, cider, and cassis
