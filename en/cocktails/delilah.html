溝酒神燈

Delilah

Flavor Notes

FloralCitrusHerbal

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur20 ml
  • Lemon Juice20 ml

Garnish

Lemon twist or edible flower

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

A refreshing and floral cocktail.

Featuring:

  • Gin base for a botanical profile.
  • Elderflower liqueur for floral sweetness.
  • Lemon juice for tartness and balance.
