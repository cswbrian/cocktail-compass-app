Brandy Crusta

Flavor Notes

CitrusFruityFloralVanillaWoody

Ingredients

  • Cognac60 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur15 ml
  • Bitters2 dashes
  • Sugar2 tsp

Garnish

Large lemon or orange peel, carefully fitted inside the glass

Instructions

Sugar the rim of a glass with lemon juice and sugar. Carefully place a long citrus peel inside the glass. Shake ingredients with ice and strain into the prepared glass.

Elegant Brandy Cocktail

Considered by some to be the predecessor of the Sidecar, featuring:

  • Sugared rim for added sweetness and visual appeal.
  • Citrus oils expressed from the garnish which enhances the aroma.
