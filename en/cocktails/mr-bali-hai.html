Mr. Bali Hai

Flavor Notes

TropicalFruitySpicyNutty

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Light Rum30 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur15 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Garnish

Orchid, pineapple spear, and/or paper umbrella

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into mug filled with fresh ice.

A potent and complex tiki drink.

Mr. Bali Hai is a strong cocktail featuring multiple rums and tropical flavors.

  • A creation of Don the Beachcomber
  • Served in a distinctive Mr. Bali Hai mug.
