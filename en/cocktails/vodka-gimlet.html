Vodka Gimlet

Flavor Notes

CitrusFruityHerbal

Ingredients

  • Vodka60 ml
  • Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Garnish

Lime wheel or wedge

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

Classic, Refreshing Cocktail

A simple yet elegant cocktail featuring:

  • Vodka and lime juice for a tart, clean taste
  • Simple syrup to balance the acidity.
