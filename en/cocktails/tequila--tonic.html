Tequila & Tonic

Flavor Notes

CitrusHerbalBitter

Ingredients

  • Tequila50 ml
  • Tonic Water100 ml

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel

Instructions

Pour tequila into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with tonic water. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Simple and Refreshing Highball

A classic combination of tequila and tonic water:

  • Easy to make and customizable
  • Great for warm weather
  • Can be garnished with lime or other citrus
