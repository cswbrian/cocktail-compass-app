Bijou

Flavor Notes

HerbalBitterCitrusFloral

Ingredients

  • Gin30 ml
  • Green Chartreuse30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Garnish

Orange peel twist

Instructions

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

Classic cocktail showcasing balance and complexity

The Bijou Cocktail is a classic, spirit-forward drink known for its harmonious blend of flavors. It features equal parts gin, green Chartreuse, and sweet vermouth, with a dash of orange bitters. The name "Bijou" (French for jewel) refers to the colors of the ingredients: gin for diamond, Chartreuse for emerald, and vermouth for ruby.
