Tipperary

Flavor Notes

HerbalBitterFruityWoody

Ingredients

  • Irish Whiskey45 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Green Chartreuse15 ml

Garnish

Orange peel

Instructions

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass.

A Pre-Prohibition Classic

The Tipperary is a classic cocktail featuring Irish whiskey, sweet vermouth, and Green Chartreuse. It's a spirit-forward drink with a complex herbal and slightly sweet profile. Often described as a Manhattan variation, it offers a unique depth of flavor.
