Flavor Notes

YeastyGrassyBitter

Ingredients

  • Vodka50 ml

Garnish

None

Instructions

Pour vodka into the glass, then top with beer.

Simple and Potent Combination

The Yorsh is a rudimentary mix of beer and vodka, often consumed for its high alcoholic content and ease of preparation.

  • Typically uses lager-style beer.
  • Ratio can vary based on preference.
  • Consumed quickly.
