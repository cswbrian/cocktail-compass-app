Claret Cup

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusHerbalSpicyWoody

Ingredients

  • Red Wine750 ml
  • Orange Liqueur (e.g., Triple Sec)60 ml
  • Orange1 pc
  • Lemon1 pc
  • Berries (Strawberries, Raspberries)100 g
  • Cinnamon Sticks2 pc
  • Mint Sprigs5 pc
  • Sugar100 g

Garnish

Orange slices, lemon slices, berries, mint sprigs

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a punch bowl. Add ice and top with sparkling water or lemonade before serving.

Refreshing Summer Beverage

A large format cocktail, perfect for parties:

  • Combines red wine with various fruits and flavorings
  • Often topped with sparkling water or lemonade for effervescence
  • Can be customized with different herbs and spices
