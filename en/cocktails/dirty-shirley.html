Dirty Shirley

Flavor Notes

FruityVanillaCitrus

Ingredients

  • Vodka45 ml
  • Grenadine30 ml
  • Ginger Ale90 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Garnish

Maraschino cherry

Instructions

Build. Add vodka and grenadine to a highball glass filled with ice. Top with ginger ale. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

A Sweet and Simple Classic

The Dirty Shirley is a playful variation on the Shirley Temple cocktail. It offers:

  • A sweet and nostalgic flavor profile.
  • An easy-to-make and approachable cocktail.
  • A vibrant and visually appealing presentation.
