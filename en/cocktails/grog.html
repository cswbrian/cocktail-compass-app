Grog

Flavor Notes

SpicyTropicalWoodyFruityHerbal

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Tablespoon of Brown Sugar1 tbsp
  • Hot Water120 ml
  • Dash of Nutmeg1 dash

Garnish

Orange slice and cinnamon stick

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a mug and stir until sugar is dissolved.

A Simple, Warming Sailor's Drink

A classic concoction traditionally made with rum, water, sugar, and spices. It's a comforting and customizable beverage, often enjoyed during colder months or on long sea voyages. The recipe varies, but the core elements remain the same:

  • Rum as the base spirit.
  • Sweetness from sugar or honey.
  • Dilution with water or sometimes tea.
  • Spices for added warmth and flavor, such as cinnamon, nutmeg, or cloves.
