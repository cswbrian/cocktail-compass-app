Hoppel Poppel

Flavor Notes

CreamyVanillaNuttySpicyFruity

Ingredients

  • Rum45 ml
  • Vanilla Extract15 ml
  • Egg yolks2 pc
  • Sugar15 g
  • Milk60 ml
  • Cream30 ml
  • Almonds2 pc

Garnish

Grated nutmeg or cinnamon stick

Instructions

Whisk egg yolks and sugar, then gradually add milk, cream, rum, and vanilla extract. Heat gently without boiling. Garnish.

Hoppel Poppel: A German-Inspired Egg Nog

A rich and creamy egg-based drink, similar to eggnog but with a distinctive flavor profile. It's a comforting and indulgent beverage, typically served warm.

  • Features rum for a warming kick
  • Milk and cream for richness
  • Eggs for texture and flavor
