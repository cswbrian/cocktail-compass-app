Breakfast Martini

Flavor Notes

CitrusFruityBitterHerbal

Ingredients

  • Gin50 ml
  • Cointreau15 ml
  • Orange Marmalade2 bar spoon
  • Lemon Juice15 ml

Garnish

Orange peel

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and fine strain into chilled glass.

Modern Classic Cocktail

A sophisticated and tart cocktail perfect for brunch, featuring:

  • Gin base for a classic feel
  • Orange marmalade for sweetness and citrus notes
  • Lemon juice for acidity
  • Cointreau for added orange complexity
