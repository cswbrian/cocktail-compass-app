Cocktails with Water

Bumbo

SpicySweetTropical
  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Water15 ml
  • Brown Sugar15 ml

Preparation

In a mug or heat-resistant glass, dissolve brown sugar in warm water. Add dark rum. Stir well. Grate fresh nutmeg over the top. Serve warm.

Garnish

Grated nutmeg

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 3

Body: 5

Complexity: 4

Booziness: 6

Fish House Punch

FruityCitrusTropicalWoodySpicy
  • Dark Jamaican Rum250 ml
  • Cognac250 ml
  • Peach Brandy250 ml
  • Lemon Juice375 ml
  • Simple Syrup (1:1)250 ml
  • Water750 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a large punch bowl. Add a large block of ice. Stir well until chilled and slightly diluted. Let sit for at least 1 hour for flavors to meld. Add lemon slices and/or grated nutmeg before serving. Ladle into punch glasses or cups.

Garnish

Lemon slices and/or grated nutmeg floated on top.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 6

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 7

Sherry Cobbler

FruityCitrusNuttyWoodyFloral
  • Oloroso Sherry90 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp
  • Water3 ml

Preparation

In a shaker, muddle sugar with water until sugar is dissolved. Add Oloroso sherry. Fill the shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a tall glass filled with shaved or crushed ice. Garnish generously with assorted fresh fruit and a mint sprig. Dust with powdered sugar.

Garnish

Assorted fresh fruit (berries, orange slices, lemon slices), mint sprig, powdered sugar dusting

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 3

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 2

Old Fashioned

BitterWoodySpicyVanillaCitrus
  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Water15 ml

Preparation

Place sugar cube in an old-fashioned glass. Add bitters and water. Muddle until sugar is dissolved. Fill the glass with ice. Add bourbon. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with an orange peel, expressed and inserted. Optionally add a brandied cherry.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and inserted, or optional brandied cherry

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 2

Body: 7

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 8

Quentão

SpicyFruityCitrusWoodySweet
  • Cachaça500 ml
  • Water200 ml
  • Granulated Sugar200 g
  • Fresh Ginger50 g
  • Cinnamon Sticks3 pc
  • Whole Cloves6 pc
  • Orange1 pc
  • Lime1 pc

Preparation

1. Slice the ginger into thin coins. Cut the orange and lime into quarters. 2. In a large pot, combine the water, sugar, ginger, cinnamon sticks, and cloves. 3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring until the sugar is dissolved. 4. Reduce the heat to low and add the orange and lime pieces. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse. 5. Stir in the Cachaça. 6. Heat gently for another 5 minutes, being careful not to boil off the alcohol. (The mixture is supposed to be gently heated). 7. Strain the mixture to remove the solids. 8. Serve hot in mugs or heat-resistant glasses. Garnish as specified.

Garnish

Orange slices studded with cloves and cinnamon sticks.

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 3

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 4

Brandy Old Fashioned

FruitySpicyWoodyVanilla
  • Brandy60 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Water30 ml

Preparation

Place sugar cube in an old fashioned glass. Saturate with Angostura bitters. Add water. Muddle until sugar is dissolved. Fill the glass with ice. Add brandy. Stir gently to combine. Garnish.

Garnish

Orange peel and/or Maraschino cherry. Express the oils from the orange peel over the drink and optionally add the cherry.

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 2

Body: 5

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 6
