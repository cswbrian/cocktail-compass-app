Cocktails with Apricot Brandy

Paradise

FruityCitrusFloral
  • Gin35 ml
  • Apricot Brandy15 ml
  • Orange Juice20 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker. Fill with ice and shake well until chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and use as garnish.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 6

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 6

My Fair Lady

FruityCitrusFloralSweetSour
  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Brandy15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml
  • Grenadine7 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, dry shake all ingredients vigorously for 15 seconds to emulsify. 2. Add ice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled (approximately 15-20 seconds). 4. Double strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass. 5. Garnish with a lemon twist or an edible flower.

Garnish

Lemon twist or edible flower (e.g., pansy)

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 6

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 4

Old Etonian

FruityCitrusHerbalBitter
  • Gin30 ml
  • Apricot Brandy15 ml
  • Lillet Blanc30 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Chill a coupe or cocktail glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange twist over the drink and discard.

Garnish

Orange twist, expressed and discarded

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 6
