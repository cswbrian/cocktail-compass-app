Cocktails with Pickle Brine

Pickleback

SaltySourSavory
  • Whiskey45 ml
  • Pickle Brine45 ml

Preparation

1. Pour the whiskey into a shot glass. 2. Pour the pickle brine into a separate shot glass. 3. Drink the whiskey shot. 4. Immediately follow with the pickle brine shot.

Garnish

Optional: A small pickle spear or slice on the side.
