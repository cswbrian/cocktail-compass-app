Cocktails with Oloroso Sherry

Sherry Cobbler

FruityCitrusNuttyWoodyFloral
  • Oloroso Sherry90 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp
  • Water3 ml

Preparation

In a shaker, muddle sugar with water until sugar is dissolved. Add Oloroso sherry. Fill the shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a tall glass filled with shaved or crushed ice. Garnish generously with assorted fresh fruit and a mint sprig. Dust with powdered sugar.

Garnish

Assorted fresh fruit (berries, orange slices, lemon slices), mint sprig, powdered sugar dusting

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 3

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 2
