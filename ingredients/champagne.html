Cocktails with Champagne

Black Velvet

YeastyBitterRoastedFruityCreamy
  • Champagne90 ml
  • Stout90 ml

Preparation

1. Chill both stout and champagne. 2. Gently pour chilled champagne into a flute glass, filling halfway. 3. Slowly pour the chilled stout over the back of a spoon so that it layers on top of the champagne, filling the glass. Be careful to maintain the separation of the liquids.

Garnish

None

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 2

Body: 7

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 6

Mimosa

FruityCitrusYeasty
  • Champagne75 ml
  • Orange Juice75 ml

Preparation

1. Chill Champagne and orange juice thoroughly. 2. Gently pour equal parts of chilled Champagne and orange juice into a champagne flute. 3. Stir gently to combine. 4. Garnish with an orange slice or zest if desired. Serve immediately.

Garnish

Orange slice or zest, optional

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 5

Body: 3

Complexity: 4

Booziness: 2

Death in the Afternoon

HerbalAniseFruityFloral
  • Absinthe45 ml
  • Champagne120 ml

Preparation

Pour absinthe into a Champagne flute. Top with chilled Champagne. Gently stir until the drink turns opalescent.

Garnish

None typically, though a lemon twist is sometimes used as a modern addition.

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 2

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 8

Hangman's Blood

BitterFruitySpicySmokyYeasty
  • Gin25 ml
  • Rum25 ml
  • Vodka25 ml
  • Whiskey25 ml
  • Tequila25 ml
  • Stout100 ml
  • Champagne100 ml

Preparation

Combine all base spirits in a shaker with ice. Stir gently. Strain into a highball or large glass filled with ice. Top with stout, then gently add the champagne. Stir carefully to combine without losing too much fizz.

Garnish

Orange slice

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 9
