Cocktails with Orange Curaçao

Mai Tai

TropicalNuttyCitrusFruityHerbal
  • Aged Jamaican Rum30 ml
  • Aged Rhum Agricole30 ml
  • Orange Curaçao15 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice22 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a double rocks glass filled with crushed ice. Garnish as directed.

Garnish

Mint sprig and lime wheel, express lime peel over the drink.

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 6

Body: 6

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 6

The Blenheim

CitrusHerbalBitter
  • Gin60 ml
  • Orange Curaçao7 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until chilled, approximately 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

Garnish

Orange twist, expressed over the drink and then placed on the rim.

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 3

Body: 4

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 6

Chicago Cocktail

FruityCitrusBitterHerbal
  • Brandy45 ml
  • Orange Curaçao15 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

In an old-fashioned glass, muddle the sugar cube with the bitters and a little water until dissolved. Add the brandy and Orange Curaçao. Fill the glass with ice. Stir gently to combine and chill. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice and maraschino cherry

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 7

Suffering Bastard

HerbalSpicyCitrusFruity
  • Gin15 ml
  • Brandy15 ml
  • Orange Curaçao15 ml
  • Lime Juice20 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Ginger Ale90 ml

Preparation

Add gin, brandy, orange curaçao, and lime juice to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. Top with ginger ale. Dash Angostura bitters on top. Garnish with a mint sprig and cucumber slice.

Garnish

Mint sprig, cucumber slice, and a dash of Angostura bitters on top

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 6

Body: 4

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5

El Presidente

FruityCitrusHerbalSweet
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Orange Curaçao7 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Grenadine1 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and place the peel in the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5
