Cocktails with Dry Sherry

Bamboo Cocktail

HerbalNuttyDryFloralWoody
  • Dry Sherry45 ml
  • Dry Vermouth45 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. Add sherry, vermouth, orange bitters, and Angostura bitters to a mixing glass filled with ice. 3. Stir well until chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). 4. Strain into the chilled glass. 5. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and drop it in.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped in.
