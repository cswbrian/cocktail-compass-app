Cocktails with Apple Brandy

Diki-Diki

FruityCitrusSpicySweetTart
  • Apple Brandy45 ml
  • Swedish Punsch15 ml
  • Fresh Grapefruit Juice22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

Garnish

Grapefruit twist, expressed over the drink

Cooperstown

FruityCitrusHerbalSweet
  • Apple Brandy45 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Maple Syrup7 ml
  • Aromatic Bitters2 dashes
  • Absinthe1 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with an apple slice or lemon twist.

Garnish

Apple slice or lemon twist

Jack Rose

FruityCitrusSweetSour
  • Apple Brandy60 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Garnish

Apple slice or a lemon twist

Widow's Kiss

FruityHerbalSpicyFloralBitter
  • Apple Brandy30 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Yellow Chartreuse15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Maraschino cherry
