Cocktails with Amaro Averna

Averna Spritz

BitterCitrusHerbalFruity
  • Amaro Averna90 ml
  • Prosecco60 ml
  • Soda Water30 ml

Preparation

Fill a wine glass with ice. Add Amaro Averna. Top with Prosecco, then soda water. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with an orange slice.

Garnish

Orange slice
