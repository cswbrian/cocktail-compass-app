Cocktails with Ice Cream

Bushwacker

TropicalNuttyChocolateCoffeeVanillaCreamy
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur30 ml
  • Chocolate Liqueur30 ml
  • Ice Cream60 ml
  • Milk60 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and garnish.

Garnish

Grated nutmeg and a drizzle of chocolate syrup.
