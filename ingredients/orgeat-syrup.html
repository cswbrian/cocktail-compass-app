Trinidad Sour

BitterSpicyCitrusNuttyHerbal
  • Angostura Bitters45 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup30 ml
  • Rye Whiskey15 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously until well-chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a mint sprig and a few extra dashes of Angostura bitters on top.

Garnish

Mint sprig and 2-3 dashes of Angostura bitters on top of the foam.

Flaming Volcano

TropicalFruitySweetSpicy
  • Light Rum60 ml
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Brandy30 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Orange Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc

Preparation

1. Combine light rum, dark rum, brandy, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and orgeat syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until chilled. 3. Strain into a volcano bowl or large serving dish. 4. Soak a sugar cube in high-proof rum (at least 75% ABV). 5. Place the sugar cube on a heat-resistant surface in the center of the bowl. 6. Carefully ignite the sugar cube using a long lighter or match. 7. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and cherry.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge, cherry, and a flaming sugar cube soaked in high-proof rum. Ensure sugar cube is placed on a heat-resistant surface within the bowl before ignition.
