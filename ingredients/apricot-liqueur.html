Cocktails with Apricot Liqueur

Cloister

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur22 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Egg White15 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker without ice and dry shake vigorously to emulsify the egg white. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass. Express oils from a lemon twist over the drink and use as garnish.

Garnish

Lemon twist

Angel Face

FruityFloralHerbalCitrus
  • Gin20 ml
  • Brandy20 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur20 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. Add all ingredients to a shaker filled with ice. 3. Shake well until well-chilled. 4. Double strain into the chilled glass. 5. Garnish (optional).

Garnish

Apple slice or brandied cherry (optional)

Delilah

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur15 ml
  • Lillet Blanc15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or a brandied cherry.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim, or a brandied cherry.

Moon River

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine gin, apricot liqueur, and lemon juice in a shaker with ice. Shake well until well-chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express a lemon twist over the drink and use it as garnish.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim.
