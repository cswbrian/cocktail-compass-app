Cocktails with Maple Syrup

Smoked Maple Old Fashioned

SmokyWoodySweetVanillaSpicyCitrus
  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Maple Syrup5 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Smoke the bourbon: Use a smoking gun to infuse the bourbon with smoke flavor, then strain into a mixing glass. 2. Add maple syrup and bitters to the mixing glass. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass. 4. Stir gently until well-chilled. 5. Strain into a rocks glass filled with one large ice cube. 6. Express an orange peel over the drink and garnish.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and inserted, optionally brandied cherry.

Last of the Mohicans

WoodyVanillaSpicyCitrusChocolateMaple
  • Bourbon45 ml
  • Chocolate Bitters2 ml
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Combine bourbon, maple syrup, and chocolate bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass filled with a large ice cube. Express the oils from an orange zest over the drink and place on the rim.

Garnish

Orange zest twist, expressed over the drink and then placed on the rim.

Cooperstown

FruityCitrusHerbalSweet
  • Apple Brandy45 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Maple Syrup7 ml
  • Aromatic Bitters2 dashes
  • Absinthe1 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with an apple slice or lemon twist.

Garnish

Apple slice or lemon twist

Four Score

NuttyWoodyVanillaCaramelBitterSpicySweet
  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Nocino (Walnut Liqueur)15 ml
  • Black Walnut Bitters2 dashes
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled, about 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Express the oil from an orange peel over the drink, then discard the peel. Garnish with a brandied cherry.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and discarded, plus a brandied cherry.

Campfire Cocktail

SmokyVanillaMapleChocolateWoodySweet
  • Bourbon Whiskey45 ml
  • Smoked Liquor10 ml
  • Chocolate Bitters2 dashes
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a rocks glass. 2. Add bourbon, smoked liquor, maple syrup, and chocolate bitters to a mixing glass. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass. 4. Stir until well-chilled (approx. 20 seconds). 5. Strain into the chilled rocks glass filled with one large ice cube. 6. Toast a marshmallow and place it on a skewer. 7. Garnish with the toasted marshmallow.

Garnish

Toasted Marshmallow, skewered
