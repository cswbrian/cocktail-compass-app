Cocktails with Raspberry Syrup

Clover Club

FruityCitrusFloralSourSweet
  • Gin45 ml
  • Raspberry Syrup15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Egg White15 ml

Preparation

1. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker without ice (dry shake). 2. Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake again until well-chilled (about 15 seconds). 5. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass.

Garnish

Three fresh raspberries, skewered.

Clover Club

FruityCitrusFloralHerbalCreamy
  • Gin45 ml
  • Raspberry Syrup15 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Egg White15 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker without ice (dry shake). 3. Vigorously shake for about 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 4. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. 5. Double strain into the chilled glass. 6. Garnish with fresh raspberries or a lemon twist.

Garnish

3 fresh raspberries skewered on a cocktail pick or a delicate lemon twist.
