Cocktails with Coffee Liqueur

Mr. Bali Hai

TropicalCoffeeSpicyFruity
  • Dark Rum45 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur30 ml
  • Pineapple Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Angostura bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a Mr. Bali Hai mug filled with crushed ice.

Garnish

A Mr. Bali Hai mug (or suitable tiki mug) with a pineapple spear, a cocktail umbrella, and a fresh orchid.

Black Russian

CoffeeBitterSweet
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml

Preparation

Fill an old-fashioned glass with ice cubes. Add vodka and coffee liqueur. Stir gently to combine.

Garnish

None traditionally. Optional: Coffee beans

Red Russian

CoffeeSweetCreamyChocolate
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur25 ml
  • Heavy Cream25 ml

Preparation

Fill an old-fashioned glass with ice. Add vodka and coffee liqueur. Gently pour cream over the top. Stir gently to combine before serving, if desired. Alternatively serve in layers for visual appeal.

Garnish

None typically, but a coffee bean or dusting of cocoa powder are acceptable.

Smoked Vanilla Espresso Martini

CoffeeVanillaSmokySweetBitter
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Freshly Brewed Espresso (cooled)30 ml
  • Vanilla Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Chill a martini glass. Combine vodka, coffee liqueur, vanilla syrup, and cooled espresso in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously until well-chilled. Double strain into the chilled martini glass. Garnish with 3 coffee beans and a smoked vanilla bean, or sprinkle a pinch of smoked salt on top.

Garnish

3 coffee beans and a smoked vanilla bean, if available, or a pinch of smoked salt sprinkled on top of the foam.

White Russian

CoffeeCreamySweetVanillaChocolate
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur30 ml
  • Heavy Cream30 ml

Preparation

Fill a rocks glass with ice. Add vodka and coffee liqueur. Gently pour heavy cream over the top. Stir lightly to combine, being careful not to overmix.

Garnish

None typically, but can be garnished with coffee beans or a light dusting of cocoa powder for visual appeal.

B-52

CoffeeChocolateCreamyCitrusVanilla
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Irish Cream20 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass using the back of a spoon to prevent mixing. First, pour the coffee liqueur into the shot glass. Next, gently layer the Irish cream on top of the coffee liqueur by pouring it slowly over the back of a spoon. Finally, layer the orange liqueur on top of the Irish cream, again using the back of a spoon to maintain distinct layers. For an optional flaming effect, float a small amount of high-proof spirit on top of the orange liqueur and ignite it just before serving.

Garnish

None typically, can be flamed for visual effect (optional)

Espresso Martini

CoffeeChocolateBitterVanillaCreamy
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Freshly Brewed Espresso30 ml
  • Simple Syrup5 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a martini glass or coupe. 2. Combine vodka, freshly brewed espresso, coffee liqueur, and simple syrup into a cocktail shaker. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Close and shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to create a frothy crema. 5. Double strain into the chilled glass. 6. Garnish with three coffee beans, floated on top of the crema.

Garnish

3 coffee beans, floated on top of the crema, arranged artfully

Baby Guinness

ChocolateCoffeeCreamyVanilla
  • Coffee Liqueur45 ml
  • Irish Cream Liqueur15 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the Irish cream liqueur on top of the coffee liqueur in a shot glass. Tilt a spoon upside down over the coffee liqueur and gently pour the Irish cream over the back of the spoon to ensure it floats. Serve immediately.

Garnish

None
