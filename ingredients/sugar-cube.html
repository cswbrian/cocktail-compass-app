Cocktails with Sugar Cube

Sazerac

HerbalSpicyBitterCitrusWoody
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Absinthe7 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Peychaud's Bitters3 dashes

Preparation

Chill an Old Fashioned glass. In a separate mixing glass, muddle the sugar cube with the Peychaud's Bitters. Add the rye whiskey and ice. Stir well until chilled. Rinse the chilled Old Fashioned glass with the absinthe, coating the inside of the glass, then discard the excess. Strain the whiskey mixture into the absinthe-rinsed glass. Express the oil from the lemon peel over the glass and discard.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the glass and discarded. The oils add aroma and a hint of citrus without adding juice.

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 2

Body: 7

Complexity: 9

Booziness: 8

Old Fashioned

BitterWoodySpicyVanillaCitrus
  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Water15 ml

Preparation

Place sugar cube in an old-fashioned glass. Add bitters and water. Muddle until sugar is dissolved. Fill the glass with ice. Add bourbon. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with an orange peel, expressed and inserted. Optionally add a brandied cherry.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and inserted, or optional brandied cherry

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 2

Body: 7

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 8

Flaming Volcano

TropicalFruitySweetSpicy
  • Light Rum60 ml
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Brandy30 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Orange Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc

Preparation

1. Combine light rum, dark rum, brandy, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and orgeat syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until chilled. 3. Strain into a volcano bowl or large serving dish. 4. Soak a sugar cube in high-proof rum (at least 75% ABV). 5. Place the sugar cube on a heat-resistant surface in the center of the bowl. 6. Carefully ignite the sugar cube using a long lighter or match. 7. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and cherry.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge, cherry, and a flaming sugar cube soaked in high-proof rum. Ensure sugar cube is placed on a heat-resistant surface within the bowl before ignition.

Sweetness: 8

Sourness: 4

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 8

Chicago Cocktail

FruityCitrusBitterHerbal
  • Brandy45 ml
  • Orange Curaçao15 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

In an old-fashioned glass, muddle the sugar cube with the bitters and a little water until dissolved. Add the brandy and Orange Curaçao. Fill the glass with ice. Stir gently to combine and chill. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice and maraschino cherry

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 7

Brandy Old Fashioned

FruitySpicyWoodyVanilla
  • Brandy60 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Water30 ml

Preparation

Place sugar cube in an old fashioned glass. Saturate with Angostura bitters. Add water. Muddle until sugar is dissolved. Fill the glass with ice. Add brandy. Stir gently to combine. Garnish.

Garnish

Orange peel and/or Maraschino cherry. Express the oils from the orange peel over the drink and optionally add the cherry.

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 2

Body: 5

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 6
