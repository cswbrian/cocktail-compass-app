Cocktails with Vanilla Syrup

Smoked Vanilla Espresso Martini

CoffeeVanillaSmokySweetBitter
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Freshly Brewed Espresso (cooled)30 ml
  • Vanilla Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Chill a martini glass. Combine vodka, coffee liqueur, vanilla syrup, and cooled espresso in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously until well-chilled. Double strain into the chilled martini glass. Garnish with 3 coffee beans and a smoked vanilla bean, or sprinkle a pinch of smoked salt on top.

Garnish

3 coffee beans and a smoked vanilla bean, if available, or a pinch of smoked salt sprinkled on top of the foam.
