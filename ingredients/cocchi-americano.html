Cocktails with Cocchi Americano

Vesper

CitrusFloralBitterHerbalWoody
  • Gin60 ml
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Cocchi Americano7 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine gin, vodka, and Cocchi Americano in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled (approximately 20 seconds). Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and place the twist in the glass.

Garnish

Large lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and placed in the glass, releasing citrus oils.
