Cocktails with Sparkling Wine

Royal Arrival

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal
  • Gin30 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur15 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Simple Syrup10 ml
  • Sparkling Wine60 ml

Preparation

Combine gin, elderflower liqueur, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or champagne flute. Top with sparkling wine. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 5

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 4

Old Cuban

FruityCitrusHerbalSpicyTropical
  • Aged Rum45 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Mint Leaves6 leaves
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Sparkling Wine60 ml

Preparation

Gently muddle mint leaves with simple syrup in a shaker. Add aged rum, lime juice, and Angostura bitters. Fill the shaker with ice and shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass. Top with sparkling wine. Garnish with a mint sprig (slapped gently to release aromas).

Garnish

Mint sprig (slapped gently to release aromas)

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 6

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5

Campari Spritz

BitterFruityCitrusHerbal
  • Campari90 ml
  • Sparkling Wine60 ml
  • Soda Water30 ml

Preparation

Fill a wine glass with ice. Add the Campari. Top with sparkling wine, then add soda water. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with an orange slice.

Garnish

Orange slice, placed on the rim of the glass.

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 4

Body: 3

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 3
