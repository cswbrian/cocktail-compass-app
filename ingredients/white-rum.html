Cocktails with White Rum

Piña Colada

TropicalFruityCreamySweet
  • White Rum50 ml
  • Pineapple Juice100 ml
  • Cream of Coconut50 ml

Preparation

Combine white rum, pineapple juice, and cream of coconut in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a glass filled with ice. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a Maraschino cherry. Optionally, a paper umbrella can be added for visual appeal.

Sweetness: 9

Sourness: 2

Body: 8

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 4

Mojito

CitrusHerbalTropicalSweet
  • White Rum60 ml
  • Mint Leaves12 Leaves
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp
  • Soda Water60 ml

Preparation

1. Gently muddle the mint leaves with sugar in a sturdy glass. Avoid over-muddling, which can make the mint bitter. 2. Add lime juice and rum to the glass. 3. Fill the glass with ice. 4. Top with soda water. 5. Gently stir to combine. 6. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge.

Garnish

Mint sprig and lime wedge

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 7

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 3

Between the Sheets

CitrusFruityWarmSweetSour
  • White Rum22 ml
  • Brandy22 ml
  • Orange Liqueur22 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients (White Rum, Brandy, Orange Liqueur, and Lemon Juice) into a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake well until well-chilled, approximately 15-20 seconds. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express lemon peel over the drink and drop it into the glass.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped into the glass.

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 7

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 7

U-Boot

SweetYeastySpicyMalty
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • White Rum30 ml
  • Light Lager Beer250 ml

Preparation

Pour the light lager into a pint glass. Carefully drop the shot glass containing both rums into the beer, allowing it to sink to the bottom. Serve immediately and drink responsibly.

Garnish

None typically, though a lime wedge on the rim is sometimes added

Cuban Sunset

FruityTropicalCitrusSweet
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a shaker with ice. 2. Add white rum, pineapple juice, and lime juice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled. 4. Strain into a chilled glass (such as a hurricane glass or highball glass) filled with ice. 5. Gently pour grenadine down the side of the glass so that it settles at the bottom, creating a layered effect.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry on a cocktail pick.

Body: 4

Three-Mile Long Island Iced Tea

CitrusFruityHerbalWoodySmoky
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Gin15 ml
  • White Rum15 ml
  • Tequila15 ml
  • Whiskey15 ml
  • Triple Sec15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Cola75 ml

Preparation

1. Combine all base spirits (vodka, gin, rum, tequila, and whiskey), triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 3. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice. 4. Top with cola. 5. Garnish with a lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry. Prepare by slicing a fresh lemon into wedges. Optionally, add a maraschino cherry to the rim of the glass for visual appeal.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 6

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 9

El Presidente

FruityCitrusHerbalSweet
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Orange Curaçao7 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Grenadine1 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and place the peel in the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5
