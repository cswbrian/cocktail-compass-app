Traditionally, the Paloma is made with tequila, grapefruit soda (such as Jarritos or Squirt), and lime juice, served over ice with a salt rim. Today, the Paloma is enjoyed worldwide as a refreshing and easy-to-make cocktail, perfect for warm weather and casual gatherings.
