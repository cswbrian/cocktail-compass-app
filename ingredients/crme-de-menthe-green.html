Cocktails with Crème de Menthe (Green)

Springbokkie

VanillaMintCreamyChocolate
  • Crème de Menthe (Green)25 ml
  • Amarula Cream Liqueur25 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the Amarula Cream Liqueur on top of the Crème de Menthe in a shot glass, using the back of a spoon to help prevent mixing. The goal is to maintain distinct layers of green and cream.

Garnish

None typically, but a dusting of cocoa powder or chocolate shavings can be added for visual appeal.

Sweetness: 8

Sourness: 1

Body: 6

Complexity: 3

Booziness: 3
