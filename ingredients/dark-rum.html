Bumbo

SpicySweetTropical

Preparation

Flaming Volcano

TropicalFruitySweetSpicy
  • Light Rum60 ml
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Brandy30 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Orange Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc

Preparation

1. Combine light rum, dark rum, brandy, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and orgeat syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until chilled. 3. Strain into a volcano bowl or large serving dish. 4. Soak a sugar cube in high-proof rum (at least 75% ABV). 5. Place the sugar cube on a heat-resistant surface in the center of the bowl. 6. Carefully ignite the sugar cube using a long lighter or match. 7. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and cherry.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge, cherry, and a flaming sugar cube soaked in high-proof rum. Ensure sugar cube is placed on a heat-resistant surface within the bowl before ignition.

Tom and Jerry

CreamySpicySweetVanillaNuttyWoody
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Cognac15 ml
  • Egg2 whole
  • Sugar20 g
  • Ground Cinnamon0 pinch
  • Ground Nutmeg0 pinch
  • Ground Cloves0 pinch
  • Hot Milk120 ml

Preparation

1. Separate the egg white and yolk. 2. Whisk the egg white until stiff peaks form. Gradually add half of the sugar while whisking. Set aside. 3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolk with the remaining sugar until light and creamy. Add the cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves to the yolk mixture and combine. 4. Gently fold the egg white mixture into the yolk mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. 5. In a preheated mug, combine the dark rum and cognac. 6. Add 2-3 tablespoons of the batter to the mug. 7. Top with hot milk, stirring gently to combine. 8. Grate fresh nutmeg on top for garnish.

Garnish

Freshly grated nutmeg on top
