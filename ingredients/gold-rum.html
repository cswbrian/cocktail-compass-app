Cocktails with Gold Rum

Barracuda

FruityTropicalSweetCitrusVanillaHerbal
  • Gold Rum45 ml
  • Galliano15 ml
  • Prosecco60 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice7 ml

Preparation

Combine rum, pineapple juice, lime juice, and Galliano in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled champagne flute or coupe glass. Top with Prosecco. Garnish with a lime or pineapple wedge.

Garnish

Lime wedge or Pineapple wedge

Zombie

TropicalFruitySweetSpicyHerbal
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Dark Rum45 ml
  • Gold Rum30 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur7 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Falernum15 ml
  • Grenadine10 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml

Preparation

1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until chilled. 3. Strain into a zombie glass filled with ice cubes. 4. Garnish with a mint sprig, cherry, and orange slice.

Garnish

Mint sprig, cherry, orange slice
