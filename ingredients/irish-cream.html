Cocktails with Irish Cream

Slippery Nipple

SweetCreamyVanillaFruity
  • Sambuca20 ml
  • Irish Cream20 ml
  • Grenadine5 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass. First, pour the grenadine into the bottom of the shot glass. Next, gently pour the sambuca on top of the grenadine, being careful not to mix the layers. Finally, slowly layer the Irish cream on top of the sambuca, creating a distinct creamy layer.

Garnish

None

B-52

CoffeeChocolateCreamyCitrusVanilla
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Irish Cream20 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass using the back of a spoon to prevent mixing. First, pour the coffee liqueur into the shot glass. Next, gently layer the Irish cream on top of the coffee liqueur by pouring it slowly over the back of a spoon. Finally, layer the orange liqueur on top of the Irish cream, again using the back of a spoon to maintain distinct layers. For an optional flaming effect, float a small amount of high-proof spirit on top of the orange liqueur and ignite it just before serving.

Garnish

None typically, can be flamed for visual effect (optional)

Quick Fuck

SweetCreamyCoffeeNutty
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur15 ml
  • Irish Cream15 ml
  • Amaretto15 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass in the following order: coffee liqueur, Irish cream, and then amaretto. Pour slowly over the back of a spoon to maintain distinct layers.

Garnish

None
