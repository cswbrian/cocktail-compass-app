Cocktails with Dark Crème de Cacao

Brandy Alexander

ChocolateVanillaCreamyNutty
  • Brandy30 ml
  • Dark Crème de Cacao30 ml
  • Heavy Cream30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass. Garnish with freshly grated nutmeg.

Garnish

Freshly grated nutmeg sprinkled on top.

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 1

Body: 8

Complexity: 5
