Cocktails with Cherry Heering

Wolfram

SpicyFruityHerbalBitterCitrus
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Cherry Heering15 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or Nick & Nora glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and drop it in.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 5

Body: 7

Complexity: 9

Booziness: 8

Blood & Sand

FruityCitrusSmokyHerbal
  • Scotch Whisky22 ml
  • Cherry Heering22 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth22 ml
  • Fresh Orange Juice22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim.

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 5

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5
