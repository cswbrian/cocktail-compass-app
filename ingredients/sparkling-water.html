Cocktails with Sparkling Water

Spritzer

FruityCitrusFloralGrassyYeasty
  • White Wine60 ml
  • Sparkling Water60 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Fill a wine glass with ice. Add white wine and simple syrup. Top with sparkling water. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with a slice of lemon or lime, or a sprig of mint.

Garnish

Slice of lemon or lime, or a sprig of mint
