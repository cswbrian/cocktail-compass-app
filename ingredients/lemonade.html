Cocktails with Lemonade

Pimm's Cup

FruityCitrusHerbalSpicyBitter
  • Pimm's No. 150 ml
  • Lemonade150 ml
  • Cucumber2 slices
  • Orange2 slices
  • Strawberry2 halves
  • Mint5 leaves

Preparation

Fill a highball glass or jug with ice. Add Pimm's No. 1. Top with lemonade. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with cucumber ribbons, orange slices, strawberry halves, and a sprig of mint.

Garnish

Cucumber ribbons, orange slices, strawberry halves, and a sprig of mint.

Shandy

FruityCitrusGrassyYeasty
  • Beer150 ml
  • Lemonade150 ml

Preparation

1. Chill both the beer and lemonade. 2. Pour beer into a glass. 3. Top with lemonade. 4. Gently stir to combine.

Garnish
