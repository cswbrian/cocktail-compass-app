Cocktails with Cream of Coconut

Piña Colada

TropicalFruityCreamySweet
  • White Rum50 ml
  • Pineapple Juice100 ml
  • Cream of Coconut50 ml

Preparation

Combine white rum, pineapple juice, and cream of coconut in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a glass filled with ice. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a Maraschino cherry. Optionally, a paper umbrella can be added for visual appeal.

Sweetness: 9

Sourness: 2

Body: 8

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 4

Blue Hawaiian

FruityTropicalSweetCitrus
  • White Rum30 ml
  • Vodka30 ml
  • Blue Curaçao30 ml
  • Cream of Coconut30 ml
  • Pineapple Juice90 ml

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a blender with 1 cup of ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into a tall glass (such as a hurricane glass).

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry

Sweetness: 8

Sourness: 4

Body: 5

Complexity: 4

Booziness: 5
