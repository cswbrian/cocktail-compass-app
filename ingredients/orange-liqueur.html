Cocktails with Orange Liqueur

Breakfast Martini

CitrusFruityBitterSweet
  • Gin50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur10 ml
  • Orange Marmalade1 bar spoon
  • Fresh Lemon Juice15 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a martini glass. 2. Add orange marmalade, gin, orange liqueur, and lemon juice to a shaker with ice. 3. Shake well until chilled. 4. Double strain into the chilled martini glass. 5. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and discard.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and discarded (optional: lightly sugar rim)

Corpse Reviver #2

CitrusHerbalFloralBitter
  • Gin22 ml
  • Orange Liqueur22 ml
  • Lillet Blanc22 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and then drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped into the glass.

Blueberry Tea

NuttyFruityCitrusSweetWarm
  • Amaretto15 ml
  • Orange Liqueur15 ml
  • Hot Water90 ml

Preparation

Combine amaretto and orange liqueur in a mug. Top with hot water. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with an orange slice or lemon twist.

Garnish

Orange slice or lemon twist

Between the Sheets

CitrusFruityWarmSweetSour
  • White Rum22 ml
  • Brandy22 ml
  • Orange Liqueur22 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients (White Rum, Brandy, Orange Liqueur, and Lemon Juice) into a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake well until well-chilled, approximately 15-20 seconds. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express lemon peel over the drink and drop it into the glass.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped into the glass.

Smoked Pineapple Margarita

SmokyFruityCitrusTropicalSweetSour
  • Blanco Tequila50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lime Juice50 ml
  • Smoked Pineapple Chunks50 grams
  • Agave Nectar15 ml

Preparation

1. Smoke pineapple chunks using a smoker or grill until lightly charred. 2. Muddle smoked pineapple chunks in a shaker. 3. Add tequila, fresh lime juice, orange liqueur and agave nectar. 4. Add ice and shake well until chilled. 5. Double strain into a salt-rimmed (optional) rocks glass filled with ice. 6. Garnish with a pineapple wedge.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge, salt rim (optional)

Margarita

CitrusFruitySourSalty
  • Tequila50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur25 ml
  • Lime Juice25 ml

Preparation

If desired, salt the rim of a chilled cocktail glass by running a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in salt. Combine tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into the prepared glass filled with ice. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Garnish

Lime wedge; optional: salt rim (coarse salt preferred)

B-52

CoffeeChocolateCreamyCitrusVanilla
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Irish Cream20 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass using the back of a spoon to prevent mixing. First, pour the coffee liqueur into the shot glass. Next, gently layer the Irish cream on top of the coffee liqueur by pouring it slowly over the back of a spoon. Finally, layer the orange liqueur on top of the Irish cream, again using the back of a spoon to maintain distinct layers. For an optional flaming effect, float a small amount of high-proof spirit on top of the orange liqueur and ignite it just before serving.

Garnish

None typically, can be flamed for visual effect (optional)

Smoked Fig and Bourbon Sour

SmokyFruityCitrusWoodyVanilla
  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Orange Liqueur7 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Egg White15 ml
  • Smoked Fig Syrup22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker without ice. Dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a smoked fig slice on a cocktail pick or an expressed orange peel.

Garnish

Smoked fig slice on a cocktail pick, or expressed orange peel.

Sidecar

CitrusFruitySweetSour
  • Cognac50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. If desired, rim the glass with sugar. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker. Fill with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink, then use the peel as garnish on the rim.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and then placed on the rim of the glass. Alternatively, sugar rim.

Mezcal Margarita

SmokyCitrusFruityEarthySalty
  • Mezcal50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lime Juice25 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml

Preparation

1. Prepare the glass: If desired, salt the rim of a chilled rocks glass by running a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in salt. 2. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine the mezcal, lime juice, orange liqueur, and agave nectar. 3. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 4. Shake well: Shake vigorously until well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. 5. Strain: Strain the mixture into the prepared rocks glass filled with fresh ice. 6. Garnish: Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel, optionally a salted rim. Salt application: rub a lime wedge around the rim, then dip into salt.

Fluffy Critter

CreamyVanillaCitrusSweet
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Orange Liqueur10 ml

Preparation
