Cocktails with Irish Whiskey

Blackthorn

HerbalBitterSpicyWoody
  • Irish Whiskey60 ml
  • Dry Vermouth30 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink, then drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped in.

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 2

Body: 5

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 6

Irish Car Bomb

CoffeeChocolateCreamyBitterEarthyWoody
  • Irish Whiskey30 ml
  • Irish Stout236 ml
  • Irish Cream Liqueur15 ml

Preparation

1. Pour the Irish stout into a pint glass, filling approximately three-quarters full. 2. Carefully layer the Irish cream liqueur on top of the Irish whiskey in a shot glass. 3. Drop the shot glass into the stout and consume immediately.

Garnish

None

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 3

Body: 7

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 6

Irish Coffee

CoffeeVanillaCreamyNuttyWoodySpicy
  • Irish Whiskey50 ml
  • Hot Coffee120 ml
  • Brown Sugar1 tsp
  • Lightly Whipped Cream50 ml

Preparation

1. Warm a heatproof glass (such as a stemmed Irish coffee glass) by filling it with hot water, then discard the water. 2. Add brown sugar to the warmed glass. 3. Pour in hot coffee and stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. 4. Add Irish whiskey and stir gently to combine. 5. Gently pour lightly whipped cream over the back of a spoon, allowing it to float on top of the coffee. Do not stir. 6. Garnish with a grating of fresh nutmeg.

Garnish

Light grating of nutmeg on top of the cream.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 2

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 4
