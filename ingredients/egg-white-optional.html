Cocktails with Egg White (Optional)

Scotch Sour

SmokyCitrusSourWoody
  • Scotch Whisky60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, add Scotch, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a shaker without ice (dry shake). Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 2. Add ice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled (about 15 seconds). 4. Strain into a chilled rocks glass or coupe. 5. Garnish with an orange peel or brandied cherry.

Garnish

Orange peel or brandied cherry

New York Sour

FruityCitrusWoodySpicySavory
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Dry Red Wine15 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White (optional)30 ml

Preparation

1. Combine rye whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white (if using) in a shaker. 2. Dry-shake vigorously for 30 seconds (without ice). 3. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. 4. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. 5. Gently pour the red wine over the back of a spoon to float it on top of the drink.

Garnish

Orange peel or brandied cherry. The orange peel expresses oils to add aroma and slight bitterness, while the cherry adds a touch of sweetness and visual appeal.

Mango Sour

FruityCitrusTropicalSweetSour
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Fresh Mango Puree30 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, dry shake all ingredients without ice for 15 seconds. 2. Add ice to the shaker. 3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled (approximately 15-20 seconds). 4. Double strain into a chilled coupe or sour glass. 5. Garnish with a mango slice or lime wedge.

Garnish

Mango slice or lime wedge

My Fair Lady

FruityCitrusFloralSweetSour
  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Brandy15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml
  • Grenadine7 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, dry shake all ingredients vigorously for 15 seconds to emulsify. 2. Add ice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled (approximately 15-20 seconds). 4. Double strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass. 5. Garnish with a lemon twist or an edible flower.

Garnish

Lemon twist or edible flower (e.g., pansy)

Brandy Sour

FruityCitrusSourWoody
  • Brandy60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)20 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. If using egg white, dry shake brandy and egg white vigorously for 15 seconds to emulsify. 2. Add lemon juice, simple syrup, and bitters to a shaker. 3. Add ice and shake well until chilled. 4. Double strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. 5. Garnish with an orange slice and/or a Maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice and/or Maraschino cherry

Sloe Gin Fizz

FruityCitrusFloral
  • Sloe Gin45 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, add sloe gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a cocktail shaker without ice (dry shake). Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 2. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. 3. Strain into a chilled highball glass filled with ice. 4. Top with soda water.

Garnish

Lemon wedge or a few fresh berries (such as raspberries or blackberries) for visual appeal and an extra burst of flavor.
