Cocktails with Cointreau

Singapore Sling

FruityCitrusHerbalTropicalSpicy
  • Gin30 ml
  • Cherry Liqueur15 ml
  • Cointreau8 ml
  • Benedictine8 ml
  • Pineapple Juice120 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Grenadine10 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Preparation

1. Chill a sling glass or hurricane glass. 2. In a cocktail shaker, combine gin, cherry liqueur, Cointreau, Benedictine, pineapple juice, lime juice, and grenadine. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until the shaker is well-chilled. 5. Strain into the chilled glass filled with ice. 6. Top with a dash of Angostura bitters.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry

Savoy Corpse Reviver No. 2

CitrusHerbalFloralBitterFruity
  • Gin22 ml
  • Lillet Blanc (or Cocchi Americano)22 ml
  • Cointreau22 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Absinthe2 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well until well-chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass.

Garnish

Orange zest, expressed over the drink and discarded.

Japanese Slipper

FruityCitrusTropicalSweet
  • Midori Melon Liqueur30 ml
  • Cointreau30 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add Midori, Cointreau, and lemon juice to a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into the chilled glass.

Garnish

A maraschino cherry, placed on a cocktail pick or directly into the glass.

Corpse Reviver No. 2

CitrusHerbalFloralBitter
  • Gin22 ml
  • Lillet Blanc22 ml
  • Cointreau22 ml
  • Absinthe1 dash
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe glass. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled coupe glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and discard the peel.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist (expressed and discarded)
