Cocktails with Grenadine

Monaco

FruitySweetCitrusYeasty
  • Light Beer30 ml
  • Grenadine30 ml
  • Lemon-Lime Soda60 ml

Preparation

Pour the grenadine into a glass. Add the lemon-lime soda. Gently pour the beer on top to create a layered effect, or stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Garnish

Lemon wedge or slice

Tequila Sunrise

FruityCitrusSweetTropical
  • Tequila60 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml
  • Orange Juice120 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice. 2. Add Tequila. 3. Top with orange juice. 4. Gently pour grenadine down the side of the glass so it settles at the bottom, creating the sunrise effect. Do not stir. 5. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice and a maraschino cherry. The orange slice adds a citrus aroma and visual appeal, while the cherry complements the sweetness of the grenadine.

Singapore Sling

FruityCitrusHerbalTropicalSpicy
  • Gin30 ml
  • Cherry Liqueur15 ml
  • Cointreau8 ml
  • Benedictine8 ml
  • Pineapple Juice120 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Grenadine10 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Preparation

1. Chill a sling glass or hurricane glass. 2. In a cocktail shaker, combine gin, cherry liqueur, Cointreau, Benedictine, pineapple juice, lime juice, and grenadine. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until the shaker is well-chilled. 5. Strain into the chilled glass filled with ice. 6. Top with a dash of Angostura bitters.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry

Slippery Nipple

SweetCreamyVanillaFruity
  • Sambuca20 ml
  • Irish Cream20 ml
  • Grenadine5 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass. First, pour the grenadine into the bottom of the shot glass. Next, gently pour the sambuca on top of the grenadine, being careful not to mix the layers. Finally, slowly layer the Irish cream on top of the sambuca, creating a distinct creamy layer.

Garnish

None

Cuban Sunset

FruityTropicalCitrusSweet
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a shaker with ice. 2. Add white rum, pineapple juice, and lime juice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled. 4. Strain into a chilled glass (such as a hurricane glass or highball glass) filled with ice. 5. Gently pour grenadine down the side of the glass so that it settles at the bottom, creating a layered effect.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry on a cocktail pick.

My Fair Lady

FruityCitrusFloralSweetSour
  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Brandy15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml
  • Grenadine7 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, dry shake all ingredients vigorously for 15 seconds to emulsify. 2. Add ice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled (approximately 15-20 seconds). 4. Double strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass. 5. Garnish with a lemon twist or an edible flower.

Garnish

Lemon twist or edible flower (e.g., pansy)

El Presidente

FruityCitrusHerbalSweet
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Orange Curaçao7 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Grenadine1 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and place the peel in the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass
