Cocktails with Aperol

Paper Plane

CitrusBitterHerbalFruity
  • Bourbon22 ml
  • Aperol22 ml
  • Amaro Nonino Quintessentia22 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a coupe or Nick & Nora glass. Garnish with an orange peel.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and discarded or placed on the rim of the glass.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 6

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5

Aperol Spritz

BitterCitrusFruityHerbal
  • Aperol60 ml
  • Prosecco90 ml
  • Soda Water30 ml

Preparation

Fill a large wine glass with ice. Add Prosecco, followed by Aperol. Top with soda water. Gently stir to combine.

Garnish

Orange slice, placed on the rim of the glass.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 4

Body: 3

Complexity: 5

Booziness: 2

Spaghett

FruityCitrusBitterFloralTropical
  • Aperol28 ml
  • Prosecco28 ml

Preparation

Combine Aperol and Prosecco in a wine glass filled with ice. Gently stir to combine. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and drop the twist into the glass.

Garnish

Large lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed in the glass.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 4

Body: 3

Complexity: 5

Booziness: 3
