Cocktails with Honey Syrup (3:1 Honey to Water)

Bee's Knees

CitrusFloralHerbalSweetSour
  • Gin60 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Honey Syrup (3:1 Honey to Water)22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped in or discarded. Alternatively, a small edible flower can be used.
