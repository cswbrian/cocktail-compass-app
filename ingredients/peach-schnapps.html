Cocktails with Peach Schnapps

Sex on the Beach

FruityTropicalSweet
  • Vodka40 ml
  • Peach Schnapps20 ml
  • Cranberry Juice40 ml
  • Orange Juice40 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes. 2. Add vodka, peach schnapps, cranberry juice, and orange juice. 3. Stir gently to combine. 4. Garnish with an orange slice or a cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice or cherry

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 5

Body: 4

Complexity: 3

Booziness: 3

Redheaded Slut

FruityHerbalSweet
  • Jägermeister15 ml
  • Peach Schnapps15 ml
  • Cranberry Juice15 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass in the following order: Peach Schnapps, Jägermeister, then Cranberry Juice. The layering effect is achieved by gently pouring each liquid over the back of a spoon to minimize mixing.

Garnish

None typically, but occasionally a cherry can be added.

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 3

Body: 3

Complexity: 5

Booziness: 4

Fuzzy Navel

FruityTropicalSweet
  • Peach Schnapps45 ml
  • Orange Juice90 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add peach schnapps. Top with orange juice. Stir gently. Garnish as desired.

Garnish

Orange slice or cherry (optional)

Sweetness: 8

Sourness: 3

Body: 4

Complexity: 2

Booziness: 2
