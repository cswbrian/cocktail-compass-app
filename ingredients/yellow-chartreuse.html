Cocktails with Yellow Chartreuse

Diamondback

HerbalFruitySpicyWoody
  • Rye Whiskey45 ml
  • Apple Brandy (Calvados)15 ml
  • Yellow Chartreuse30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled, approximately 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or Nick & Nora glass. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and discard the peel (optional).

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed and discarded (optional)

Widow's Kiss

FruityHerbalSpicyFloralBitter
  • Apple Brandy30 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Yellow Chartreuse15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Maraschino cherry

Greenpoint

HerbalBitterSpicyWoody
  • Rye Whiskey50 ml
  • Yellow Chartreuse15 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Orange Bitters1 dash

Preparation

Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass until well-chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or Nick & Nora glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and discard the peel.

Garnish

Lemon peel, expressed and discarded
