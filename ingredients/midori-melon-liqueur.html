Cocktails with Midori Melon Liqueur

Japanese Slipper

FruityCitrusTropicalSweet
  • Midori Melon Liqueur30 ml
  • Cointreau30 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add Midori, Cointreau, and lemon juice to a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into the chilled glass.

Garnish

A maraschino cherry, placed on a cocktail pick or directly into the glass.
