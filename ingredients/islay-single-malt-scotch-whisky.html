Cocktails with Islay Single Malt Scotch Whisky

Penicillin

SmokySpicyCitrusHerbalSweetEarthy
  • Blended Scotch Whisky60 ml
  • Islay Single Malt Scotch Whisky7 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Honey-Ginger Syrup22 ml

Preparation

Muddle ginger slices (if using fresh ginger), add blended Scotch, lemon juice, and honey-ginger syrup to a shaker. Add ice and shake well until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. Float the Islay Scotch on top. Garnish with candied ginger and/or a lemon peel.

Garnish

Candied ginger and/or a lemon peel
