Cocktails with Elderflower Liqueur

Royal Arrival

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal
  • Gin30 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur15 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Simple Syrup10 ml
  • Sparkling Wine60 ml

Preparation

Combine gin, elderflower liqueur, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or champagne flute. Top with sparkling wine. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Jazmin Sour

CitrusFloralHerbalFruitySourSweet
  • Gin60 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Egg White20 ml
  • Simple Syrup2 dashes

Preparation

1. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker without ice. 2. Dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until chilled. 5. Double strain into a chilled coupe or sour glass. 6. Garnish with an edible flower petal or lemon twist.

Garnish

Edible flower petal or Lemon twist

Platinum Blonde

FruityFloralCitrus
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur15 ml
  • White Cranberry Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Garnish

Lime wheel

Green Beast

HerbalCitrusFruityFloral
  • Vodka30 ml
  • Absinthe20 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur20 ml
  • Cucumber4 slices
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Soda Water60 ml

Preparation

Muddle cucumber slices in a shaker. Add vodka, lime juice, absinthe, and elderflower liqueur. Fill the shaker with ice and shake well until chilled. Double strain into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with soda water. Garnish with a cucumber ribbon or wheel and a lime wedge.

Garnish

Cucumber ribbon or wheel, lime wedge

Spicy Fifty

SpicyFloralCitrusSweetFruity
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur20 ml
  • Simple Syrup infused with Red Chili2 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with a thin slice of red chili pepper on the rim.

Garnish

Thin slice of red chili pepper, placed delicately on the rim of the glass.

Irish Maid

FruityCitrusHerbalFloralGrassy
  • Irish Whiskey60 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur22 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Cucumber Slices3 slices

Preparation

Muddle cucumber slices gently in a shaker. Add Irish whiskey, lemon juice, and elderflower liqueur to the shaker. Fill with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a rocks glass filled with ice.

Garnish

Cucumber wheel
