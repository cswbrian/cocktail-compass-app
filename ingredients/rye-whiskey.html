Cocktails with Rye Whiskey

Sazerac

HerbalSpicyBitterCitrusWoody
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Absinthe7 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Peychaud's Bitters3 dashes

Preparation

Chill an Old Fashioned glass. In a separate mixing glass, muddle the sugar cube with the Peychaud's Bitters. Add the rye whiskey and ice. Stir well until chilled. Rinse the chilled Old Fashioned glass with the absinthe, coating the inside of the glass, then discard the excess. Strain the whiskey mixture into the absinthe-rinsed glass. Express the oil from the lemon peel over the glass and discard.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the glass and discarded. The oils add aroma and a hint of citrus without adding juice.

Horsefeather

SpicyCitrusHerbalWoody
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Ginger Beer120 ml
  • Dash Angostura Bitters2 dash

Preparation

Combine rye whiskey, lime juice, and Angostura bitters in a highball glass filled with ice. Top with ginger beer. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge. Optionally add a piece of candied ginger.

Garnish

Lime wedge, optionally candied ginger

Wolfram

SpicyFruityHerbalBitterCitrus
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Cherry Heering15 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or Nick & Nora glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and drop it in.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Trinidad Sour

BitterSpicyCitrusNuttyHerbal
  • Angostura Bitters45 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup30 ml
  • Rye Whiskey15 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously until well-chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a mint sprig and a few extra dashes of Angostura bitters on top.

Garnish

Mint sprig and 2-3 dashes of Angostura bitters on top of the foam.

Revolving Door

SpicyBitterHerbalWoody
  • Rye Whiskey45 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Sugar Syrup1 bar spoon

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or cocktail glass. 2. In a mixing glass, combine the rye whiskey, Benedictine, sugar syrup, and Angostura bitters. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass and stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). 4. Strain the mixture into the chilled glass. 5. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and discard the peel.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and discarded.

New York Sour

FruityCitrusWoodySpicySavory
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Dry Red Wine15 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White (optional)30 ml

Preparation

1. Combine rye whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white (if using) in a shaker. 2. Dry-shake vigorously for 30 seconds (without ice). 3. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. 4. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. 5. Gently pour the red wine over the back of a spoon to float it on top of the drink.

Garnish

Orange peel or brandied cherry. The orange peel expresses oils to add aroma and slight bitterness, while the cherry adds a touch of sweetness and visual appeal.

Manhattan

BitterHerbalSpicyWoodyFruity
  • Rye Whiskey50 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth20 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. In a mixing glass, combine rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, and Angostura bitters. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass. 4. Stir well until the mixing glass is well-chilled. 5. Strain into the chilled glass. 6. Garnish with a maraschino cherry or expressed orange peel.

Garnish

Maraschino cherry, preferably Luxardo, or an orange peel twist, expressed over the drink.
