Cocktails with Campari

Negroni Sbagliato

BitterFruityCitrusHerbalFloral
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Prosecco90 ml

Preparation

Pour Campari and Sweet Vermouth into a rocks glass filled with ice. Top with Prosecco. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with an orange slice or zest.

Garnish

Orange slice or zest

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 4

Americano

BitterHerbalCitrusFruity
  • Campari1 oz
  • Sweet Vermouth1 oz
  • Soda Water3 oz

Preparation

Fill a rocks glass with ice. Add Campari and sweet vermouth. Top with soda water. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with an orange peel twist.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 3

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 2

Boulevardier

BitterHerbalFruityWoody
  • Bourbon30 ml
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass over a large ice cube.

Garnish

Orange peel twist (expressed and inserted)

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 3

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 7

Negroni

BitterHerbalCitrus
  • Gin30 ml
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled, approximately 20-30 seconds. Strain into an old-fashioned glass filled with ice. Garnish with an orange peel.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 4

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 7

Campari Spritz

BitterFruityCitrusHerbal
  • Campari90 ml
  • Sparkling Wine60 ml
  • Soda Water30 ml

Preparation

Fill a wine glass with ice. Add the Campari. Top with sparkling wine, then add soda water. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with an orange slice.

Garnish

Orange slice, placed on the rim of the glass.

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 4

Body: 3

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 3

Old Pal

BitterHerbalSpicyWoody
  • Rye Whiskey45 ml
  • Campari30 ml
  • Dry Vermouth30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a lemon or orange peel, expressed over the drink and discarded.

Garnish

Lemon or orange peel, expressed and discarded or affixed to the rim; use lemon for dryness or orange for slight sweeteness

Sweetness: 2

Sourness: 3

Body: 6

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 7

Mezcal Negroni

BitterHerbalSmokyCitrusEarthy
  • Mezcal30 ml
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into an old-fashioned glass filled with ice. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 4

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 7
