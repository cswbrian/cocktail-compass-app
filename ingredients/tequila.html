Cocktails with Tequila

Tequila Sunrise

FruityCitrusSweetTropical
  • Tequila60 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml
  • Orange Juice120 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice. 2. Add Tequila. 3. Top with orange juice. 4. Gently pour grenadine down the side of the glass so it settles at the bottom, creating the sunrise effect. Do not stir. 5. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice and a maraschino cherry. The orange slice adds a citrus aroma and visual appeal, while the cherry complements the sweetness of the grenadine.

Long Island Iced Tea

CitrusFruitySweetBoozy
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Gin15 ml
  • White Rum15 ml
  • Tequila15 ml
  • Triple Sec15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Coca-Cola30 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice. 2. Add vodka, gin, white rum, tequila, and triple sec to the glass. 3. Add lemon juice. 4. Top with Coca-Cola. 5. Stir gently to combine. 6. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Garnish

Lemon wedge, for garnish. Prepare by slicing a fresh lemon into wedges.

Margarita

CitrusFruitySourSalty
  • Tequila50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur25 ml
  • Lime Juice25 ml

Preparation

If desired, salt the rim of a chilled cocktail glass by running a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in salt. Combine tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into the prepared glass filled with ice. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Garnish

Lime wedge; optional: salt rim (coarse salt preferred)

Oaxaca Old Fashioned

SmokyHerbalSpicyEarthySweet
  • Tequila45 ml
  • Mezcal15 ml
  • Agave Nectar5 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine tequila, mezcal, agave nectar, and Angostura bitters in a mixing glass. Add ice and stir until well-chilled and properly diluted (about 20-30 seconds). Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Express the oils from an orange peel over the glass, then drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass.

Ranch Water

CitrusSaltyEarthyHerbal
  • Tequila60 ml
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Sparkling Mineral Water180 ml

Preparation

1. Optional: Salt the rim of a highball glass. 2. Fill the glass with ice. 3. Add tequila. 4. Squeeze in the lime juice. 5. Top with sparkling mineral water. 6. Gently stir to combine. 7. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel, for squeezing and rimming (optional: salt rim)

Tommy's Margarita

CitrusFruityTropical
  • Tequila60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Agave Nectar20 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or rocks glass. 2. Add Tequila, lime juice, and agave nectar to a shaker with ice. 3. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 4. Strain into the chilled glass. 5. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel

Hangman's Blood

BitterFruitySpicySmokyYeasty
  • Gin25 ml
  • Rum25 ml
  • Vodka25 ml
  • Whiskey25 ml
  • Tequila25 ml
  • Stout100 ml
  • Champagne100 ml

Preparation

Combine all base spirits in a shaker with ice. Stir gently. Strain into a highball or large glass filled with ice. Top with stout, then gently add the champagne. Stir carefully to combine without losing too much fizz.

Garnish

Orange slice

Three-Mile Long Island Iced Tea

CitrusFruityHerbalWoodySmoky
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Gin15 ml
  • White Rum15 ml
  • Tequila15 ml
  • Whiskey15 ml
  • Triple Sec15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Cola75 ml

Preparation

1. Combine all base spirits (vodka, gin, rum, tequila, and whiskey), triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 3. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice. 4. Top with cola. 5. Garnish with a lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry. Prepare by slicing a fresh lemon into wedges. Optionally, add a maraschino cherry to the rim of the glass for visual appeal.
