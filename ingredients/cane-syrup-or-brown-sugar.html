Cocktails with Cane Syrup or Brown Sugar

Ti' Punch

HerbalGrassyCitrusEarthyTropical
  • Rhum Agricole50 ml
  • Lime1 wedge
  • Cane Syrup or Brown Sugar1 barspoon

Preparation

In an old-fashioned glass, gently muddle the lime wedge and cane syrup or brown sugar. Add the rhum agricole. Add one small ice cube (optional, but traditional) and stir briefly to combine, allowing the ice to chill and slightly dilute the drink. The drink is traditionally served without a strainer.

Garnish

Lime wheel or twist. Express the oils over the drink before placing it in the glass.
