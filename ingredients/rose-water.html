Cocktails with Rose Water

Rose Kennedy

FruityCitrusFloralSweet
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Grenadine7 ml
  • Lime Juice22 ml
  • Rose Water2 drops

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Garnish

Lime wheel
