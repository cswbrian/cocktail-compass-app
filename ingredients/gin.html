Royal Arrival

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal
  • Gin30 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur15 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Simple Syrup10 ml
  • Sparkling Wine60 ml

Preparation

Combine gin, elderflower liqueur, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or champagne flute. Top with sparkling wine. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Gin Sour

CitrusHerbalSourSweet
  • Gin60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker without ice (dry shake) for 15 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 2. Add ice to the shaker and shake vigorously for another 15-20 seconds to chill and further emulsify the mixture. 3. Double strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. If serving in a rocks glass, fill with fresh ice. 4. Garnish with a lemon peel twist or a few drops of bitters on the egg white foam (if used).

Garnish

Lemon peel twist or a few drops of bitters on the egg white foam (if used). Express the oils from the lemon peel over the drink before placing it on the rim.

Agua de Valencia

FruityCitrusSweet
  • Gin50 ml
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Cava100 ml
  • Fresh Orange Juice150 ml

Preparation

Combine gin, vodka, orange juice, and simple syrup (if using) in a pitcher. Stir well to combine. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld. Just before serving, top with chilled cava. Stir gently to combine. Pour into champagne flutes or wine glasses. Garnish with an orange slice or zest.

Garnish

Orange slice or zest, or optionally a few grapes.

Ramos Gin Fizz

CitrusFloralCreamyFruityHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Orange Flower Water3 drops
  • Heavy Cream30 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice15 ml
  • Egg White1 pc
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp

Preparation

1. Chill a Collins glass. 2. In a shaker, combine gin, heavy cream, lemon juice, lime juice, egg white, orange flower water, and sugar. 3. Dry shake (without ice) vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 4. Add ice to the shaker and shake again vigorously for at least 1 minute, or until the shaker is very cold. 5. Strain the mixture into the chilled Collins glass. 6. Slowly top with soda water to create a frothy head, pouring in a thin stream. 7. Allow the foam to rise above the rim of the glass. 8. Optional spritz of orange flower water on top. 9. Garnish.

Garnish

Orange blossom water spritz and floated edible flowers such as pansies or violets

Suffering Bastard

HerbalSpicyCitrusFruity
  • Gin15 ml
  • Brandy15 ml
  • Orange Curaçao15 ml
  • Lime Juice20 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Ginger Ale90 ml

Preparation

Add gin, brandy, orange curaçao, and lime juice to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. Top with ginger ale. Dash Angostura bitters on top. Garnish with a mint sprig and cucumber slice.

Garnish

Mint sprig, cucumber slice, and a dash of Angostura bitters on top

Three-Mile Long Island Iced Tea

CitrusFruityHerbalWoodySmoky
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Gin15 ml
  • White Rum15 ml
  • Tequila15 ml
  • Whiskey15 ml
  • Triple Sec15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Cola75 ml

Preparation

1. Combine all base spirits (vodka, gin, rum, tequila, and whiskey), triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 3. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice. 4. Top with cola. 5. Garnish with a lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry. Prepare by slicing a fresh lemon into wedges. Optionally, add a maraschino cherry to the rim of the glass for visual appeal.
