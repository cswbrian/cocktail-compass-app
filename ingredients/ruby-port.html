Cocktails with Ruby Port

Porto Flip

FruityCreamyNuttyVanillaChocolateWine
  • Ruby Port60 ml
  • Brandy15 ml
  • Egg Yolk1 large
  • Simple Syrup20 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. Combine the Ruby Port, brandy, egg yolk, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. 3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled and emulsified. 4. Double strain into the chilled glass. 5. Grate fresh nutmeg over the top as garnish.

Garnish

Freshly grated nutmeg
