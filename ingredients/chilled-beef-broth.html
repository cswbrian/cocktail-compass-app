Cocktails with Chilled Beef Broth

Bull Shot

SaltyUmamiSavorySpicy
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Chilled Beef Broth120 ml
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 dashes
  • Hot Sauce2 dashes
  • Black Pepper1 pinch
  • Celery Salt1 pinch

Preparation

In a highball glass filled with ice, add vodka, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, black pepper, and celery salt. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lemon wedge or celery stalk.

Garnish

Lemon wedge or celery stalk
