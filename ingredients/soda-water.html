Cocktails with Soda Water

Americano

BitterHerbalCitrusFruity
  • Campari1 oz
  • Sweet Vermouth1 oz
  • Soda Water3 oz

Preparation

Fill a rocks glass with ice. Add Campari and sweet vermouth. Top with soda water. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with an orange peel twist.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Ramos Gin Fizz

CitrusFloralCreamyFruityHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Orange Flower Water3 drops
  • Heavy Cream30 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice15 ml
  • Egg White1 pc
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp

Preparation

1. Chill a Collins glass. 2. In a shaker, combine gin, heavy cream, lemon juice, lime juice, egg white, orange flower water, and sugar. 3. Dry shake (without ice) vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 4. Add ice to the shaker and shake again vigorously for at least 1 minute, or until the shaker is very cold. 5. Strain the mixture into the chilled Collins glass. 6. Slowly top with soda water to create a frothy head, pouring in a thin stream. 7. Allow the foam to rise above the rim of the glass. 8. Optional spritz of orange flower water on top. 9. Garnish.

Garnish

Orange blossom water spritz and floated edible flowers such as pansies or violets

Mojito

CitrusHerbalTropicalSweet
  • White Rum60 ml
  • Mint Leaves12 Leaves
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp
  • Soda Water60 ml

Preparation

1. Gently muddle the mint leaves with sugar in a sturdy glass. Avoid over-muddling, which can make the mint bitter. 2. Add lime juice and rum to the glass. 3. Fill the glass with ice. 4. Top with soda water. 5. Gently stir to combine. 6. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge.

Garnish

Mint sprig and lime wedge

Gin Fizz

CitrusHerbalGrassyFloral
  • Gin45 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Soda Water90 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a highball glass. 2. In a shaker, combine gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until chilled. 5. Strain into the chilled highball glass filled with ice. 6. Top with soda water. 7. Gently stir to combine. 8. Garnish with a lemon wheel or wedge.

Garnish

Lemon wheel or wedge

Fluffy Duck

CreamyVanillaChocolateFruitySweet
  • Gin20 ml
  • Advocaat20 ml
  • White Crème de Cacao10 ml
  • Fresh Cream20 ml
  • Soda Water50 ml

Preparation

Combine gin, Advocaat, crème de cacao, and fresh cream in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass. Top with soda water. Optionally garnish with grated nutmeg or chocolate shavings.

Garnish

Grated nutmeg or chocolate shavings, optional

Virgin Mojito

CitrusHerbalTropicalFruityGrassy
  • Fresh Mint Leaves12 leaves
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Soda Water120 ml
  • Lime Wedges4 wedges

Preparation

1. Gently muddle the mint leaves, lime wedges, and simple syrup in a sturdy glass. Avoid over-muddling, as this can release bitter compounds from the mint. 2. Add the lime juice. 3. Fill the glass with ice. 4. Top with soda water. 5. Stir gently to combine. 6. Garnish with a mint sprig and a lime wedge.

Garnish

Mint sprig and lime wedge. Gently slap the mint sprig to release its aromas before placing it on the drink.

Sloe Gin Fizz

FruityCitrusFloral
  • Sloe Gin45 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, add sloe gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a cocktail shaker without ice (dry shake). Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 2. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. 3. Strain into a chilled highball glass filled with ice. 4. Top with soda water.

Garnish

Lemon wedge or a few fresh berries (such as raspberries or blackberries) for visual appeal and an extra burst of flavor.
