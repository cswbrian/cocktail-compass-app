Cocktails with Orange Bitters

Blackthorn

HerbalBitterSpicyWoody
  • Irish Whiskey60 ml
  • Dry Vermouth30 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink, then drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped in.

The Blenheim

CitrusHerbalBitter
  • Gin60 ml
  • Orange Curaçao7 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until chilled, approximately 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

Garnish

Orange twist, expressed over the drink and then placed on the rim.

Queen's Cocktail

FruityHerbalCitrusBittersweet
  • Gin30 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth15 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine gin, dry vermouth, sweet vermouth, and orange bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange twist over the drink and drop it in.

Garnish

Orange twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped in.

Bamboo Cocktail

HerbalNuttyDryFloralWoody
  • Dry Sherry45 ml
  • Dry Vermouth45 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. Add sherry, vermouth, orange bitters, and Angostura bitters to a mixing glass filled with ice. 3. Stir well until chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). 4. Strain into the chilled glass. 5. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and drop it in.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped in.
