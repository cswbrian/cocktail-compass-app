Singapore Sling

  • Cointreau8 ml
  • Benedictine8 ml
  • Pineapple Juice120 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Grenadine10 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Preparation

1. Chill a sling glass or hurricane glass. 2. In a cocktail shaker, combine gin, cherry liqueur, Cointreau, Benedictine, pineapple juice, lime juice, and grenadine. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry

Flaming Volcano

TropicalFruitySweetSpicy
  • Light Rum60 ml
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Brandy30 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Orange Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc

Preparation

1. Combine light rum, dark rum, brandy, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and orgeat syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until chilled. 3. Strain into a volcano bowl or large serving dish. 4. Soak a sugar cube in high-proof rum (at least 75% ABV). 5. Place the sugar cube on a heat-resistant surface in the center of the bowl. 6. Carefully ignite the sugar cube using a long lighter or match. 7. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and cherry.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge, cherry, and a flaming sugar cube soaked in high-proof rum. Ensure sugar cube is placed on a heat-resistant surface within the bowl before ignition.

Cuban Sunset

FruityTropicalCitrusSweet
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a shaker with ice. 2. Add white rum, pineapple juice, and lime juice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled. 4. Strain into a chilled glass (such as a hurricane glass or highball glass) filled with ice. 5. Gently pour grenadine down the side of the glass so that it settles at the bottom, creating a layered effect.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry on a cocktail pick.
