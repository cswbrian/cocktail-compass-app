Cocktails with Peach Brandy

Fish House Punch

FruityCitrusTropicalWoodySpicy
  • Dark Jamaican Rum250 ml
  • Cognac250 ml
  • Peach Brandy250 ml
  • Lemon Juice375 ml
  • Simple Syrup (1:1)250 ml
  • Water750 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a large punch bowl. Add a large block of ice. Stir well until chilled and slightly diluted. Let sit for at least 1 hour for flavors to meld. Add lemon slices and/or grated nutmeg before serving. Ladle into punch glasses or cups.

Garnish

Lemon slices and/or grated nutmeg floated on top.
