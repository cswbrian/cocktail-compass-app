Cocktails with Vanilla Ice Cream

Mudslide

ChocolateCoffeeVanillaCreamyNutty
  • Vodka30 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur30 ml
  • Irish Cream Liqueur30 ml
  • Vanilla Ice Cream60 ml

Preparation

Combine vodka, coffee liqueur, Irish cream liqueur, and vanilla ice cream in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a chilled glass. Garnish with chocolate shavings or chocolate syrup drizzle and optional whipped cream.

Garnish

Chocolate shavings or a drizzle of chocolate syrup inside the glass, optional whipped cream.
