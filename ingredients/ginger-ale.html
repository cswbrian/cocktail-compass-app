Cocktails with Ginger Ale

Horse's Neck

CitrusSpicyWoodyVanilla
  • Bourbon Whiskey44 ml
  • Ginger Ale120 ml
  • Angostura bitters3 dashes

Preparation

1. Prepare the lemon spiral by cutting a long, continuous strip of peel from the lemon, starting at the top and working your way around. 2. Drape the lemon spiral over the rim of a highball glass, with the majority of the peel hanging outside the glass. 3. Fill the glass with ice cubes. 4. Add Bourbon whiskey to the glass. 5. Add Angostura bitters. 6. Top with ginger ale. 7. Gently stir to combine. 8. Serve immediately.

Garnish

Long spiral of lemon peel, draped over the rim of the glass.
