Cocktails with Prosecco

Negroni Sbagliato

BitterFruityCitrusHerbalFloral
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Prosecco90 ml

Preparation

Pour Campari and Sweet Vermouth into a rocks glass filled with ice. Top with Prosecco. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with an orange slice or zest.

Garnish

Orange slice or zest

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 4

Porn Star Martini

FruityCitrusTropicalVanillaSweet
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Vanilla Liqueur20 ml
  • Passion Fruit Puree30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Prosecco50 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a martini glass. 2. In a shaker, combine vodka, passion fruit puree, vanilla liqueur, and lime juice. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until the shaker is well-chilled. 5. Double strain into the chilled martini glass. 6. Garnish with half a fresh passion fruit, floated on top. 7. Serve with a shot of chilled Prosecco on the side.

Garnish

Half a fresh passion fruit, floated on top of the drink. Serve with a shot of chilled Prosecco on the side.

Sweetness: 8

Sourness: 6

Body: 5

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 4

Bellini

FruityTropicalSweetFloral
  • Prosecco100 ml
  • White Peach Puree50 ml

Preparation

Pour the white peach puree into a chilled flute glass. Gently top with Prosecco, ensuring it doesn't overflow. Stir lightly to combine. Add garnish if desired.

Garnish

Optional: Peach slice or raspberry.

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 2

Body: 3

Complexity: 4

Booziness: 2

Smoked Peach Bellini

FruitySmokySweet
  • Prosecco30 ml
  • Peach Liqueur5 ml
  • Smoked Peach Puree50 ml

Preparation

1. Smoke fresh peaches using a smoker or by placing peach halves cut-side up in a foil pan and smoking with wood chips for approximately 1-2 hours, depending on the intensity of smoke desired. Cool the peaches. 2. Blend the smoked peaches into a smooth puree. Strain if desired. 3. In a chilled champagne flute, gently pour in the smoked peach puree. 4. Add the peach liqueur. 5. Top with chilled Prosecco, pouring slowly to maintain the bubbles. 6. Gently stir to combine.

Garnish

Thin peach slice, gently fanned on the rim of the glass. Optionally, add a small sprig of fresh thyme.

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 2

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 3

Barracuda

FruityTropicalSweetCitrusVanillaHerbal
  • Gold Rum45 ml
  • Galliano15 ml
  • Prosecco60 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice7 ml

Preparation

Combine rum, pineapple juice, lime juice, and Galliano in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled champagne flute or coupe glass. Top with Prosecco. Garnish with a lime or pineapple wedge.

Garnish

Lime wedge or Pineapple wedge

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 4

Body: 5

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 5

Spaghett

FruityCitrusBitterFloralTropical
  • Aperol28 ml
  • Prosecco28 ml

Preparation

Combine Aperol and Prosecco in a wine glass filled with ice. Gently stir to combine. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and drop the twist into the glass.

Garnish

Large lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed in the glass.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 4

Body: 3

Complexity: 5

Booziness: 3
