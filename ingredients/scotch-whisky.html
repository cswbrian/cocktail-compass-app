Cocktails with Scotch Whisky

Scotch Sour

SmokyCitrusSourWoody
  • Scotch Whisky60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, add Scotch, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a shaker without ice (dry shake). Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 2. Add ice to the shaker. 3. Shake well until chilled (about 15 seconds). 4. Strain into a chilled rocks glass or coupe. 5. Garnish with an orange peel or brandied cherry.

Garnish

Orange peel or brandied cherry

Godfather

NuttyWoodySweetSmoky
  • Scotch Whisky50 ml
  • Amaretto25 ml

Preparation

Pour Scotch whisky and amaretto into an Old Fashioned glass filled with ice. Stir gently to combine. Garnish as desired.

Garnish

Optional: Lemon or orange twist. Express oils over the drink if used.

Rob Roy

WoodyHerbalSpicyVanilla
  • Scotch Whisky60 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine Scotch, sweet vermouth, and bitters in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass. Garnish with a maraschino cherry or a lemon twist.

Garnish

Maraschino cherry, or optionally a lemon twist

Blood & Sand

FruityCitrusSmokyHerbal
  • Scotch Whisky22 ml
  • Cherry Heering22 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth22 ml
  • Fresh Orange Juice22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim.

Rusty Nail

SmokyHerbalSweetWoody
  • Scotch Whisky50 ml
  • Drambuie25 ml

Preparation

Combine Scotch whisky and Drambuie in an old-fashioned glass filled with ice. Stir gently until well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. Express the oils of a lemon peel over the drink and drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink to release oils, then dropped into the glass.

Churchill

SmokyWoodyHerbal
  • Scotch Whisky60 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine Scotch whisky and dry vermouth in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and discard the peel.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and discarded.

Scotch and Soda

SmokyGrassyWoodyEarthyVanilla
  • Scotch Whisky50 ml
  • Soda Water120 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes. 2. Add Scotch whisky to the glass. 3. Top with soda water. 4. Gently stir to combine. 5. Garnish as desired.

Garnish

Optional: Lemon twist or orange peel, expressed over the drink.
