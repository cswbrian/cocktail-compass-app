Cocktails with Grapefruit Juice

Sea Breeze

FruityCitrusTart
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Cranberry Juice120 ml
  • Grapefruit Juice45 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add vodka, cranberry juice, and grapefruit juice. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel. Express the oils over the drink and then place on the rim of the glass.

Greyhound

FruityCitrusTart
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Grapefruit Juice120 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add vodka. Top with grapefruit juice. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge, if desired.

Garnish

Grapefruit wedge or slice (optional)

Salty Dog

SaltyCitrusBitter
  • Gin60 ml
  • Grapefruit Juice120 ml

Preparation

1. Salt the rim of a highball glass by running a grapefruit wedge around the rim and then dipping it into salt. 2. Fill the glass with ice. 3. Add gin and grapefruit juice. 4. Stir gently to combine. 5. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge (optional).

Garnish

Salted rim (kosher or sea salt preferred), Grapefruit wedge optional.
