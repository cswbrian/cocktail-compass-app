Cocktails with Drambuie

Rusty Nail

SmokyHerbalSweetWoody
  • Scotch Whisky50 ml
  • Drambuie25 ml

Preparation

Combine Scotch whisky and Drambuie in an old-fashioned glass filled with ice. Stir gently until well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. Express the oils of a lemon peel over the drink and drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink to release oils, then dropped into the glass.
