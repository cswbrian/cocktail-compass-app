Cocktails with Smoked Peach Puree

Smoked Peach Bellini

FruitySmokySweet
  • Prosecco30 ml
  • Peach Liqueur5 ml
  • Smoked Peach Puree50 ml

Preparation

1. Smoke fresh peaches using a smoker or by placing peach halves cut-side up in a foil pan and smoking with wood chips for approximately 1-2 hours, depending on the intensity of smoke desired. Cool the peaches. 2. Blend the smoked peaches into a smooth puree. Strain if desired. 3. In a chilled champagne flute, gently pour in the smoked peach puree. 4. Add the peach liqueur. 5. Top with chilled Prosecco, pouring slowly to maintain the bubbles. 6. Gently stir to combine.

Garnish

Thin peach slice, gently fanned on the rim of the glass. Optionally, add a small sprig of fresh thyme.
