Cocktails with Triple Sec

Long Island Iced Tea

CitrusFruitySweetBoozy
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Gin15 ml
  • White Rum15 ml
  • Tequila15 ml
  • Triple Sec15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Coca-Cola30 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice. 2. Add vodka, gin, white rum, tequila, and triple sec to the glass. 3. Add lemon juice. 4. Top with Coca-Cola. 5. Stir gently to combine. 6. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Garnish

Lemon wedge, for garnish. Prepare by slicing a fresh lemon into wedges.

Lynchburg Lemonade

CitrusFruitySweet
  • Whiskey40 ml
  • Triple Sec20 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Lemon-Lime Soda120 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice. 2. Add whiskey, triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup. 3. Top with lemon-lime soda. 4. Stir gently to combine. 5. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Garnish

Lemon wedge

White Lady

CitrusFloralFruitySourHerbal
  • Gin40 ml
  • Triple Sec20 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice20 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim.

Three-Mile Long Island Iced Tea

CitrusFruityHerbalWoodySmoky
  • Vodka15 ml
  • Gin15 ml
  • White Rum15 ml
  • Tequila15 ml
  • Whiskey15 ml
  • Triple Sec15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Cola75 ml

Preparation

1. Combine all base spirits (vodka, gin, rum, tequila, and whiskey), triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 3. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice. 4. Top with cola. 5. Garnish with a lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon wedge and/or a maraschino cherry. Prepare by slicing a fresh lemon into wedges. Optionally, add a maraschino cherry to the rim of the glass for visual appeal.
