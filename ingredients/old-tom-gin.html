Cocktails with Old Tom Gin

Martinez

FruityHerbalBitterFloral
  • Old Tom Gin45 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur7 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth20 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass until well-chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass or a cocktail cherry.

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 2

Body: 6

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 7
