Cocktails with Absinthe

Chrysanthemum

FloralHerbalBitter
  • Dry Vermouth45 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add dry vermouth, Benedictine, and absinthe to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and then discard the peel.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and then discarded

Sazerac

HerbalSpicyBitterCitrusWoody
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Absinthe7 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Peychaud's Bitters3 dashes

Preparation

Chill an Old Fashioned glass. In a separate mixing glass, muddle the sugar cube with the Peychaud's Bitters. Add the rye whiskey and ice. Stir well until chilled. Rinse the chilled Old Fashioned glass with the absinthe, coating the inside of the glass, then discard the excess. Strain the whiskey mixture into the absinthe-rinsed glass. Express the oil from the lemon peel over the glass and discard.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the glass and discarded. The oils add aroma and a hint of citrus without adding juice.

Death in the Afternoon

HerbalAniseFruityFloral
  • Absinthe45 ml
  • Champagne120 ml

Preparation

Pour absinthe into a Champagne flute. Top with chilled Champagne. Gently stir until the drink turns opalescent.

Garnish

None typically, though a lemon twist is sometimes used as a modern addition.

La Louisiane

HerbalSpicyBitterSweetWoodyAnise
  • Rye Whiskey45 ml
  • Bénédictine15 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth15 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Peychaud's Bitters2 dash

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Rinse the chilled glass with absinthe, discarding any excess. In a mixing glass, combine rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, Bénédictine, and Peychaud's Bitters. Add ice and stir until well-chilled. Strain into the prepared glass. Garnish with a Luxardo cherry.

Garnish

Luxardo cherry

Zombie

TropicalFruitySweetSpicyHerbal
  • White Rum45 ml
  • Dark Rum45 ml
  • Gold Rum30 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur7 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Falernum15 ml
  • Grenadine10 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml

Preparation

1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until chilled. 3. Strain into a zombie glass filled with ice cubes. 4. Garnish with a mint sprig, cherry, and orange slice.

Garnish

Mint sprig, cherry, orange slice
