Cocktails with Honey Syrup

Bee's Knees

CitrusFloralHerbalSweet
  • Gin60 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Honey Syrup22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Gold Rush

CitrusFruityVanillaWoody
  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Honey Syrup30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and place the peel in the glass.

Garnish

Lemon peel, expressed and placed in the glass.
