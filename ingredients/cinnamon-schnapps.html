Cocktails with Cinnamon Schnapps

Oatmeal Cookie

SweetCreamyVanillaNuttyCinnamonCoffee
  • Vodka30 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur15 ml
  • Cinnamon Schnapps15 ml
  • Irish Cream Liqueur30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a dusting of cinnamon or graham cracker rim.

Garnish

Cinnamon powder dusting or optional graham cracker rim
