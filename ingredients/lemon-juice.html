Royal Arrival

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal

  • Lemon Juice15 ml

Gin Sour

CitrusHerbalSourSweet
  • Gin60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker without ice (dry shake) for 15 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 2. Add ice to the shaker and shake vigorously for another 15-20 seconds to chill and further emulsify the mixture. 3. Double strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. If serving in a rocks glass, fill with fresh ice. 4. Garnish with a lemon peel twist or a few drops of bitters on the egg white foam (if used).

Garnish

Lemon peel twist or a few drops of bitters on the egg white foam (if used). Express the oils from the lemon peel over the drink before placing it on the rim.

Brandy Crusta

CitrusFruityFloralVanilla
  • Brandy60 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur10 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Syrup2 ml
  • Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Prepare the glass: Moisten the rim of a chilled coupe or wine glass with a lemon wedge. Dip the rim in sugar to create a sugared crust. 2. Prepare the lemon peel: Carefully peel a lemon in one long, continuous spiral. Drape the peel inside the glass, so it lines the entire inner surface. 3. Chill the glass: Place the prepared glass in the freezer to chill thoroughly. 4. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine brandy, lemon juice, maraschino liqueur, sugar syrup, and bitters. 5. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 6. Shake well: Shake vigorously until well-chilled. 7. Strain: Strain the mixture into the prepared glass, being careful not to disturb the lemon peel. 8. Serve immediately.

Garnish

Large lemon peel, carefully peeled in one long spiral, to line the inside of the glass; sugared rim.

Smash

  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Mint Leaves8 leaves
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml

Mint sprig and a fresh fruit slice (e.g., lemon wheel, berry, or peach slice depending on the fruit used in the drink). Gently slap the mint sprig to release its aroma before placing it on the drink.
