Cocktails with Creme de Violette

Takumi's Aviation

CitrusFloralHerbalFruity
  • Gin45 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur7 ml
  • Creme de Violette7 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass.

Garnish

Brandied cherry
