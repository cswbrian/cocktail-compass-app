Cocktails with Cranberry Juice

Sex on the Beach

FruityTropicalSweet
  • Vodka40 ml
  • Peach Schnapps20 ml
  • Cranberry Juice40 ml
  • Orange Juice40 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes. 2. Add vodka, peach schnapps, cranberry juice, and orange juice. 3. Stir gently to combine. 4. Garnish with an orange slice or a cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice or cherry

Sea Breeze

FruityCitrusTart
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Cranberry Juice120 ml
  • Grapefruit Juice45 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add vodka, cranberry juice, and grapefruit juice. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel. Express the oils over the drink and then place on the rim of the glass.

Redheaded Slut

FruityHerbalSweet
  • Jägermeister15 ml
  • Peach Schnapps15 ml
  • Cranberry Juice15 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the ingredients in a shot glass in the following order: Peach Schnapps, Jägermeister, then Cranberry Juice. The layering effect is achieved by gently pouring each liquid over the back of a spoon to minimize mixing.

Garnish

None typically, but occasionally a cherry can be added.

Cosmopolitan

FruityCitrusSour
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Orange Liqueur (e.g., Cointreau)15 ml
  • Cranberry Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine vodka, cranberry juice, lime juice, and orange liqueur in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and place the peel on the rim to garnish.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed on the rim.
