Cocktails with Black Walnut Bitters

Four Score

NuttyWoodyVanillaCaramelBitterSpicySweet
  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Nocino (Walnut Liqueur)15 ml
  • Black Walnut Bitters2 dashes
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled, about 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Express the oil from an orange peel over the drink, then discard the peel. Garnish with a brandied cherry.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and discarded, plus a brandied cherry.
