Cocktails with Carbonated Water

John Collins

CitrusHerbalFruitySweetSourRefreshing
  • Gin60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Carbonated Water80 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a Collins glass. 2. Add gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice. 3. Shake well until well-chilled. 4. Strain into the chilled Collins glass filled with ice. 5. Top with carbonated water. 6. Gently stir to combine. 7. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon wheel and a maraschino cherry. The lemon wheel provides an aromatic citrus element, while the maraschino cherry adds a touch of sweetness and visual appeal.
