Cocktails with Irish cream liqueur

Orgasm

CreamyVanillaChocolateCoffeeNuttySweet
  • Coffee liqueur25 ml
  • Irish cream liqueur25 ml
  • Cream liqueur25 ml
  • Heavy cream25 ml

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass or small rocks glass. Garnish as desired.

Garnish

Optional: Grated nutmeg or chocolate shavings

Oatmeal Cookie

SweetCreamyVanillaNuttyCinnamonCoffee
  • Vodka30 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur15 ml
  • Cinnamon Schnapps15 ml
  • Irish Cream Liqueur30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a dusting of cinnamon or graham cracker rim.

Garnish

Cinnamon powder dusting or optional graham cracker rim

Irish Car Bomb

CoffeeChocolateCreamyBitterEarthyWoody
  • Irish Whiskey30 ml
  • Irish Stout236 ml
  • Irish Cream Liqueur15 ml

Preparation

1. Pour the Irish stout into a pint glass, filling approximately three-quarters full. 2. Carefully layer the Irish cream liqueur on top of the Irish whiskey in a shot glass. 3. Drop the shot glass into the stout and consume immediately.

Garnish

None

Baby Guinness

ChocolateCoffeeCreamyVanilla
  • Coffee Liqueur45 ml
  • Irish Cream Liqueur15 ml

Preparation

Carefully layer the Irish cream liqueur on top of the coffee liqueur in a shot glass. Tilt a spoon upside down over the coffee liqueur and gently pour the Irish cream over the back of the spoon to ensure it floats. Serve immediately.

Garnish

None
