Cocktails with Aged Cuban Rum

Daisy de Santiago

FruityCitrusTropicalVanilla
  • Aged Cuban Rum60 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur7 ml
  • Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. In a shaker, combine the aged Cuban rum, fresh lime juice, simple syrup, and maraschino liqueur. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 5. Double strain into the chilled glass. 6. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Garnish

Lime wedge
