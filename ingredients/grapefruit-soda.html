Cocktails with Grapefruit Soda

Smoky Paloma

SmokyCitrusFruitySaltyBitter
  • Mezcal60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Agave Nectar7 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda120 ml
  • Pinch of Salt1 pinch

Preparation

1. If desired, rim a highball glass with salt. 2. Fill the glass with ice. 3. Add mezcal, lime juice, and agave nectar. 4. Top with grapefruit soda. 5. Gently stir to combine. 6. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.

Garnish

Grapefruit wedge and salt rim (optional)

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 6

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 6

Paloma

CitrusFruityBitterSaltyTropical
  • Tequila Blanco60 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda90 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

Prepare the glass by rimming it with salt, if desired. Fill a highball glass with ice. Add Tequila Blanco and Lime Juice, then top with Grapefruit Soda. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.

Garnish

Grapefruit wedge and salt rim (optional). Cut a grapefruit wedge and rub it along the rim of the glass. Then, roll the rim in coarse salt before adding ice and the cocktail.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 7

Body: 4

Complexity: 5

Booziness: 5

Charred Orange Paloma

CitrusSmokyBitterFruityTropical
  • Tequila Blanco60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda90 ml
  • Orange Wheel2 wheel

Preparation

First, char an orange wheel using a kitchen torch or by placing it under a broiler until slightly blackened. Rim a highball glass with smoked salt. Fill the glass with ice. Add tequila, fresh lime juice, and agave nectar to the glass. Top with grapefruit soda. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with the charred orange wheel.

Garnish

Charred orange wheel and a pinch of smoked salt on the rim.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 7

Body: 4

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5
