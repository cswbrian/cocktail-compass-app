Cocktails with Sugar Syrup

Revolving Door

SpicyBitterHerbalWoody
  • Rye Whiskey45 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Sugar Syrup1 bar spoon

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or cocktail glass. 2. In a mixing glass, combine the rye whiskey, Benedictine, sugar syrup, and Angostura bitters. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass and stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). 4. Strain the mixture into the chilled glass. 5. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and discard the peel.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and discarded.

Brandy Crusta

CitrusFruityFloralVanilla
  • Brandy60 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur10 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Syrup2 ml
  • Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Prepare the glass: Moisten the rim of a chilled coupe or wine glass with a lemon wedge. Dip the rim in sugar to create a sugared crust. 2. Prepare the lemon peel: Carefully peel a lemon in one long, continuous spiral. Drape the peel inside the glass, so it lines the entire inner surface. 3. Chill the glass: Place the prepared glass in the freezer to chill thoroughly. 4. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine brandy, lemon juice, maraschino liqueur, sugar syrup, and bitters. 5. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 6. Shake well: Shake vigorously until well-chilled. 7. Strain: Strain the mixture into the prepared glass, being careful not to disturb the lemon peel. 8. Serve immediately.

Garnish

Large lemon peel, carefully peeled in one long spiral, to line the inside of the glass; sugared rim.
