Cocktails with Club Soda

Tom Collins

CitrusFruityHerbalSweetSour
  • Gin50 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Club Soda100 ml

Preparation

In a Collins glass filled with ice, combine gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup. Stir well to combine. Top with club soda. Gently stir again to incorporate the soda. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon wheel and a maraschino cherry
