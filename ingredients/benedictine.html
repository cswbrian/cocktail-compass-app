Cocktails with Benedictine

Chrysanthemum

FloralHerbalBitter
  • Dry Vermouth45 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add dry vermouth, Benedictine, and absinthe to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and then discard the peel.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and then discarded

Singapore Sling

FruityCitrusHerbalTropicalSpicy
  • Gin30 ml
  • Cherry Liqueur15 ml
  • Cointreau8 ml
  • Benedictine8 ml
  • Pineapple Juice120 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Grenadine10 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Preparation

1. Chill a sling glass or hurricane glass. 2. In a cocktail shaker, combine gin, cherry liqueur, Cointreau, Benedictine, pineapple juice, lime juice, and grenadine. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until the shaker is well-chilled. 5. Strain into the chilled glass filled with ice. 6. Top with a dash of Angostura bitters.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry

Wolfram

SpicyFruityHerbalBitterCitrus
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Cherry Heering15 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or Nick & Nora glass. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and drop it in.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the drink and dropped in.

Revolving Door

SpicyBitterHerbalWoody
  • Rye Whiskey45 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Sugar Syrup1 bar spoon

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or cocktail glass. 2. In a mixing glass, combine the rye whiskey, Benedictine, sugar syrup, and Angostura bitters. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass and stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). 4. Strain the mixture into the chilled glass. 5. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and discard the peel.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and discarded.

Gunsmoke

SmokySpicyHerbalBitterWoody
  • Rye Whiskey45 ml
  • Benedictine15 ml
  • Mezcal15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or rocks glass. 2. Add rye whiskey, mezcal, Benedictine, and Angostura bitters to a mixing glass. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass. 4. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). 5. Strain into the chilled glass. 6. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and place the peel in the glass or flame the orange peel for a more intense aroma.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass, or a flamed orange peel for added aromatics.
