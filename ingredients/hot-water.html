Cocktails with Hot Water

Grog

FruityCitrusSpicyTropicalWoody
  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Hot Water90 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Sugar1 tsp

Preparation

Combine dark rum, hot water, lime juice, and sugar in a mug. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved. Garnish with an orange slice and a cinnamon stick. Serve hot.

Garnish

Orange slice and a cinnamon stick

Blueberry Tea

NuttyFruityCitrusSweetWarm
  • Amaretto15 ml
  • Orange Liqueur15 ml
  • Hot Water90 ml

Preparation

Combine amaretto and orange liqueur in a mug. Top with hot water. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with an orange slice or lemon twist.

Garnish

Orange slice or lemon twist

Hot Buttered Rum

SpicyNuttyVanillaCreamyWoody
  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Butter2 tbsp
  • Brown Sugar1 tbsp
  • Honey1 tsp
  • Ground Cinnamon0 tsp
  • Ground Nutmeg0 tsp
  • Ground Cloves0 tsp
  • Ground Allspice0 tsp
  • Hot Water180 ml

Preparation

1. In a mug, combine the butter, brown sugar, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice. 2. Add the dark rum. 3. Top with hot water and stir until the butter is melted and all ingredients are well combined. 4. Garnish with a cinnamon stick or a star anise.

Garnish

Cinnamon stick or a star anise
