Cocktails with Brown Sugar

Bumbo

SpicySweetTropical
  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Water15 ml
  • Brown Sugar15 ml

Preparation

In a mug or heat-resistant glass, dissolve brown sugar in warm water. Add dark rum. Stir well. Grate fresh nutmeg over the top. Serve warm.

Garnish

Grated nutmeg

Hot Buttered Rum

SpicyNuttyVanillaCreamyWoody
  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Butter2 tbsp
  • Brown Sugar1 tbsp
  • Honey1 tsp
  • Ground Cinnamon0 tsp
  • Ground Nutmeg0 tsp
  • Ground Cloves0 tsp
  • Ground Allspice0 tsp
  • Hot Water180 ml

Preparation

1. In a mug, combine the butter, brown sugar, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice. 2. Add the dark rum. 3. Top with hot water and stir until the butter is melted and all ingredients are well combined. 4. Garnish with a cinnamon stick or a star anise.

Garnish

Cinnamon stick or a star anise

Irish Coffee

CoffeeVanillaCreamyNuttyWoodySpicy
  • Irish Whiskey50 ml
  • Hot Coffee120 ml
  • Brown Sugar1 tsp
  • Lightly Whipped Cream50 ml

Preparation

1. Warm a heatproof glass (such as a stemmed Irish coffee glass) by filling it with hot water, then discard the water. 2. Add brown sugar to the warmed glass. 3. Pour in hot coffee and stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. 4. Add Irish whiskey and stir gently to combine. 5. Gently pour lightly whipped cream over the back of a spoon, allowing it to float on top of the coffee. Do not stir. 6. Garnish with a grating of fresh nutmeg.

Garnish

Light grating of nutmeg on top of the cream.
