Cocktails with Half-and-Half

Moose Milk

CreamyVanillaNuttyChocolateSweetWoody
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Bourbon30 ml
  • Chocolate Liqueur30 ml
  • Whole Milk90 ml
  • Half-and-Half30 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Vanilla Extract2 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a large mug or glass. Garnish with freshly grated nutmeg and a dusting of cocoa powder.

Garnish

Grated nutmeg and a dusting of cocoa powder
