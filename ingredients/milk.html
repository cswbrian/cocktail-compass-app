Hoppel poppel

CreamyVanillaNuttySpicySweet
  • Genever30 ml
  • Egg1 whole
  • Milk60 ml
  • Sugar15 ml
  • Vanilla Extract2 dashes

Preparation

In a shaker (or saucepan), combine the egg, milk, sugar, genever and vanilla extract. Shake well, or whisk vigorously if preparing warm. If serving cold, add ice to shaker and shake until well-chilled. If serving hot, gently heat the mixture in a saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly, until warmed through but not boiling. Strain into a glass. Garnish with grated nutmeg or cinnamon.

Garnish

Grated nutmeg or cinnamon

Fluffy Critter

CreamyVanillaCitrusSweet
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Orange Liqueur10 ml
  • Milk30 ml
  • Heavy Cream15 ml
  • Vanilla Extract5 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass. Garnish with whipped cream and a sprinkle of chocolate shavings.

Garnish

Whipped cream and a sprinkle of chocolate shavings
