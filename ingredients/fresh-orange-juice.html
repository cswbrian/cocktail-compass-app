Cocktails with Fresh Orange Juice

Agua de Valencia

FruityCitrusSweet
  • Gin50 ml
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Cava100 ml
  • Fresh Orange Juice150 ml

Preparation

Combine gin, vodka, orange juice, and simple syrup (if using) in a pitcher. Stir well to combine. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld. Just before serving, top with chilled cava. Stir gently to combine. Pour into champagne flutes or wine glasses. Garnish with an orange slice or zest.

Garnish

Orange slice or zest, or optionally a few grapes.

Monkey Gland

CitrusFruityHerbalSweet
  • Gin50 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Grenadine10 ml
  • Fresh Orange Juice30 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until well-chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass.

Garnish

Orange twist, expressed and dropped in

Blood & Sand

FruityCitrusSmokyHerbal
  • Scotch Whisky22 ml
  • Cherry Heering22 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth22 ml
  • Fresh Orange Juice22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim.

Bronx

CitrusHerbalFruity
  • Gin45 ml
  • Fresh Orange Juice15 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or Martini glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange twist over the drink and use as garnish.

Garnish

Orange twist, expressed over the drink
