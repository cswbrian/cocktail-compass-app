Cocktails with High-Proof Rum (151 proof or higher)

Flaming Doctor Pepper

NuttySweetWarmAlmond
  • Amaretto22 ml
  • High-Proof Rum (151 proof or higher)22 ml
  • Beer (Light Lager)150 ml

Preparation

1. Pour the beer into a heat-resistant glass, leaving about an inch of space at the top. 2. In a separate heat-resistant shot glass, carefully layer the amaretto. 3. Float the high-proof rum on top of the amaretto in the shot glass. 4. Carefully ignite the rum in the shot glass using a long lighter or match. 5. Allow the flame to burn for a few seconds (no more than 5-10 seconds). 6. Extinguish the flame by dropping the shot glass into the beer-filled glass, be careful to use a bar spoon to avoid splashing. 7. Consume immediately and responsibly.

Garnish

None recommended due to the nature of the drink. Safety is the priority.
