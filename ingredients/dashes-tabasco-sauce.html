Cocktails with Dashes Tabasco Sauce

Red Snapper

SavorySpicyUmamiHerbalCitrus
  • Gin60 ml
  • Tomato Juice120 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Dashes Worcestershire Sauce2 dash
  • Dashes Tabasco Sauce2 dash
  • Pinch Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Pinch Black Pepper1 pinch

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Roll (or gently stir) to combine. Strain into an ice-filled highball glass. Garnish.

Garnish

Celery stalk, lemon wedge, and/or olives. Can also use a spiced rim.
