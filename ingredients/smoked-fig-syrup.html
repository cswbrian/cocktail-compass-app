Cocktails with Smoked Fig Syrup

Smoked Fig and Bourbon Sour

SmokyFruityCitrusWoodyVanilla
  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Orange Liqueur7 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Egg White15 ml
  • Smoked Fig Syrup22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker without ice. Dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a smoked fig slice on a cocktail pick or an expressed orange peel.

Garnish

Smoked fig slice on a cocktail pick, or expressed orange peel.
