Cocktails with Tonic Water

Gin and Tonic

BitterCitrusHerbal
  • Gin50 ml
  • Tonic Water150 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add gin. Top with tonic water. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel, expressed and dropped in. Alternatively, a sprig of rosemary can be used for an herbal variation.
