Cocktails with Honey Syrup (1:1)

Orange Tundra

CitrusFruitySweetRefreshing
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Orange Juice15 ml
  • Honey Syrup (1:1)10 ml
  • Soda Water30 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a highball glass. 2. In a shaker with ice, combine the vodka, orange juice, and honey syrup. 3. Shake well until chilled. 4. Strain into the chilled highball glass filled with ice. 5. Top with soda water. 6. Garnish with an orange slice or twist.

Garnish

Orange slice or twist
