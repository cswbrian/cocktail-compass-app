Cocktails with Fresh Lime Juice

Mai Tai

TropicalNuttyCitrusFruityHerbal
  • Aged Jamaican Rum30 ml
  • Aged Rhum Agricole30 ml
  • Orange Curaçao15 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice22 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a double rocks glass filled with crushed ice. Garnish as directed.

Garnish

Mint sprig and lime wheel, express lime peel over the drink.

Old Cuban

FruityCitrusHerbalSpicyTropical
  • Aged Rum45 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Mint Leaves6 leaves
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Sparkling Wine60 ml

Preparation

Gently muddle mint leaves with simple syrup in a shaker. Add aged rum, lime juice, and Angostura bitters. Fill the shaker with ice and shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass. Top with sparkling wine. Garnish with a mint sprig (slapped gently to release aromas).

Garnish

Mint sprig (slapped gently to release aromas)

Queen's Park Swizzle

TropicalHerbalCitrusSpicyWoodyGrassy
  • Aged Rum60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Mint Leaves8 leaves
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Gently Muddle: In a highball glass, gently muddle the mint leaves with the simple syrup. Be careful not to over-muddle, as this can release bitter compounds. 2. Add Ingredients: Add the aged rum, lime juice, and Angostura bitters to the glass. 3. Fill with Crushed Ice: Fill the glass completely with crushed ice. 4. Swizzle: Insert a swizzle stick (or a long spoon) into the glass and swizzle vigorously, using a back-and-forth motion. The goal is to chill and aerate the drink simultaneously, creating a frosty effect. Continue swizzling until the glass is frosted on the outside. 5. Add More Ice: Add more crushed ice to form a cone above the rim of the glass. 6. Garnish: Garnish with a generous sprig of fresh mint. Optionally, dust the mint sprig with powdered sugar.

Garnish

Mint sprig, dusted with powdered sugar (optional)

Ramos Gin Fizz

CitrusFloralCreamyFruityHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Orange Flower Water3 drops
  • Heavy Cream30 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice15 ml
  • Egg White1 pc
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp

Preparation

1. Chill a Collins glass. 2. In a shaker, combine gin, heavy cream, lemon juice, lime juice, egg white, orange flower water, and sugar. 3. Dry shake (without ice) vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 4. Add ice to the shaker and shake again vigorously for at least 1 minute, or until the shaker is very cold. 5. Strain the mixture into the chilled Collins glass. 6. Slowly top with soda water to create a frothy head, pouring in a thin stream. 7. Allow the foam to rise above the rim of the glass. 8. Optional spritz of orange flower water on top. 9. Garnish.

Garnish

Orange blossom water spritz and floated edible flowers such as pansies or violets

Mango Sour

FruityCitrusTropicalSweetSour
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Fresh Mango Puree30 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Preparation

1. If using egg white, dry shake all ingredients without ice for 15 seconds. 2. Add ice to the shaker. 3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled (approximately 15-20 seconds). 4. Double strain into a chilled coupe or sour glass. 5. Garnish with a mango slice or lime wedge.

Garnish

Mango slice or lime wedge

Spicy Fifty

SpicyFloralCitrusSweetFruity
  • Vodka50 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur20 ml
  • Simple Syrup infused with Red Chili2 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with a thin slice of red chili pepper on the rim.

Garnish

Thin slice of red chili pepper, placed delicately on the rim of the glass.

Daiquiri

CitrusFruityTropicalSourSweet
  • White Rum60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or cocktail glass. 2. Add the white rum, fresh lime juice, and simple syrup to a cocktail shaker. 3. Fill the shaker with ice. 4. Shake well until the shaker is well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. 5. Double strain into the chilled glass. 6. Garnish with a lime wheel or lime wedge (optional).

Garnish

Lime wheel or lime wedge (optional)

Charred Orange Paloma

CitrusSmokyBitterFruityTropical
  • Tequila Blanco60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda90 ml
  • Orange Wheel2 wheel

Preparation

First, char an orange wheel using a kitchen torch or by placing it under a broiler until slightly blackened. Rim a highball glass with smoked salt. Fill the glass with ice. Add tequila, fresh lime juice, and agave nectar to the glass. Top with grapefruit soda. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with the charred orange wheel.

Garnish

Charred orange wheel and a pinch of smoked salt on the rim.
