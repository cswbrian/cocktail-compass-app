Cocktails with Sweet and Sour Mix

Blue Hawaii

FruityTropicalSweet
  • Light Rum30 ml
  • Vodka30 ml
  • Blue Curaçao30 ml
  • Pineapple Juice90 ml
  • Sweet and Sour Mix30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a hurricane glass filled with ice.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge and cocktail cherry. Optionally, a paper umbrella for added flair.

Sweetness: 8

Sourness: 4

Body: 5

Complexity: 4

Booziness: 4
