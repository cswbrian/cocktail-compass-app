Cocktails with Agave Nectar

Smoky Paloma

SmokyCitrusFruitySaltyBitter
  • Mezcal60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Agave Nectar7 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda120 ml
  • Pinch of Salt1 pinch

Preparation

1. If desired, rim a highball glass with salt. 2. Fill the glass with ice. 3. Add mezcal, lime juice, and agave nectar. 4. Top with grapefruit soda. 5. Gently stir to combine. 6. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.

Garnish

Grapefruit wedge and salt rim (optional)

Smoked Pineapple Margarita

SmokyFruityCitrusTropicalSweetSour
  • Blanco Tequila50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lime Juice50 ml
  • Smoked Pineapple Chunks50 grams
  • Agave Nectar15 ml

Preparation

1. Smoke pineapple chunks using a smoker or grill until lightly charred. 2. Muddle smoked pineapple chunks in a shaker. 3. Add tequila, fresh lime juice, orange liqueur and agave nectar. 4. Add ice and shake well until chilled. 5. Double strain into a salt-rimmed (optional) rocks glass filled with ice. 6. Garnish with a pineapple wedge.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge, salt rim (optional)

Oaxaca Old Fashioned

SmokyHerbalSpicyEarthySweet
  • Tequila45 ml
  • Mezcal15 ml
  • Agave Nectar5 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Combine tequila, mezcal, agave nectar, and Angostura bitters in a mixing glass. Add ice and stir until well-chilled and properly diluted (about 20-30 seconds). Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Express the oils from an orange peel over the glass, then drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass.

Tommy's Margarita

CitrusFruityTropical
  • Tequila60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Agave Nectar20 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a coupe or rocks glass. 2. Add Tequila, lime juice, and agave nectar to a shaker with ice. 3. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 4. Strain into the chilled glass. 5. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel

Mezcal Margarita

SmokyCitrusFruityEarthySalty
  • Mezcal50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lime Juice25 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml

Preparation

1. Prepare the glass: If desired, salt the rim of a chilled rocks glass by running a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in salt. 2. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine the mezcal, lime juice, orange liqueur, and agave nectar. 3. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 4. Shake well: Shake vigorously until well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. 5. Strain: Strain the mixture into the prepared rocks glass filled with fresh ice. 6. Garnish: Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel, optionally a salted rim. Salt application: rub a lime wedge around the rim, then dip into salt.

Charred Orange Paloma

CitrusSmokyBitterFruityTropical
  • Tequila Blanco60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda90 ml
  • Orange Wheel2 wheel

Preparation

First, char an orange wheel using a kitchen torch or by placing it under a broiler until slightly blackened. Rim a highball glass with smoked salt. Fill the glass with ice. Add tequila, fresh lime juice, and agave nectar to the glass. Top with grapefruit soda. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with the charred orange wheel.

Garnish

Charred orange wheel and a pinch of smoked salt on the rim.
