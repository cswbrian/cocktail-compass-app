Cocktails with Lemon-Lime Soda

Monaco

FruitySweetCitrusYeasty
  • Light Beer30 ml
  • Grenadine30 ml
  • Lemon-Lime Soda60 ml

Preparation

Pour the grenadine into a glass. Add the lemon-lime soda. Gently pour the beer on top to create a layered effect, or stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Garnish

Lemon wedge or slice

Lynchburg Lemonade

CitrusFruitySweet
  • Whiskey40 ml
  • Triple Sec20 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Lemon-Lime Soda120 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice. 2. Add whiskey, triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup. 3. Top with lemon-lime soda. 4. Stir gently to combine. 5. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Garnish

Lemon wedge
