Cocktails with Lillet Blanc

Corpse Reviver #2

CitrusHerbalFloralBitter
  • Gin22 ml
  • Orange Liqueur22 ml
  • Lillet Blanc22 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and then drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped into the glass.

Delilah

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur15 ml
  • Lillet Blanc15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or a brandied cherry.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim, or a brandied cherry.

Old Etonian

FruityCitrusHerbalBitter
  • Gin30 ml
  • Apricot Brandy15 ml
  • Lillet Blanc30 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Chill a coupe or cocktail glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange twist over the drink and discard.

Garnish

Orange twist, expressed and discarded
