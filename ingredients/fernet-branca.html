Cocktails with Fernet Branca

Fernet con Coca

BitterHerbalSpicySweet
  • Fernet Branca50 ml
  • Coca-Cola120 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add Fernet Branca. Top with Coca-Cola. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with a lemon or orange peel, if desired.

Garnish

Lemon or orange peel (optional)

Hanky Panky

BitterHerbalCitrusFloral
  • Gin45 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth22 ml
  • Fernet-Branca2 ml

Preparation

Chill a cocktail glass. Combine gin and sweet vermouth in a mixing glass filled with ice. Add Fernet-Branca. Stir well until thoroughly chilled. Strain into the chilled cocktail glass. Express the oils from an orange zest over the drink and drop into the glass.

Garnish

Orange zest, expressed over the drink and then dropped into the glass.

Toronto

BitterHerbalSpicyWoody
  • Canadian Whisky60 ml
  • Fernet-Branca15 ml
  • Simple Syrup7 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled, about 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and discard or place on the rim.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and then discarded or placed on the rim.
