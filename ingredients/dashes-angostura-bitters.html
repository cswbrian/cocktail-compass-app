Cocktails with dashes Angostura bitters

Chartreuse Swizzle

HerbalCitrusTropicalSpicy
  • Green Chartreuse1 oz
  • Falernum0 5 oz
  • Lime Juice1 oz
  • Pineapple Juice1 oz
  • dashes Angostura bitters4 dashes

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a tall glass filled with crushed ice. Swizzle vigorously with a swizzle stick or bar spoon until the glass is frosted. Add more crushed ice to fill. Garnish with a mint sprig and grated nutmeg.

Garnish

Mint sprig, grated nutmeg
