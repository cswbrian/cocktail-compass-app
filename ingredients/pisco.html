Cocktails with Pisco

Aguaymanto Sour

FruityCitrusTropicalSourSweet
  • Pisco60 ml
  • Aguaymanto Puree30 ml
  • Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White1 pc

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker without ice. Dry shake vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass.

Garnish

3 drops of Angostura bitters, Aguaymanto fruit half

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 7

Body: 5

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5

Piscola

FruityVanillaCitrusSweet
  • Pisco60 ml
  • Cola120 ml
  • Ice60 grams

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add pisco and cola. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lemon or lime wedge, if desired.

Garnish

Lemon or lime wedge, optional.

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 2

Body: 4

Complexity: 3

Booziness: 4

Pisco Sour

CitrusFruitySourHerbal
  • Pisco75 ml
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup25 ml
  • Egg White25 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker without ice and dry shake vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white. Add ice and shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or sour glass.

Garnish

3 drops of Angostura bitters, feathered with a toothpick

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 8

Body: 7

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 6
