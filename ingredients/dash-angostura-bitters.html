Cocktails with Dash Angostura Bitters

Cobra's Fang

TropicalFruityCitrusSpicyHerbal
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Passion Fruit Syrup15 ml
  • Falernum15 ml
  • Orange Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Dash Angostura Bitters1 dash

Preparation

1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 3. Strain into a large tiki mug or glass filled with ice. 4. Garnish with a mint sprig, an orange slice, and a cherry.

Garnish

Mint sprig, orange slice, and a cherry

Horsefeather

SpicyCitrusHerbalWoody
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Ginger Beer120 ml
  • Dash Angostura Bitters2 dash

Preparation

Combine rye whiskey, lime juice, and Angostura bitters in a highball glass filled with ice. Top with ginger beer. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge. Optionally add a piece of candied ginger.

Garnish

Lime wedge, optionally candied ginger
