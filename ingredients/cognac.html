Fish House Punch

FruityCitrusTropicalWoodySpicy
  • Dark Jamaican Rum250 ml
  • Cognac250 ml
  • Peach Brandy250 ml
  • Lemon Juice375 ml
  • Simple Syrup (1:1)250 ml
  • Water750 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a large punch bowl. Add a large block of ice. Stir well until chilled and slightly diluted. Let sit for at least 1 hour for flavors to meld. Add lemon slices and/or grated nutmeg before serving. Ladle into punch glasses or cups.

Garnish

Lemon slices and/or grated nutmeg floated on top.

Vieux Carré

HerbalSpicyBitterFruityWoody
  • Rye Whiskey22 ml
  • Cognac22 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth22 ml
  • Bénédictine7 ml
  • Peychaud's Bitters2 dashes
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into a chilled rocks glass or coupe. Express the oils from a lemon peel over the drink and drop the peel into the glass. Optionally, garnish with a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon peel, expressed and placed in the glass. Optional: maraschino cherry.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 3

Body: 7

Complexity: 9

Booziness: 8

Sidecar

CitrusFruitySweetSour
  • Cognac50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe or martini glass. If desired, rim the glass with sugar. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker. Fill with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink, then use the peel as garnish on the rim.

Garnish

Orange peel twist, expressed over the drink and then placed on the rim of the glass. Alternatively, sugar rim.

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 7

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 7

Tom and Jerry

CreamySpicySweetVanillaNuttyWoody
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Cognac15 ml
  • Egg2 whole
  • Sugar20 g
  • Ground Cinnamon0 pinch
  • Ground Nutmeg0 pinch
  • Ground Cloves0 pinch
  • Hot Milk120 ml

Preparation

1. Separate the egg white and yolk. 2. Whisk the egg white until stiff peaks form. Gradually add half of the sugar while whisking. Set aside. 3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolk with the remaining sugar until light and creamy. Add the cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves to the yolk mixture and combine. 4. Gently fold the egg white mixture into the yolk mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. 5. In a preheated mug, combine the dark rum and cognac. 6. Add 2-3 tablespoons of the batter to the mug. 7. Top with hot milk, stirring gently to combine. 8. Grate fresh nutmeg on top for garnish.

Garnish

Freshly grated nutmeg on top

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 2

Body: 8

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5
