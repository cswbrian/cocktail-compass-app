Cocktails with Bourbon Whiskey

Mint Julep

HerbalSweetWoodyEarthyVanilla
  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Fresh Mint Leaves10 leaves
  • Simple Syrup10 ml

Preparation

1. Gently muddle mint leaves and simple syrup in a julep cup or sturdy rocks glass. Avoid over-muddling, which can release bitter compounds. 2. Fill the cup completely with crushed ice. 3. Add bourbon. 4. Gently stir until the cup is frosted. 5. Add more crushed ice to form a mound. 6. Garnish with a generous sprig of fresh mint. 7. Optionally, dust with powdered sugar.

Garnish

A generous sprig of fresh mint, lightly slapped to release its aroma. Optionally, dust with powdered sugar.

Old Fashioned

BitterWoodySpicyVanillaCitrus
  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes
  • Water15 ml

Preparation

Place sugar cube in an old-fashioned glass. Add bitters and water. Muddle until sugar is dissolved. Fill the glass with ice. Add bourbon. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with an orange peel, expressed and inserted. Optionally add a brandied cherry.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and inserted, or optional brandied cherry

Horse's Neck

CitrusSpicyWoodyVanilla
  • Bourbon Whiskey44 ml
  • Ginger Ale120 ml
  • Angostura bitters3 dashes

Preparation

1. Prepare the lemon spiral by cutting a long, continuous strip of peel from the lemon, starting at the top and working your way around. 2. Drape the lemon spiral over the rim of a highball glass, with the majority of the peel hanging outside the glass. 3. Fill the glass with ice cubes. 4. Add Bourbon whiskey to the glass. 5. Add Angostura bitters. 6. Top with ginger ale. 7. Gently stir to combine. 8. Serve immediately.

Garnish

Long spiral of lemon peel, draped over the rim of the glass.

Whiskey Sour

CitrusSourFruityWoodyVanilla
  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Egg White30 ml

Preparation

1. Combine bourbon whiskey, fresh lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white (if using) in a shaker. 2. Dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 3. Add ice to the shaker and shake well until chilled. 4. Strain into an ice-filled rocks glass. 5. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice and maraschino cherry

Campfire Cocktail

SmokyVanillaMapleChocolateWoodySweet
  • Bourbon Whiskey45 ml
  • Smoked Liquor10 ml
  • Chocolate Bitters2 dashes
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a rocks glass. 2. Add bourbon, smoked liquor, maple syrup, and chocolate bitters to a mixing glass. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass. 4. Stir until well-chilled (approx. 20 seconds). 5. Strain into the chilled rocks glass filled with one large ice cube. 6. Toast a marshmallow and place it on a skewer. 7. Garnish with the toasted marshmallow.

Garnish

Toasted Marshmallow, skewered
