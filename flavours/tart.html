Cocktails with Tart flavor

Sea Breeze

FruityCitrusTart
  • Vodka45 ml
  • Cranberry Juice120 ml
  • Grapefruit Juice45 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add vodka, cranberry juice, and grapefruit juice. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel. Express the oils over the drink and then place on the rim of the glass.

Diki-Diki

FruityCitrusSpicySweetTart
  • Apple Brandy45 ml
  • Swedish Punsch15 ml
  • Fresh Grapefruit Juice22 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

Garnish

Grapefruit twist, expressed over the drink

Greyhound

FruityCitrusTart
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Grapefruit Juice120 ml

Preparation

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add vodka. Top with grapefruit juice. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge, if desired.

Garnish

Grapefruit wedge or slice (optional)

Vargtass

FruityCitrusTart
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Lingonberry Concentrate30 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a rocks glass or old-fashioned glass. 2. Add vodka and lingonberry concentrate to the glass. 3. Add ice cubes. 4. Stir gently to combine. 5. Garnish with fresh or frozen lingonberries, if desired.

Garnish

Lingonberries (fresh or frozen), optional
