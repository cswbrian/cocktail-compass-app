Sazerac

HerbalSpicyBitterCitrusWoody
  • Rye Whiskey60 ml
  • Absinthe7 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc
  • Peychaud's Bitters3 dashes

Preparation

Chill an Old Fashioned glass. In a separate mixing glass, muddle the sugar cube with the Peychaud's Bitters. Add the rye whiskey and ice. Stir well until chilled. Rinse the chilled Old Fashioned glass with the absinthe, coating the inside of the glass, then discard the excess. Strain the whiskey mixture into the absinthe-rinsed glass. Express the oil from the lemon peel over the glass and discard.

Garnish

Lemon peel twist, expressed over the glass and discarded. The oils add aroma and a hint of citrus without adding juice.

  • Dry Vermouth15 ml

Black Velvet

YeastyBitterRoastedFruityCreamy
  • Champagne90 ml
  • Stout90 ml

Preparation

1. Chill both stout and champagne. 2. Gently pour chilled champagne into a flute glass, filling halfway. 3. Slowly pour the chilled stout over the back of a spoon so that it layers on top of the champagne, filling the glass. Be careful to maintain the separation of the liquids.

Garnish

None

Smoked Vanilla Espresso Martini

  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Freshly Brewed Espresso (cooled)30 ml

Black and Tan

BitterYeastyRoasty
  • Pale Ale or Lager180 ml
  • Stout180 ml

Preparation

1. Gently pour the stout into a pint glass, filling it halfway. 2. Using a spoon held upside down just above the surface of the stout, slowly pour the pale ale or lager over the back of the spoon. This distributes the lighter beer evenly and prevents it from mixing with the stout. Aim to create a distinct layer of pale ale/lager floating on top of the stout.

Garnish

None

Preparation

Casino

CitrusFruityHerbalBitter
  • Gin45 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur7 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

Chill a coupe or Martini glass. Add all ingredients to a shaker filled with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass.

Garnish

Maraschino cherry, skewered

Mezcal Negroni

BitterHerbalSmokyCitrusEarthy
  • Mezcal30 ml
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into an old-fashioned glass filled with ice. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and drop the peel into the glass.

Garnish

Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass.
