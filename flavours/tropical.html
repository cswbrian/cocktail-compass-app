Tequila Sunrise

FruityCitrusSweetTropical
  • Tequila60 ml
  • Grenadine15 ml
  • Orange Juice120 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a highball glass with ice. 2. Add Tequila. 3. Top with orange juice. 4. Gently pour grenadine down the side of the glass so it settles at the bottom, creating the sunrise effect. Do not stir. 5. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Orange slice and a maraschino cherry. The orange slice adds a citrus aroma and visual appeal, while the cherry complements the sweetness of the grenadine.

Queen's Park Swizzle

TropicalHerbalCitrusSpicyWoodyGrassy
  • Aged Rum60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup22 ml
  • Mint Leaves8 leaves
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Gently Muddle: In a highball glass, gently muddle the mint leaves with the simple syrup. Be careful not to over-muddle, as this can release bitter compounds. 2. Add Ingredients: Add the aged rum, lime juice, and Angostura bitters to the glass. 3. Fill with Crushed Ice: Fill the glass completely with crushed ice. 4. Swizzle: Insert a swizzle stick (or a long spoon) into the glass and swizzle vigorously, using a back-and-forth motion. The goal is to chill and aerate the drink simultaneously, creating a frosty effect. Continue swizzling until the glass is frosted on the outside. 5. Add More Ice: Add more crushed ice to form a cone above the rim of the glass. 6. Garnish: Garnish with a generous sprig of fresh mint. Optionally, dust the mint sprig with powdered sugar.

Garnish

Mint sprig, dusted with powdered sugar (optional)

Flaming Volcano

TropicalFruitySweetSpicy
  • Light Rum60 ml
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Brandy30 ml
  • Orgeat Syrup15 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Orange Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Cube1 pc

Preparation

1. Combine light rum, dark rum, brandy, pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and orgeat syrup in a shaker with ice. 2. Shake well until chilled. 3. Strain into a volcano bowl or large serving dish. 4. Soak a sugar cube in high-proof rum (at least 75% ABV). 5. Place the sugar cube on a heat-resistant surface in the center of the bowl. 6. Carefully ignite the sugar cube using a long lighter or match. 7. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and cherry.

Garnish

Pineapple wedge, cherry, and a flaming sugar cube soaked in high-proof rum. Ensure sugar cube is placed on a heat-resistant surface within the bowl before ignition.
