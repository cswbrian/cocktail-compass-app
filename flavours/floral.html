Ramos Gin Fizz

CitrusFloralCreamyFruityHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Orange Flower Water3 drops
  • Heavy Cream30 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice15 ml
  • Egg White1 pc
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp

Preparation

1. Chill a Collins glass. 2. In a shaker, combine gin, heavy cream, lemon juice, lime juice, egg white, orange flower water, and sugar. 3. Dry shake (without ice) vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 4. Add ice to the shaker and shake again vigorously for at least 1 minute, or until the shaker is very cold. 5. Strain the mixture into the chilled Collins glass. 6. Slowly top with soda water to create a frothy head, pouring in a thin stream. 7. Allow the foam to rise above the rim of the glass. 8. Optional spritz of orange flower water on top. 9. Garnish.

Garnish

Orange blossom water spritz and floated edible flowers such as pansies or violets

Brandy Crusta

CitrusFruityFloralVanilla
  • Brandy60 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur10 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Sugar Syrup2 ml
  • Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

1. Prepare the glass: Moisten the rim of a chilled coupe or wine glass with a lemon wedge. Dip the rim in sugar to create a sugared crust. 2. Prepare the lemon peel: Carefully peel a lemon in one long, continuous spiral. Drape the peel inside the glass, so it lines the entire inner surface. 3. Chill the glass: Place the prepared glass in the freezer to chill thoroughly. 4. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine brandy, lemon juice, maraschino liqueur, sugar syrup, and bitters. 5. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 6. Shake well: Shake vigorously until well-chilled. 7. Strain: Strain the mixture into the prepared glass, being careful not to disturb the lemon peel. 8. Serve immediately.

Garnish

Large lemon peel, carefully peeled in one long spiral, to line the inside of the glass; sugared rim.
