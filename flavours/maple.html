Cocktails with Maple flavor

Last of the Mohicans

WoodyVanillaSpicyCitrusChocolateMaple
  • Bourbon45 ml
  • Chocolate Bitters2 ml
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

Combine bourbon, maple syrup, and chocolate bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass filled with a large ice cube. Express the oils from an orange zest over the drink and place on the rim.

Garnish

Orange zest twist, expressed over the drink and then placed on the rim.

Campfire Cocktail

SmokyVanillaMapleChocolateWoodySweet
  • Bourbon Whiskey45 ml
  • Smoked Liquor10 ml
  • Chocolate Bitters2 dashes
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a rocks glass. 2. Add bourbon, smoked liquor, maple syrup, and chocolate bitters to a mixing glass. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass. 4. Stir until well-chilled (approx. 20 seconds). 5. Strain into the chilled rocks glass filled with one large ice cube. 6. Toast a marshmallow and place it on a skewer. 7. Garnish with the toasted marshmallow.

Garnish

Toasted Marshmallow, skewered
