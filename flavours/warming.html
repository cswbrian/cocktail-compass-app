Gunfire

CoffeeRumBittersweetWarmingEarthy
  • Dark Rum50 ml
  • Hot Coffee150 ml

Preparation

Pour the dark rum into a mug or heat-resistant glass. Top with hot coffee. Stir gently to combine.

Garnish

None traditionally, but a dusting of nutmeg or a coffee bean can add a touch of elegance.
