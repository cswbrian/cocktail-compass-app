Ramos Gin Fizz

CitrusFloralCreamyFruityHerbal
  • Gin45 ml
  • Orange Flower Water3 drops
  • Heavy Cream30 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice15 ml
  • Egg White1 pc
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Sugar2 tsp

Preparation

1. Chill a Collins glass. 2. In a shaker, combine gin, heavy cream, lemon juice, lime juice, egg white, orange flower water, and sugar. 3. Dry shake (without ice) vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 4. Add ice to the shaker and shake again vigorously for at least 1 minute, or until the shaker is very cold. 5. Strain the mixture into the chilled Collins glass. 6. Slowly top with soda water to create a frothy head, pouring in a thin stream. 7. Allow the foam to rise above the rim of the glass. 8. Optional spritz of orange flower water on top. 9. Garnish.

Garnish

Orange blossom water spritz and floated edible flowers such as pansies or violets

Irish Coffee

CoffeeVanillaCreamyNuttyWoodySpicy
  • Irish Whiskey50 ml
  • Hot Coffee120 ml
  • Brown Sugar1 tsp
  • Lightly Whipped Cream50 ml

Preparation

1. Warm a heatproof glass (such as a stemmed Irish coffee glass) by filling it with hot water, then discard the water. 2. Add brown sugar to the warmed glass. 3. Pour in hot coffee and stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. 4. Add Irish whiskey and stir gently to combine. 5. Gently pour lightly whipped cream over the back of a spoon, allowing it to float on top of the coffee. Do not stir. 6. Garnish with a grating of fresh nutmeg.

Garnish

Light grating of nutmeg on top of the cream.

Flip

  • Brandy60 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Egg Yolk1 pc

Preparation

1. Combine brandy, simple syrup, and egg yolk in a shaker. 2. Dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg yolk, increasing volume, and creating a velvety texture. 3. Tom and Jerry

CreamySpicySweetVanillaNuttyWoody
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Cognac15 ml
  • Egg2 whole
  • Sugar20 g
  • Ground Cinnamon0 pinch
  • Ground Nutmeg0 pinch
  • Ground Cloves0 pinch
  • Hot Milk120 ml

Preparation

1. Separate the egg white and yolk. 2. Whisk the egg white until stiff peaks form. Gradually add half of the sugar while whisking. Set aside. 3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolk with the remaining sugar until light and creamy. Add the cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves to the yolk mixture and combine. 4. Gently fold the egg white mixture into the yolk mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. 5. In a preheated mug, combine the dark rum and cognac. 6. Add 2-3 tablespoons of the batter to the mug. 7. Top with hot milk, stirring gently to combine. 8. Grate fresh nutmeg on top for garnish.

Garnish

Freshly grated nutmeg on top
