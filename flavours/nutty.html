Irish Coffee

CoffeeVanillaCreamyNuttyWoodySpicy
  • Irish Whiskey50 ml
  • Hot Coffee120 ml
  • Brown Sugar1 tsp
  • Lightly Whipped Cream50 ml

Preparation

1. Warm a heatproof glass (such as a stemmed Irish coffee glass) by filling it with hot water, then discard the water. 2. Add brown sugar to the warmed glass. 3. Pour in hot coffee and stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. 4. Add Irish whiskey and stir gently to combine. 5. Cremat

CitrusSpicyFruityNuttyWoodySmoky
  • Rum200 ml
  • Granulated Sugar100 g
  • Lemon Peel1 pc
  • Orange Peel1 pc
  • Coffee Beans5 pc
  • Cinnamon Stick1 pc

Preparation

1. In a heat-resistant earthenware bowl or pot, combine the rum, sugar, lemon peel, orange peel, coffee beans, and cinnamon stick. 2. Stir the mixture gently to dissolve some of the sugar. 3. Carefully heat the rum mixture, either on a stovetop or using a heat source under the bowl. Ensure it doesn't boil. 4. Using a ladle, scoop some of the heated rum and pour it back over the sugar and spices. This helps to dissolve the sugar further and infuse the flavors. 5. Once the sugar is mostly dissolved, carefully ignite the surface of the rum with a long match or lighter. The alcohol fumes will catch fire and burn with a blue flame. 6. Allow the rum to burn for a few minutes, stirring occasionally with a ladle. The longer it burns, the more alcohol evaporates, reducing the overall booziness. 7. Extinguish the flames by covering the bowl with a lid or by carefully adding a small amount of water or coffee. 8. Ladle the Cremat into heat-resistant glasses or mugs, ensuring that each serving contains a portion of the citrus peels and coffee beans. 9. Serve immediately and enjoy warm.

Garnish

Lemon peel, orange peel, coffee beans (3-5), caramelized sugar around the rim (optional)

Flaming Doctor Pepper

NuttySweetWarmAlmond
  • Amaretto22 ml
  • High-Proof Rum (151 proof or higher)22 ml
  • Beer (Light Lager)150 ml

Preparation

1. Pour the beer into a heat-resistant glass, leaving about an inch of space at the top. 2. In a separate heat-resistant shot glass, carefully layer the amaretto. 3. Float the high-proof rum on top of the amaretto in the shot glass. 4. Carefully ignite the rum in the shot glass using a long lighter or match. 5. Allow the flame to burn for a few seconds (no more than 5-10 seconds). 6. Extinguish the flame by dropping the shot glass into the beer-filled glass, be careful to use a bar spoon to avoid splashing. 7. Consume immediately and responsibly.

Garnish

None recommended due to the nature of the drink. Safety is the priority.

Tom and Jerry

CreamySpicySweetVanillaNuttyWoody
  • Dark Rum30 ml
  • Cognac15 ml
  • Egg2 whole
  • Sugar20 g
  • Ground Cinnamon0 pinch
  • Ground Nutmeg0 pinch
  • Ground Cloves0 pinch
  • Hot Milk120 ml

Preparation

1. Separate the egg white and yolk. 2. Whisk the egg white until stiff peaks form. Gradually add half of the sugar while whisking. Set aside. 3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolk with the remaining sugar until light and creamy. Add the cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves to the yolk mixture and combine. 4. Gently fold the egg white mixture into the yolk mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix. 5. In a preheated mug, combine the dark rum and cognac. 6. Add 2-3 tablespoons of the batter to the mug. 7. Top with hot milk, stirring gently to combine. 8. Grate fresh nutmeg on top for garnish.

Garnish

Freshly grated nutmeg on top
