Cocktails with Refreshing flavor

Moscow Mule

SpicyCitrusRefreshing
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Ginger Beer90 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml

Preparation

Fill a copper mug with ice. Add vodka and lime juice. Top with ginger beer. Gently stir to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Garnish

Lime wedge (fresh)

Orange Tundra

CitrusFruitySweetRefreshing
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Orange Juice15 ml
  • Honey Syrup (1:1)10 ml
  • Soda Water30 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a highball glass. 2. In a shaker with ice, combine the vodka, orange juice, and honey syrup. 3. Shake well until chilled. 4. Strain into the chilled highball glass filled with ice. 5. Top with soda water. 6. Garnish with an orange slice or twist.

Garnish

Orange slice or twist

Southside

CitrusHerbalFruityRefreshing
  • Gin60 ml
  • Mint Leaves8 leaves
  • Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Preparation

In a cocktail shaker, gently muddle mint leaves with simple syrup. Add gin and lime juice. Fill the shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Garnish

Mint sprig

John Collins

CitrusHerbalFruitySweetSourRefreshing
  • Gin60 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup20 ml
  • Carbonated Water80 ml

Preparation

1. Chill a Collins glass. 2. Add gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice. 3. Shake well until well-chilled. 4. Strain into the chilled Collins glass filled with ice. 5. Top with carbonated water. 6. Gently stir to combine. 7. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a maraschino cherry.

Garnish

Lemon wheel and a maraschino cherry. The lemon wheel provides an aromatic citrus element, while the maraschino cherry adds a touch of sweetness and visual appeal.
