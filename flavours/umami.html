Cocktails with Umami flavor

Queen Mary

SaltyUmamiCitrusSpicySavory
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Tomato Juice90 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 dashes
  • Tabasco Sauce2 dashes
  • Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Black Pepper1 pinch

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled highball glass filled with ice.

Garnish

Celery stalk, lemon wedge, and optionally, a skewer of olives or pickled vegetables.

Bloody Mary

SaltyUmamiSpicySavory
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Tomato Juice120 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 ml
  • Hot Sauce2 ml
  • Horseradish1 tsp
  • Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Black Pepper1 pinch

Preparation

1. Rim a highball glass with celery salt. 2. Fill the glass with ice. 3. Add vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, horseradish, celery salt, and black pepper to the glass. 4. Stir well to combine all ingredients. 5. Garnish with a celery stalk, lemon wedge, olive, and any other desired additions.

Garnish

Celery stalk, lemon wedge, olive, and optional additions such as bacon, shrimp, or pickled vegetables.

Michelada

SaltyUmamiCitrusSpicySavory
  • Mexican Lager355 ml
  • Lime Juice30 ml
  • Hot Sauce3 dashes
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 dashes
  • Soy Sauce1 dash
  • Salt0 to taste

Preparation

1. Rim a chilled glass with salt (optional: mix chili powder with salt). 2. Fill the glass with ice. 3. Add lime juice, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and soy sauce. 4. Top with Mexican lager. 5. Stir gently to combine. 6. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Garnish

Lime wedge, salt rim (optional: chili powder mixed with salt)

Sake Bomb

UmamiYeastyGrassy
  • Sake45 ml
  • Beer355 ml

Preparation

1. Fill a pint glass about 3/4 full with beer. 2. Balance a pair of chopsticks horizontally across the top of the glass. 3. Place a shot glass containing sake on top of the chopsticks, balancing it in the middle. 4. Coordinate a shout (e.g., 'Sake Bomb!') with your companions, then sharply strike the table, causing the chopsticks to separate and the shot of sake to fall into the beer. 5. Immediately consume the mixture.

Garnish

None

Bacon Bourbon Manhattan

SmokySaltyUmamiWoodyVanillaCaramel
  • Bacon-Infused Bourbon60 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

First, prepare the bacon-infused bourbon by cooking bacon, reserving the fat, and infusing it with bourbon for approximately 4-6 hours, then freezing and straining to remove the fat. Next, chill a mixing glass. Add the bacon-infused bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Angostura bitters to the mixing glass. Fill with ice and stir until well-chilled, about 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a candied bacon strip or an orange peel, expressing the oils over the drink before placing the peel on the rim.

Garnish

Candied bacon strip or orange peel, expressed over the drink.
