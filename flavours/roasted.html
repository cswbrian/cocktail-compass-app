Black Velvet

YeastyBitterRoastedFruityCreamy
  • Champagne90 ml
  • Stout90 ml

Preparation

1. Chill both stout and champagne. 2. Gently pour chilled champagne into a flute glass, filling halfway. 3. Slowly pour the chilled stout over the back of a spoon so that it layers on top of the champagne, filling the glass. Be careful to maintain the separation of the liquids.

Garnish

None
