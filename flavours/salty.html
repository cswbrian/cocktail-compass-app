Cocktails with Salty flavor

Queen Mary

SaltyUmamiCitrusSpicySavory
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Tomato Juice90 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 dashes
  • Tabasco Sauce2 dashes
  • Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Black Pepper1 pinch

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled highball glass filled with ice.

Garnish

Celery stalk, lemon wedge, and optionally, a skewer of olives or pickled vegetables.

Bloody Mary

SaltyUmamiSpicySavory
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Tomato Juice120 ml
  • Lemon Juice15 ml
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 ml
  • Hot Sauce2 ml
  • Horseradish1 tsp
  • Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Black Pepper1 pinch

Preparation

1. Rim a highball glass with celery salt. 2. Fill the glass with ice. 3. Add vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, horseradish, celery salt, and black pepper to the glass. 4. Stir well to combine all ingredients. 5. Garnish with a celery stalk, lemon wedge, olive, and any other desired additions.

Garnish

Celery stalk, lemon wedge, olive, and optional additions such as bacon, shrimp, or pickled vegetables.

Paloma

CitrusFruityBitterSaltyTropical
  • Tequila Blanco60 ml
  • Grapefruit Soda90 ml

Preparation

Prepare the glass by rimming it with salt, if desired. Fill a highball glass with ice. Add Tequila Blanco and Lime Juice, then top with Grapefruit Soda. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.

Bacon Bourbon Manhattan

SmokySaltyUmamiWoodyVanillaCaramel
  • Bacon-Infused Bourbon60 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Preparation

First, prepare the bacon-infused bourbon by cooking bacon, reserving the fat, and infusing it with bourbon for approximately 4-6 hours, then freezing and straining to remove the fat. Next, chill a mixing glass. Add the bacon-infused bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Angostura bitters to the mixing glass. Fill with ice and stir until well-chilled, about 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a candied bacon strip or an orange peel, expressing the oils over the drink before placing the peel on the rim.

Garnish

Candied bacon strip or orange peel, expressed over the drink.

Chipotle Bloody Mary

SpicySmokyUmamiSaltySavory
  • Vodka60 ml
  • Tomato Juice120 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Worcestershire Sauce10 ml
  • Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce2 pc
  • Hot Sauce2 dashes
  • Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Black Pepper1 pinch

Preparation

1. Rim a highball glass with chili salt (optional). 2. Muddle chipotle peppers in adobo sauce in a shaker. 3. Add vodka, tomato juice, lime juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, celery salt, and black pepper to the shaker. 4. Add ice and shake well until chilled. 5. Strain into the prepared glass filled with ice. 6. Garnish with a celery stalk, lime wedge, and pickled jalapeños.

Garnish

Celery stalk, lime wedge, pickled jalapeños, chili salt rim (optional)

Mezcal Margarita

SmokyCitrusFruityEarthySalty
  • Mezcal50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lime Juice25 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml

Preparation

1. Prepare the glass: If desired, salt the rim of a chilled rocks glass by running a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in salt. 2. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine the mezcal, lime juice, orange liqueur, and agave nectar. 3. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 4. Shake well: Shake vigorously until well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. 5. Strain: Strain the mixture into the prepared rocks glass filled with fresh ice. 6. Garnish: Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel, optionally a salted rim. Salt application: rub a lime wedge around the rim, then dip into salt.
