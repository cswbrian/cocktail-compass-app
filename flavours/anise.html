Cocktails with Anise flavor

Revelation

CitrusHerbalFloralAnise
  • Gin45 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur15 ml
  • Absinthe2 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup7 ml

Preparation

Chill a coupe glass. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until properly chilled. Double strain into the chilled coupe glass. Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink and drop the twist into the glass.

Garnish

Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped into the glass.

Death in the Afternoon

HerbalAniseFruityFloral
  • Absinthe45 ml
  • Champagne120 ml

Preparation

Pour absinthe into a Champagne flute. Top with chilled Champagne. Gently stir until the drink turns opalescent.

Garnish

None typically, though a lemon twist is sometimes used as a modern addition.
