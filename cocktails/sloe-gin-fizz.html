Sloe Gin Fizz

Ingredients

  • Sloe Gin45 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Soda Water60 ml
  • Egg White (Optional)15 ml

Garnish: Lemon wedge or a few fresh berries (such as raspberries or blackberries) for visual appeal and an extra burst of flavor.

Instructions

1. If using egg white, add sloe gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a cocktail shaker without ice (dry shake). Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 2. Add ice to the shaker and shake again until well-chilled. 3. Strain into a chilled highball glass filled with ice. 4. Top with soda water.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 6

Body: 4

Complexity: 5

Booziness: 3

The Sloe Gin Fizz is a refreshing and effervescent cocktail that highlights the unique flavor of sloe gin. Sloe gin, unlike regular gin, is made by infusing gin with sloe berries, a type of wild plum. This infusion gives sloe gin its distinctive reddish hue and a flavor profile that balances sweetness and tartness. The Sloe Gin Fizz dates back to the late 19th century, gaining popularity during the golden age of cocktails. It's a simple yet elegant drink, often enjoyed as a brunch cocktail or a light afternoon refresher. The addition of lemon juice and simple syrup balances the sweetness of the sloe gin, while the soda water adds a delightful fizz. Egg white is optional, but it provides a smoother texture and a frothy head, elevating the drink's overall presentation and mouthfeel. The Sloe Gin Fizz embodies a classic cocktail's charm, offering a delightful combination of flavors and textures in a single glass. Its enduring appeal lies in its ability to be both sophisticated and approachable, making it a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts of all levels. The drink is not too strong, making it a nice brunch beverage.
