Queen's Cocktail

Flavor Notes

FruityHerbalCitrusBittersweet

Ingredients

  • Gin30 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth15 ml
  • Orange Bitters2 ml

Garnish: Orange twist, expressed over the drink and then dropped in.

Instructions

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine gin, dry vermouth, sweet vermouth, and orange bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled. Strain into the chilled glass. Express the oils from an orange twist over the drink and drop it in.

The Queen's cocktail is a classic variation on the Martini, offering a sweeter and fruitier profile compared to its drier counterpart. It typically features gin as its base, complemented by equal parts dry vermouth and sweet vermouth, and a dash of orange bitters. The combination of both dry and sweet vermouths creates a balanced complexity, bridging the gap between the crispness of a classic Martini and the richer notes of a Manhattan. The orange bitters add a subtle citrus aroma and flavor, enhancing the overall experience. The Queen's Cocktail, while not as widely known as some other classics, is appreciated for its smooth and harmonious blend of flavors. Some variations include a lemon twist rather than an orange twist, altering the citrus notes.
