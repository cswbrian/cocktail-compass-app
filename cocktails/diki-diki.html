Diki-Diki

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusSpicySweetTart

Ingredients

  • Apple Brandy45 ml
  • Swedish Punsch15 ml
  • Fresh Grapefruit Juice22 ml

Garnish: Grapefruit twist, expressed over the drink

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Double strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.

The Diki-Diki is a classic cocktail that highlights the balance between fruity brandy, tart grapefruit, and the subtle sweetness of Swedish punsch. Its origins are somewhat obscure, with no clear historical record pinpointing its creation. However, it likely emerged during the early to mid-20th century, a period of cocktail experimentation and the rise of fruit-forward drinks. The cocktail showcases the appeal of simple ingredients combined in a way that enhances each component. It's a testament to the era's ingenuity, where bartenders sought to create flavorful and refreshing drinks using available spirits and citrus. It is a relatively obscure classic. While it doesn't have a prominent place in cocktail history books, its reappearance in modern cocktail bars and online resources reflects a growing appreciation for forgotten recipes. Its refreshing flavor profile makes it well suited to be a summer drink. It can also pair well with light appetizers or served as a palate cleanser between courses.
