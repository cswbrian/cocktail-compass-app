Hot Buttered Rum

Flavor Notes

SpicyNuttyVanillaCreamyWoody

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum60 ml
  • Butter2 tbsp
  • Brown Sugar1 tbsp
  • Honey1 tsp
  • Ground Cinnamon0 tsp
  • Ground Nutmeg0 tsp
  • Ground Cloves0 tsp
  • Ground Allspice0 tsp
  • Hot Water180 ml

Garnish: Cinnamon stick or a star anise

Instructions

1. In a mug, combine the butter, brown sugar, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice. 2. Add the dark rum. 3. Top with hot water and stir until the butter is melted and all ingredients are well combined. 4. Garnish with a cinnamon stick or a star anise.

Hot Buttered Rum is a comforting and indulgent cocktail, perfect for cold evenings. Its history dates back to colonial America, where rum was a readily available spirit and butter was a staple ingredient. The combination of rum, butter, spices, and hot water creates a rich, warming beverage that has been enjoyed for centuries. Variations abound, with each family often having their own unique recipe for the buttered rum batter. Some recipes call for brown sugar, others for honey or maple syrup. Spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice are commonly used, adding depth and complexity to the flavor. The addition of hot water helps to melt the butter and dissolve the spices, creating a smooth and flavorful drink. Hot Buttered Rum remains a beloved classic, evoking feelings of nostalgia and warmth.
