Delilah

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur15 ml
  • Lillet Blanc15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Garnish: Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim, or a brandied cherry.

Instructions

Chill a coupe glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. Double strain into the chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or a brandied cherry.

The Delilah cocktail is a relatively modern creation that balances the dryness of gin with the sweetness of apricot liqueur and the tartness of lemon juice. The addition of Lillet Blanc contributes floral and honeyed notes, enhancing the drink's complexity. It's typically served in a coupe glass and garnished with a lemon twist or brandied cherry. Its exact origins are somewhat obscure, lacking definitive historical records. It is not directly associated with a particular bartender or bar, but rather seems to have emerged as a popular recipe in contemporary cocktail circles. The balance between gin, liqueur, and citrus makes it a refreshing yet sophisticated choice. The Delilah is an excellent example of a cocktail that showcases how classic ingredients can be combined to create a new and appealing flavor profile. It's a good choice for gin lovers looking for something a little different from the standard gin and tonic or martini, offering a sweeter, more approachable profile while still maintaining a degree of sophistication.
