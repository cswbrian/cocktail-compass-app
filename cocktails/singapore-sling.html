Cocktail Compass

Singapore Sling

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Cherry Heering15 ml
  • Cointreau7 ml
  • Benedictine7 ml
  • Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Grenadine7 ml
  • Soda Water30 ml
  • Angostura Bitters1 dash

Garnish: Pineapple wedge and maraschino cherry on a cocktail pick.

Instructions

Chill a sling glass or hurricane glass. Combine gin, cherry heering, cointreau, benedictine, pineapple juice, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into the chilled glass filled with ice. Top with soda water. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry on a cocktail pick.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 7

Sourness: 6

Body: 5

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 4

The Singapore Sling is a complex and iconic cocktail with a history shrouded in both fact and legend. Created at the Raffles Hotel in Singapore around 1915 by bartender Ngiam Tong Boon, it was intended as a discreet way for women to consume alcohol in a social setting. The drink's rosy hue and fruity flavors masked the presence of gin, making it appear more like a fruit punch than a potent cocktail. Over the years, the recipe has evolved, and many variations exist. The original recipe was never formally documented, adding to the drink's mystique. Some historical accounts suggest that Ngiam Tong Boon originally called it "Straits Sling", and he was inspired by the already popular gin sling. The popular versions of the cocktail generally include gin, cherry brandy, orange liqueur, pineapple juice, lime juice, and grenadine, along with a dash of bitters. Some recipes also call for a splash of soda water or club soda. The Singapore Sling is often associated with luxury and travel, evoking images of tropical evenings and colonial elegance. Its enduring appeal lies in its refreshing taste, complex flavor profile, and captivating history, making it a beloved classic cocktail worldwide. It stands as a testament to the creativity and innovation of bartenders in the early 20th century, transforming simple ingredients into an unforgettable drinking experience.
