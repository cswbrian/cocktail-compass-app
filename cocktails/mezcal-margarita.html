Mezcal Margarita

Flavor Notes

SmokyCitrusFruityEarthySalty

Ingredients

  • Mezcal50 ml
  • Orange Liqueur20 ml
  • Lime Juice25 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml

Garnish: Lime wedge or wheel, optionally a salted rim. Salt application: rub a lime wedge around the rim, then dip into salt.

Instructions

1. Prepare the glass: If desired, salt the rim of a chilled rocks glass by running a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in salt. 2. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine the mezcal, lime juice, orange liqueur, and agave nectar. 3. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 4. Shake well: Shake vigorously until well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds. 5. Strain: Strain the mixture into the prepared rocks glass filled with fresh ice. 6. Garnish: Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

The Mezcal Margarita is a smoky twist on the classic Margarita, replacing tequila with mezcal for a deeper, more complex flavor profile. The drink maintains the simple yet elegant structure of the original, highlighting the unique characteristics of mezcal. The combination of smoky mezcal, tart lime juice, and sweet agave nectar creates a balanced and refreshing cocktail. It's a perfect drink for those who enjoy the taste of tequila but seek a more adventurous flavor experience. It elevates the classic drink with an earthy note, a smoky accent, and a fascinating flavor. The Mezcal Margarita is often garnished with lime to enhance the citrusy aroma. Salt on the rim is optional and depends on the preferences of the drinker. Overall, the Mezcal Margarita is a sophisticated and flavorful cocktail that captures the essence of the classic Margarita while offering a more profound and unique drinking experience. Its versatility and ability to pair with various foods have contributed to its popularity in bars and restaurants worldwide, making it a favorite among both cocktail enthusiasts and casual drinkers.
