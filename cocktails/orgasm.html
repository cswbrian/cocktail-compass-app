Cocktail Compass

Orgasm

Flavor Notes

CreamyVanillaChocolateCoffeeNuttySweet

Ingredients

  • Coffee liqueur25 ml
  • Irish cream liqueur25 ml
  • Cream liqueur25 ml
  • Heavy cream25 ml

Garnish: Optional: Grated nutmeg or chocolate shavings

Instructions

Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass or small rocks glass. Garnish as desired.

The Orgasm cocktail is a sweet, creamy, and indulgent drink, typically served as an after-dinner treat or dessert cocktail. Its name is suggestive and playful, reflecting its rich and satisfying flavor profile. While the exact origin is debated, it likely emerged in the late 20th century as a part of a wave of sweet, layered cocktails popular in bars and lounges. Its recipe isn't set in stone, and variations are common, but the core ingredients usually include coffee liqueur, Irish cream liqueur, cream liqueur and sometimes amaretto. The drink's appeal lies in its simplicity and satisfying blend of flavors and textures, making it a popular choice for those seeking a sweet and creamy cocktail experience. It's often served in a small glass due to its richness. Its popularity stems from its easy-to-remember recipe and the readily available ingredients. The orgasm cocktail can be layered carefully for visual appeal and a gentle blend, or shaken with ice for a colder, more thoroughly mixed drink. The proportions vary, with some recipes favoring a stronger coffee flavor while others emphasize the creamy sweetness. Regardless, it remains a staple on many bar menus due to its broad appeal and fun name.
