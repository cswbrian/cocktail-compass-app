Perfect Manhattan

Flavor Notes

BitterHerbalSpicyWoody

Ingredients

  • Rye Whiskey30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth15 ml
  • Dry Vermouth15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Garnish: Brandied cherry or lemon twist

Instructions

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Add rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, dry vermouth and bitters to a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with a brandied cherry or lemon twist.

The Perfect Manhattan is a variation of the classic Manhattan cocktail that uses both sweet and dry vermouth. This creates a more balanced and nuanced flavor profile compared to a standard Manhattan, which typically uses only sweet vermouth. The cocktail is traditionally stirred, not shaken, to maintain its smooth texture. The origins of the Perfect Manhattan, like the original Manhattan, are somewhat murky, with various stories attributing its creation to different bartenders in the late 19th century. However, its appeal lies in its balanced sweetness and dryness, making it a sophisticated and approachable cocktail for whiskey lovers. The Perfect Manhattan represents a subtle refinement of a classic, appealing to those who appreciate a well-balanced and complex drink. It remains a popular choice in cocktail bars, appreciated for its elegant simplicity and depth of flavor. The drink showcases the harmonious interplay of the rye whiskey, the aromatic vermouths, and the touch of bitters.
