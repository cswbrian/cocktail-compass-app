Vargtass

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusTart

Ingredients

  • Vodka60 ml
  • Lingonberry Concentrate30 ml

Garnish: Lingonberries (fresh or frozen), optional

Instructions

1. Chill a rocks glass or old-fashioned glass. 2. Add vodka and lingonberry concentrate to the glass. 3. Add ice cubes. 4. Stir gently to combine. 5. Garnish with fresh or frozen lingonberries, if desired.
