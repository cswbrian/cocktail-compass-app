Jazmin Sour

Flavor Notes

CitrusFloralHerbalFruitySourSweet

Ingredients

  • Gin60 ml
  • Elderflower Liqueur15 ml
  • Lemon Juice25 ml
  • Egg White20 ml
  • Simple Syrup2 dashes

Garnish: Edible flower petal or Lemon twist

Instructions

1. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker without ice. 2. Dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds to emulsify the egg white. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until chilled. 5. Double strain into a chilled coupe or sour glass. 6. Garnish with an edible flower petal or lemon twist.

The Jazmin Sour is a modern variation on the classic Gin Sour, distinguished by the addition of floral notes from elderflower liqueur and the incorporation of egg white for a smooth, velvety texture. The precise origin is difficult to pinpoint, as many bartenders have likely experimented with similar combinations. However, the defining characteristics remain a balance of gin, citrus, sweetness, and floral aromatics, typically incorporating elderflower liqueur such as St. Germain. The egg white is optional but contributes significantly to the drink's mouthfeel and appearance. This cocktail aims for a sophisticated flavor profile, appealing to those who enjoy floral, citrus-forward drinks with a creamy texture. Its relatively easy preparation makes it accessible for home mixologists and a delightful addition to any cocktail menu. The Jazmin Sour stands as a testament to the enduring appeal of the sour format and its adaptability to new and exciting flavor combinations.
