Mr. Bali Hai

Flavor Notes

TropicalCoffeeSpicyFruity

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum45 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur30 ml
  • Pineapple Juice30 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Angostura bitters2 dashes

Garnish: A Mr. Bali Hai mug (or suitable tiki mug) with a pineapple spear, a cocktail umbrella, and a fresh orchid.

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a Mr. Bali Hai mug filled with crushed ice.

The Mr. Bali Hai is a classic tiki cocktail known for its complex blend of rum, coffee liqueur, and spices. Created at the Bali Hai Restaurant on Shelter Island in San Diego, California, this drink embodies the exotic flavors and escapism of the tiki culture that was very popular in the mid-20th century. The drink itself is a harmonious blend of rich coffee notes with a subtle spiced complexity, which blends wonderfully with the rum, creating a truly unique taste. The presentation, often featuring a distinctive Mr. Bali Hai mug, further adds to its allure. The Mr. Bali Hai's appeal comes from the skillful blending of its distinct flavors, achieving balance and depth without any single flavor overpowering the others. Its creation reflects the creativity and flair of the tiki era, designed to offer an immersive tropical experience. The use of high-quality ingredients and attention to detail in its preparation ensure a consistently satisfying and memorable cocktail.
