Bacon Bourbon Manhattan

Flavor Notes

SmokySaltyUmamiWoodyVanillaCaramel

Ingredients

  • Bacon-Infused Bourbon60 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Garnish: Candied bacon strip or orange peel, expressed over the drink.

Instructions

First, prepare the bacon-infused bourbon by cooking bacon, reserving the fat, and infusing it with bourbon for approximately 4-6 hours, then freezing and straining to remove the fat. Next, chill a mixing glass. Add the bacon-infused bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Angostura bitters to the mixing glass. Fill with ice and stir until well-chilled, about 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass. Garnish with a candied bacon strip or an orange peel, expressing the oils over the drink before placing the peel on the rim.

The Bacon Bourbon Manhattan offers a savory twist on the classic Manhattan cocktail, seamlessly blending the sweet and spicy notes of bourbon with the rich, smoky flavor of bacon. The infusion process imparts the bourbon with a unique umami depth, creating a cocktail that is both familiar and strikingly different. This cocktail, a modern riff, appeals to those seeking adventurous flavor combinations. The vermouth contributes herbal and slightly bitter notes, complementing both the bourbon and bacon, while the bitters add complexity. The cocktail is often garnished with a candied bacon strip or orange peel, enhancing the overall sensory experience.
