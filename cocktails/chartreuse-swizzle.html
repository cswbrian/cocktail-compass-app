Chartreuse Swizzle

Flavor Notes

HerbalCitrusTropicalSpicy

Ingredients

  • Green Chartreuse1 oz
  • Falernum0 5 oz
  • Lime Juice1 oz
  • Pineapple Juice1 oz
  • dashes Angostura bitters4 dashes

Garnish: Mint sprig, grated nutmeg

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a tall glass filled with crushed ice. Swizzle vigorously with a swizzle stick or bar spoon until the glass is frosted. Add more crushed ice to fill. Garnish with a mint sprig and grated nutmeg.

The Chartreuse Swizzle is a relatively modern classic cocktail, created by bartender Marcovaldo Dionysos at San Francisco's 15 Romolo bar in the early 2000s. It is known for its complex herbal flavors derived primarily from Green Chartreuse. The drink's appeal lies in its balance of sweetness, tartness, and the distinctive, almost medicinal, notes of Chartreuse, resulting in a refreshing and intriguing cocktail. The swizzle technique, involving crushed ice and a swizzle stick, dilutes the drink and creates a frothy texture, making it especially enjoyable on warm days. The Chartreuse Swizzle quickly gained popularity within cocktail circles for its unique flavor profile and ease of execution, becoming a staple on many craft cocktail menus and a testament to the enduring appeal of Chartreuse in contemporary mixology. Its refreshing, herbaceous nature makes it a great choice for both casual enjoyment and as an introduction to the world of more complex and esoteric cocktails. The combination of falernum, lime and chartreuse create a complex interplay of flavors that make this much more than the sum of its parts.
