Jagertee

Flavor Notes

FruitySpicyHerbalWoodySweet

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum20 ml
  • Stroh Rum (80% ABV)20 ml
  • Herbal Liqueur20 ml
  • Strong Black Tea100 ml
  • Red Wine50 ml
  • Orange, sliced1 slice
  • Lemon, sliced1 slice
  • Cinnamon Sticks2 sticks
  • Cloves2 whole

Garnish: Orange slice and/or a cinnamon stick.

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Gently heat the mixture, without boiling, until it is steaming hot. Stir to ensure all ingredients are well combined. Ladle into a heat-resistant glass or mug. Garnish with an orange slice and/or a cinnamon stick.
