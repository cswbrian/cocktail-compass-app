The Negroni is typically served over ice in an old-fashioned glass and garnished with an orange peel, further enhancing its citrusy aroma and visual appeal. Its balanced blend of gin, vermouth, and Campari makes it a perfect pre-dinner drink, stimulating the appetite and preparing the palate for the meal to come.
