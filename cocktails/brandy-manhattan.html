Cocktail Compass

Brandy Manhattan

Flavor Notes

FruityHerbalBitterWoody

Ingredients

  • Brandy50 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth25 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 ml

Garnish: Maraschino cherry, skewered, or a lemon twist, expressing oils over the drink and placing on the rim. The cherry adds sweetness, while the lemon twist provides a citrus aroma.

Instructions

Combine the brandy, sweet vermouth, and bitters in a mixing glass filled with ice. Stir well until thoroughly chilled, about 20-30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 2

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 7

The Brandy Manhattan is a variation of the classic Manhattan cocktail, substituting the traditional rye whiskey with brandy, also known as Cognac in many regions. This substitution creates a smoother, fruitier, and often slightly sweeter profile compared to its whiskey-based counterpart. The Brandy Manhattan retains the core elements of the Manhattan family, incorporating sweet vermouth and bitters to balance and complement the base spirit. It's a cocktail that emphasizes the nuances of the brandy, allowing its character to shine through while maintaining the sophisticated structure of the original Manhattan. The drink can be traced back to the late 19th century, arising alongside the increased availability and appreciation for quality brandy in American bars. Some bartenders add a dash of simple syrup to enhance the sweet vermouth when preparing the Brandy Manhattan.
