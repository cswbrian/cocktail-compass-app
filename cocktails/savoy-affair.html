Savoy Affair

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusFloral

Ingredients

  • Gin30 ml
  • Apricot Brandy15 ml
  • Orange Juice20 ml
  • Grenadine5 ml

Garnish: Orange twist

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

The Savoy Affair cocktail is a delightful blend of gin, apricot brandy, orange juice, and grenadine, resulting in a sweet and fruity concoction. It is found in the Savoy Cocktail Book. Its precise origins are not fully documented beyond its inclusion in the Savoy Cocktail Book, which suggests it was a popular drink during that era. The cocktail's balance hinges on the combination of the gin's botanicals, the apricot brandy's fruity sweetness, the orange juice's tangy brightness, and the grenadine's rich, fruity notes. The cocktail is a celebration of flavors that complement each other to make a harmonious whole. The Savoy Affair represents the creativity and elegance of cocktail culture during the early 20th century.
