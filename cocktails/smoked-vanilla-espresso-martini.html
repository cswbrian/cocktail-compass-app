Smoked Vanilla Espresso Martini

Flavor Notes

CoffeeVanillaSmokySweetBitter

Ingredients

  • Vodka45 ml
  • Coffee Liqueur20 ml
  • Freshly Brewed Espresso (cooled)30 ml
  • Vanilla Syrup15 ml

Garnish: 3 coffee beans and a smoked vanilla bean, if available, or a pinch of smoked salt sprinkled on top of the foam.

Instructions

Chill a martini glass. Combine vodka, coffee liqueur, vanilla syrup, and cooled espresso in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously until well-chilled. Double strain into the chilled martini glass. Garnish with 3 coffee beans and a smoked vanilla bean, or sprinkle a pinch of smoked salt on top.

The Smoked Vanilla Espresso Martini is a modern twist on the classic Espresso Martini, incorporating smoked elements and vanilla notes for a more complex and intriguing flavor profile. The drink combines the stimulating effects of espresso with the smoothness of vodka and the sweetness of coffee liqueur, further enhanced by the addition of vanilla syrup and a subtle smokiness achieved through the use of smoked salt or a smoked vanilla bean garnish. This variation elevates the traditional Espresso Martini to a sophisticated and memorable experience. The balance of bitter coffee, sweet vanilla, and smoky undertones creates a harmonious blend that is both energizing and indulgent, perfect as an after-dinner cocktail or a pick-me-up any time of day. It caters to those who appreciate nuanced flavors and a touch of elegance in their cocktails. The smoked element adds depth and intrigue, setting it apart from standard Espresso Martinis and making it a conversation starter. The vanilla complements the coffee beautifully, smoothing out any harsh edges and adding a layer of comforting sweetness.
