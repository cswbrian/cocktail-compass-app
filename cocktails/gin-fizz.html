Gin Fizz

Flavor Notes

CitrusHerbalGrassyFloral

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Lemon Juice30 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Soda Water90 ml

Garnish: Lemon wheel or wedge

Instructions

1. Chill a highball glass. 2. In a shaker, combine gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until chilled. 5. Strain into the chilled highball glass filled with ice. 6. Top with soda water. 7. Gently stir to combine. 8. Garnish with a lemon wheel or wedge.

The Gin Fizz is a classic cocktail known for its refreshing and effervescent character. Its origins can be traced back to the 19th century, gaining popularity during the pre-Prohibition era. The cocktail's simplicity and balance of flavors have made it a long-standing favorite. It is essentially a sour (spirit, citrus, sweetener) lengthened with carbonated water. Many variations exist including the Ramos Gin Fizz (with cream and egg white), the Silver Fizz (with egg white), the Golden Fizz (with egg yolk), and the Royal Fizz (with whole egg). The Gin Fizz represents a versatile base for experimentation, allowing for the incorporation of various fruits, herbs, and spices to create unique flavor profiles. Its effervescence makes it a refreshing choice for warm weather or as an aperitif.
