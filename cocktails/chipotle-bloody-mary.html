Chipotle Bloody Mary

Ingredients

  • Vodka60 ml
  • Tomato Juice120 ml
  • Lime Juice15 ml
  • Chipotle Pepper in Adobo Sauce2 pc
  • Worcestershire Sauce2 ml
  • Hot Sauce2 drops
  • Celery Salt1 pinch
  • Black Pepper1 pinch

Garnish: Celery stalk, lime wedge, pickled jalapeños, and a strip of crispy bacon.

Instructions

1. Muddle the chipotle pepper in a shaker. 2. Add vodka, tomato juice, lime juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, celery salt, and black pepper to the shaker. 3. Add ice and shake well until chilled. 4. Strain into a salt-rimmed highball glass filled with ice. 5. Garnish with a celery stalk, lime wedge, pickled jalapeños, and a strip of crispy bacon.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 6

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 4

The Chipotle Bloody Mary is a spicy and smoky twist on the classic brunch cocktail. The addition of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce brings a deep, smoky heat that complements the savory tomato base and other spices. This variation elevates the traditional Bloody Mary, offering a more complex and robust flavor profile that is both invigorating and satisfying. It is often garnished with items that enhance its spicy character, such as pickled jalapeños, bacon, or a lime wedge. Its creation is relatively recent, fitting into the trend of adding increasingly complex and flavorful elements to the classic recipe. While its exact origins are undocumented, it likely emerged from contemporary mixology, where bartenders experiment with bold flavors to create unique drinking experiences. The Chipotle Bloody Mary is especially popular in regions with a fondness for spicy cuisine.
