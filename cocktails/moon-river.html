Cocktail Compass

Moon River

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusFloralHerbal

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Apricot Liqueur15 ml
  • Lemon Juice22 ml

Garnish: Lemon twist, expressed over the drink and placed on the rim.

Instructions

Chill a coupe or martini glass. Combine gin, apricot liqueur, and lemon juice in a shaker with ice. Shake well until well-chilled. Double strain into the chilled glass. Express a lemon twist over the drink and use it as garnish.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 4

Body: 4

Complexity: 6

Booziness: 4

The Moon River cocktail is a delicate and refreshing drink, designed to evoke a sense of tranquility and understated elegance. Its precise origins are somewhat obscure, contributing to its mystique. It gained popularity for its balanced profile, combining the botanical notes of gin with the sweetness of apricot liqueur and the slight tartness of lemon juice. This cocktail is favored for its light and approachable nature, making it a perfect aperitif or a sophisticated choice for a casual gathering. While not steeped in centuries of history like some classics, the Moon River represents a modern appreciation for subtle flavors, aligning with the trend of lighter, more nuanced cocktails. It is often found on cocktail menus seeking to offer something that bridges the gap between traditional drinks and contemporary tastes, showcasing a delightful harmony of flavors that makes it a memorable and enjoyable experience.
