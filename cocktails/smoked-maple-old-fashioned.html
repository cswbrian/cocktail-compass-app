Smoked Maple Old Fashioned

Flavor Notes

SmokyWoodySweetVanillaSpicyCitrus

Ingredients

  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Maple Syrup5 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Garnish: Orange peel, expressed and inserted, optionally brandied cherry.

Instructions

1. Smoke the bourbon: Use a smoking gun to infuse the bourbon with smoke flavor, then strain into a mixing glass. 2. Add maple syrup and bitters to the mixing glass. 3. Add ice to the mixing glass. 4. Stir gently until well-chilled. 5. Strain into a rocks glass filled with one large ice cube. 6. Express an orange peel over the drink and garnish.

The Smoked Maple Old Fashioned elevates the classic with a touch of smoky intrigue and the rich sweetness of maple syrup. This variation offers a complex and nuanced flavor profile, perfect for those seeking a sophisticated twist on a timeless favorite. The smoking process infuses the bourbon with depth, while the maple syrup adds a layer of decadent sweetness. The traditional bitters balance the sweetness and add spicy and herbal notes, while the orange peel contributes bright citrus aromas. This cocktail is best enjoyed slowly, allowing the flavors to evolve and meld together.
