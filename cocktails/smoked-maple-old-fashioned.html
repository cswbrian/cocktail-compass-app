Smoked Maple Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Smoked Simple Syrup2 bar spoons
  • Maple Syrup0 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Garnish: Orange peel, expressed and inserted, and a brandied cherry

Instructions

In an old-fashioned glass, add smoked simple syrup, maple syrup, and Angostura bitters. Add the bourbon. Add a large ice cube. Stir gently to combine the ingredients. Express the oils from an orange peel over the glass and drop it in. Garnish with a brandied cherry.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 2

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 8

The Smoked Maple Old Fashioned elevates the classic Old Fashioned with the addition of smoked simple syrup and maple syrup. This variation provides a rich, complex flavor profile that balances the warmth of bourbon with smoky and sweet notes. The maple syrup adds a unique depth and smoothness, complementing the traditional bitters and creating a sophisticated and comforting cocktail. The smoking process infuses the syrup with a distinct aroma and flavor, setting it apart from a typical Old Fashioned. This drink is best enjoyed slowly, allowing the flavors to meld and evolve on the palate. It's an excellent choice for those seeking a more adventurous twist on a beloved classic, perfect for cooler evenings or as a contemplative drink. It is a drink of many levels.
