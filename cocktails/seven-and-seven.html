Seven and Seven

Flavor Notes

CitrusSweetGrassy

Ingredients

  • Whiskey44 ml
  • 7-Up120 ml

Garnish: Lime wedge (optional)

Instructions

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add whiskey. Top with 7-Up. Stir gently to combine. Garnish with a lime wedge, if desired.

The Seven and Seven is a simple highball cocktail consisting of Seagram's Seven Crown blended whiskey and 7-Up soda. It's a popular, easy-to-make drink often ordered by those looking for a light and refreshing alcoholic beverage. Its appeal lies in its straightforwardness and the familiar flavors of whiskey and lemon-lime soda. While not considered a sophisticated or complex cocktail, the Seven and Seven has a nostalgic quality for many, reminiscent of casual gatherings and simpler times. It's typically served in a highball glass filled with ice. The ratio of whiskey to soda can vary based on personal preference, but it generally balances the whiskey's boldness with the sweetness and carbonation of the 7-Up. The Seven and Seven remains a ubiquitous and easily recognized drink in bars and social settings, favored for its simplicity and ease of consumption.
