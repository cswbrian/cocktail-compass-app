Cocktail Compass

El Diablo

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusSpicyTropical

Ingredients

  • Tequila45 ml
  • Crème de Cassis15 ml
  • Lime Juice20 ml
  • Ginger Beer90 ml

Garnish: Lime wedge or wheel

Instructions

Combine tequila, crème de cassis, and lime juice in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled highball glass filled with ice. Top with ginger beer. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 5

Sourness: 6

Body: 4

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 4

The El Diablo is a refreshing and vibrant cocktail that balances the earthy notes of tequila with the sweet and spicy flavors of crème de cassis and ginger beer. Its vibrant red hue and effervescent quality make it a visually appealing and palate-pleasing drink, perfect for warm weather or any occasion that calls for a touch of spirited elegance. The drink's creation is often attributed to Trader Vic, circa 1946. However, definitive sources are hard to come by. While not as widely known as some other tequila-based cocktails, the El Diablo offers a unique flavor profile that sets it apart. The combination of tequila, crème de cassis, lime juice, and ginger beer creates a complex interplay of flavors that is both refreshing and satisfying. The lime juice adds a tartness that balances the sweetness of the crème de cassis, while the ginger beer provides a spicy kick and effervescence that elevates the drink. The tequila ties everything together. Its earthy notes provide a counterpoint to the other ingredients, creating a cocktail that is both complex and well-balanced.
