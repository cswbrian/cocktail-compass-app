Cocktail Compass

Fuzzy Navel

Flavor Notes

FruityTropicalSweet

Ingredients

  • Peach Schnapps45 ml
  • Orange Juice90 ml

Garnish: Orange slice or cherry (optional)

Instructions

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add peach schnapps. Top with orange juice. Stir gently. Garnish as desired.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 8

Sourness: 3

Body: 4

Complexity: 2

Booziness: 2

The Fuzzy Navel is a simple and popular highball cocktail known for its sweet and fruity flavor. It's a combination of peach schnapps and orange juice, making it an easy-to-drink and refreshing beverage. While its exact origins are unclear, it gained popularity in the 1980s and has remained a staple in casual bars and gatherings. Its accessibility and uncomplicated preparation have contributed to its enduring appeal. The name "Fuzzy Navel" is a playful combination of the "fuzzy" texture of peaches and the "navel" orange, representing the two main ingredients. The drink is often associated with warm weather and social occasions, being a light and enjoyable option for those who prefer sweeter cocktails. The Fuzzy Navel is rarely found in high end cocktail bars, but remains popular due to ease of preparation, availability of ingredients, and flavor profile. While not particularly complex, it serves as a pleasant and approachable introduction to mixed drinks, especially for those new to cocktail culture. Over time, the basic recipe has spawned numerous variations, with some adding vodka or other spirits to increase the alcoholic content or adjust the flavor balance. However, the classic combination of peach schnapps and orange juice remains the defining characteristic of this cocktail, which is very sweet and fruity.
