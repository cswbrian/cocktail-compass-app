Tommy's Margarita

Flavor Notes

CitrusFruityTropical

Ingredients

  • Tequila60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Agave Nectar20 ml

Garnish: Lime wedge or wheel

Instructions

1. Chill a coupe or rocks glass. 2. Add Tequila, lime juice, and agave nectar to a shaker with ice. 3. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 4. Strain into the chilled glass. 5. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Tommy's Margarita, created by Julio Bermejo at Tommy's Mexican Restaurant in San Francisco, is a purist's take on the classic Margarita. It replaces the traditional orange liqueur with agave nectar, highlighting the tequila's quality and agave flavors. This simple adjustment results in a drink that's both refreshing and complex, allowing the nuances of the tequila to shine. Tommy's Margarita is a testament to the idea that sometimes less is more, and it has become a favorite among tequila aficionados worldwide. The use of high-quality agave nectar complements the agave notes in the tequila, creating a harmonious balance of flavors. The lime juice provides a necessary tartness that cuts through the sweetness of the agave and the strength of the tequila, resulting in a well-rounded cocktail that's both easy to drink and satisfyingly complex. Because of its simplicity and focus on quality ingredients, Tommy's Margarita serves as an excellent showcase for premium tequilas. It also highlights the importance of balance in cocktails, demonstrating how a few well-chosen ingredients can come together to create something truly special. This particular margarita has propelled tequila into the forefront of the cocktailian world, demanding that only the best be used in this classic drink. The transparency in the ingredients highlights the differences between the terroir, production method, and aging of various tequilas. The absence of orange liqueur makes it so you can taste the distinct flavor profiles of the tequila used, while the agave nectar and lime juice simply act to balance the drink.
