Last of the Mohicans

Flavor Notes

WoodyVanillaSpicyCitrusChocolateMaple

Ingredients

  • Bourbon45 ml
  • Chocolate Bitters2 ml
  • Maple Syrup15 ml

Garnish: Orange zest twist, expressed over the drink and then placed on the rim.

Instructions

Combine bourbon, maple syrup, and chocolate bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass filled with a large ice cube. Express the oils from an orange zest over the drink and place on the rim.

The Last of the Mohicans cocktail, while not directly linked to the historical events or James Fenimore Cooper's novel of the same name, evokes a sense of rugged sophistication and balance. It is a modern cocktail, with no clear historical documentation, that combines the robust character of bourbon with the sweetness of maple syrup and the contrasting bitterness of chocolate bitters. The hint of citrus brings a zesty aroma and accentuates all the other flavors. It aims to represent a blend of strength and refinement. This cocktail's flavor profile is well-suited as an after-dinner drink or a sophisticated nightcap, offering a complex and memorable drinking experience. Its name probably draws from the novel for its adventurous and evocative feel.
