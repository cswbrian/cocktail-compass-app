Brut

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusHerbalDryFloral

Ingredients

  • Gin45 ml
  • Simple Syrup7 ml
  • Brut Champagne90 ml

Garnish: Lemon twist; expressed over the drink and then dropped in.

Instructions

Chill a coupe or champagne flute. Add gin and simple syrup to the glass. Top with chilled Brut Champagne. Gently express the oils of a lemon twist over the drink and drop it into the glass.

The Brut cocktail is a simple yet elegant drink that highlights the dryness of sparkling wine, typically Brut Champagne or a similar dry sparkling wine. It is characterized by its crisp, refreshing taste, making it an ideal aperitif or celebratory beverage. The drink's history is somewhat intertwined with the broader history of Champagne cocktails, which gained popularity in the 19th century. While not as elaborate as some of its contemporaries, the Brut cocktail stands out for its minimalist approach, focusing on the quality of its core ingredients to deliver a sophisticated and balanced drinking experience. Its simplicity allows the effervescence and flavor nuances of the sparkling wine to shine through, offering a clean and invigorating palate. The addition of gin adds a layer of botanical complexity that elevates the drink beyond a simple glass of sparkling wine. The Brut cocktail represents a classic example of how a few well-chosen ingredients can create a memorable and enjoyable drink.
