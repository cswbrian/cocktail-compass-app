Mezcal Negroni

Flavor Notes

BitterHerbalSmokyCitrusEarthy

Ingredients

  • Mezcal30 ml
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet Vermouth30 ml

Garnish: Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass.

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well-chilled (approximately 20-30 seconds). Strain into an old-fashioned glass filled with ice. Express the oils from an orange peel over the drink and drop the peel into the glass.

Flavour Profile

Booziness: 7

The Mezcal Negroni is a smoky and complex twist on the classic Negroni cocktail. It replaces the traditional gin with mezcal, offering a deeper, more intense flavor profile. The mezcal's earthy and smoky notes combine with the bitter Campari and sweet vermouth to create a well-balanced and intriguing drink. The origins of the Mezcal Negroni are relatively recent, emerging as a popular variation within the craft cocktail scene. Bartenders and cocktail enthusiasts sought to explore new flavor combinations, and mezcal's unique characteristics made it a natural fit for the Negroni framework. The drink's appeal lies in its ability to retain the core Negroni balance while introducing a layer of complexity and smokiness that elevates the experience. The Mezcal Negroni is typically served over ice in an old-fashioned glass, garnished with an orange peel, which complements both the mezcal and the Campari. The cocktail is appreciated for its robust flavor, making it a favorite among those who enjoy bolder, more adventurous drinks. It embodies the spirit of innovation in cocktail culture, showcasing how classic recipes can be reimagined with different ingredients to create something entirely new and exciting. The Mezcal Negroni offers a sophisticated and memorable drinking experience, perfect for sipping and savoring.
