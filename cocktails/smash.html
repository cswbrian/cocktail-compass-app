Cocktail Compass

Smash

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey60 ml
  • Mint Leaves8 leaves
  • Simple Syrup15 ml
  • Seasonal Fruit (e.g., Berries, Peaches, Citrus)60 grams

Garnish: Mint sprig and seasonal fruit slice (e.g., orange, strawberry, or berries).

Instructions

1. Gently muddle the fruit, mint leaves, and simple syrup in a shaker. Avoid over-muddling, which can release bitter compounds. 2. Add bourbon to the shaker. 3. Fill the shaker with ice. 4. Shake well until chilled. 5. Strain into a rocks glass filled with crushed ice. 6. Garnish with a fresh mint sprig and a slice of the seasonal fruit used in the cocktail.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 4

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 6

The Smash is a versatile and refreshing cocktail category rather than a single specific recipe. It is essentially a spirit-forward drink built around muddled fresh fruit, herbs, and sugar, topped with crushed ice. The beauty of the Smash lies in its adaptability, allowing for endless variations depending on seasonal ingredients and personal preferences. Mint is a very common herb to muddle but other herbs work well too. While the mint julep uses crushed ice, the Smash has been interpreted in many forms, depending on the bartender. As long as it has fruit, herbs and a base spirit it usually qualifies. This recipe highlights bourbon as a solid foundation, complementing it with the sweetness of the fruit and the aromatic freshness of mint.
