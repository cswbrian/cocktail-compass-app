Cocktail Compass

Widow's Kiss

Ingredients

  • Apple Brandy30 ml
  • Bénédictine15 ml
  • Yellow Chartreuse15 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Garnish: Maraschino cherry

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

Flavour Profile

Sourness: 2

Body: 6

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 6

The Widow's Kiss is a classic cocktail that dates back to the late 19th century. Its origins are somewhat obscure, but it's believed to have emerged during the golden age of cocktails. The Widow's Kiss is known for its complex and somewhat bittersweet flavor profile. The combination of apple brandy, Bénédictine, Chartreuse, and bitters creates a unique blend of fruity, herbal, and spicy notes. The apple brandy provides a fruity base, while Bénédictine contributes a honeyed sweetness and herbal complexity. The Yellow Chartreuse adds additional herbal and floral notes, and the bitters provide balance. Historically, the Widow's Kiss was served as either an after-dinner digestif or a late-night cocktail, suitable for contemplation. It is relatively obscure compared to other classic cocktails like the Manhattan or Martini. The Widow's Kiss embodies the spirit of classic mixology with its intriguing combination of ingredients and balanced flavors, making it a sophisticated choice for those who appreciate complex drinks.
