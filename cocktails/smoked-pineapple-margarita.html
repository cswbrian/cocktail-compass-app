Smoked Pineapple Margarita

Ingredients

  • Blanco Tequila50 ml
  • Mezcal (Optional)15 ml
  • Smoked Pineapple Juice60 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice30 ml
  • Agave Nectar15 ml

Garnish: Pineapple wedge, lightly charred, and a smoked salt rim (optional).

Instructions

1. Smoke the pineapple chunks: Smoke pineapple chunks using a smoker or grill for approximately 2-3 hours at low temperature (around 200°F). 2. Juice the smoked pineapple: Extract the juice from the smoked pineapple chunks using a juicer or by muddling and straining. 3. Prepare the glass: If desired, rim a rocks glass with smoked salt. 4. Combine ingredients: In a shaker, combine the tequila, smoked pineapple juice, fresh lime juice, agave nectar, and mezcal (if using). 5. Add ice: Fill the shaker with ice. 6. Shake well: Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds until well-chilled. 7. Strain and serve: Strain the mixture into the prepared rocks glass filled with ice. 8. Garnish: Garnish with a lightly charred pineapple wedge.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 6

Sourness: 7

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 5

The Smoked Pineapple Margarita elevates the classic margarita with a tropical twist and a hint of smoky intrigue. Fresh pineapple is smoked to intensify its sweetness and add a layer of complexity that complements the tequila's agave notes. The lime juice provides the necessary acidity to balance the sweetness, while the agave nectar enhances the overall sweetness and brings a cohesive element to the drink. A touch of mezcal can be added for an extra layer of smokiness, creating a truly memorable and sophisticated cocktail. This margarita is perfect for those seeking a flavorful and exciting alternative to the traditional version, offering a delightful combination of sweet, sour, and smoky flavors.
