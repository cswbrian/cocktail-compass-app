Daisy de Santiago

Flavor Notes

FruityCitrusTropicalVanilla

Ingredients

  • Aged Cuban Rum60 ml
  • Maraschino Liqueur7 ml
  • Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Garnish: Lime wedge

Instructions

1. Chill a coupe or martini glass. 2. In a shaker, combine the aged Cuban rum, fresh lime juice, simple syrup, and maraschino liqueur. 3. Add ice to the shaker. 4. Shake well until thoroughly chilled. 5. Double strain into the chilled glass. 6. Garnish with a lime wedge.

The Daisy de Santiago is a classic Cuban cocktail, celebrated for its vibrant citrus notes and underlying sweetness. Its origins trace back to Santiago de Cuba, a city renowned for its rich rum production and vibrant cocktail culture. The drink is a sophisticated variation of the classic Daisy cocktail, which typically includes a base spirit, citrus juice, and a sweetener, often grenadine or a liqueur. What distinguishes the Daisy de Santiago is its emphasis on high-quality aged Cuban rum, showcasing the region's exceptional craftsmanship. The cocktail gained prominence during the mid-20th century, a period when Cuban cocktails were highly sought after internationally. The Daisy de Santiago is appreciated for its balanced flavor profile, combining the depth of aged rum with the tangy freshness of citrus and the subtle sweetness of maraschino liqueur and simple syrup. Its elegant presentation and refreshing taste made it a favorite in upscale bars and private gatherings. The drink remains a beloved classic, representing the golden age of Cuban cocktail artistry and offering a taste of the island's rich cultural heritage. It perfectly encapsulates the spirit of Cuban cocktail culture, where simple ingredients are transformed into complex and delightful creations.
