Cocktail Compass

Ti' Punch

Ingredients

  • Rhum Agricole50 ml
  • Cane Syrup or Brown Sugar15 ml
  • Lime Wedge20 ml

Garnish: Lime wedge or zest (expressed over the drink)

Instructions

1. Muddle the lime wedge and cane syrup (or brown sugar) gently in an Old Fashioned glass. 2. Add the Rhum Agricole. 3. Add one ice cube (optional, traditionally served without ice). 4. Stir gently to combine. 5. Express the oils from a lime zest over the drink (optional).

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 3

Sourness: 5

Body: 6

Complexity: 7

Booziness: 7

Ti' Punch, meaning "small punch," is the quintessential cocktail of the French Caribbean islands, particularly Martinique and Guadeloupe. It’s not just a drink; it's a ritual, a custom, and a reflection of the island's culture and history. Unlike many cocktails with rigid recipes, the Ti' Punch is often made to personal taste, with the ingredients offered and assembled by the drinker themselves. It's a drink of the people, a simple yet profoundly satisfying concoction that highlights the quality of Rhum Agricole. The rhum is a key component that differentiates this cocktail from other rum-based drinks. Rhum Agricole, made directly from fresh sugarcane juice rather than molasses, gives Ti' Punch a distinct grassy, vegetal character that sets it apart. The tradition of Ti' Punch is deeply ingrained in the social fabric of the islands, it's the ideal drink to enjoy at any time of day. Its simplicity allows for a wide range of personal preferences, with each individual adjusting the proportions of rum, lime, and sugar to create their ideal Ti' Punch, making it a uniquely personal experience.
