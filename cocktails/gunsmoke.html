Cocktail Compass

Gunsmoke

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey45 ml
  • Peated Scotch Whisky15 ml
  • Maple Syrup5 ml
  • Angostura Bitters2 dashes

Garnish: Orange peel, expressed and placed in the glass

Instructions

Add bourbon, peated scotch, maple syrup, and bitters to a mixing glass. Add ice and stir well until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube. Express the oils from an orange peel over the glass and place the peel in the glass.

Flavour Profile

Sweetness: 4

Sourness: 2

Body: 7

Complexity: 8

Booziness: 7

The Gunsmoke cocktail offers a bold and smoky twist on the classic Old Fashioned. Its history is somewhat modern, emerging from the craft cocktail scene's exploration of intensified flavors. By integrating the peaty smokiness of scotch whisky, the drink delivers a more complex experience than a standard bourbon-based Old Fashioned. The subtle sweetness of maple syrup complements the whiskey and smoke, creating a well-balanced and memorable libation. Its popularity stems from its intriguing flavor profile, which appeals to those seeking a more adventurous and nuanced whiskey cocktail. The Gunsmoke represents a contemporary adaptation, pushing the boundaries of traditional recipes while still honoring the essence of a well-crafted Old Fashioned.
