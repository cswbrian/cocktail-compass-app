Derby

Flavor Notes

CitrusFruityWoodyVanilla

Ingredients

  • Bourbon60 ml
  • Lime Juice22 ml
  • Simple Syrup15 ml

Garnish: Lime wheel or mint sprig

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well until chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass filled with ice.

The Derby cocktail is a classic concoction featuring bourbon as its base, complemented by the refreshing flavors of lime juice and a touch of sweetness from simple syrup. Its precise origins are somewhat debated, but it's believed to have emerged around the mid-20th century. The cocktail's name likely alludes to the famous Kentucky Derby horse race, given bourbon's strong association with the state. The Derby shares similarities with other sour-style cocktails like the Whiskey Sour, but its distinct blend of ingredients sets it apart. Over time, variations of the Derby have appeared, some incorporating different citrus fruits or liqueurs to add complexity. However, the core combination of bourbon, lime, and simple syrup remains the defining characteristic of this simple yet satisfying drink. The Derby is appreciated for its easy preparation and balanced flavor profile, making it a popular choice for both home bartenders and professional mixologists seeking a classic, approachable cocktail. It represents a time when simple, well-executed drinks were highly valued, and it continues to be enjoyed for its straightforward appeal.
